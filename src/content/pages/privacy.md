## Overview

Prison Visitor Guide is designed to respect user privacy. This site collects minimal information and does not track, profile, or monetize its users in any way.

## What This Site Does Not Collect

This site does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics services, advertising networks, or any other mechanism to monitor user behavior. Specifically:

-   No cookies are set or read by this site
-   No analytics or tracking scripts are used (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, etc.)
-   No user accounts exist and no registration is required
-   No personal information is collected through browsing this site
-   No advertising is displayed
-   No data is sold or shared with third parties

## Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs (such as IP addresses and page requests) as part of its standard hosting infrastructure. This data is controlled by GitHub, not by Prison Visitor Guide. GitHub's privacy practices are described in [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

## Contact and Feedback Submissions

When users send corrections, suggestions, or questions to [prisonvisitorguide@gmail.com](mailto:prisonvisitorguide@gmail.com), the email address and any information included in the message are received by Prison Visitor Guide. This information is:

-   Used only to respond to the message and to improve site content
-   Not shared with any third party
-   Not added to any mailing list or database
-   Not used for marketing or solicitation

## External Links

This site contains links to external websites, including government agencies, advocacy organizations, and other resources. Clicking these links takes users to sites with their own privacy practices, which are not controlled by Prison Visitor Guide. Users should review the privacy policies of external sites independently.

## Children's Privacy

This site does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. No personal information is collected from any user regardless of age, as described above.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the site's data practices change. Any material changes will be noted on this page with the date of the most recent revision.
