Vermont runs a unified correctional system: the Department of Corrections is the sole agency for everything from pretrial detention through prison and community supervision, with six in-state facilities and no county jails. Women statewide are at the Chittenden Regional Correctional Facility in South Burlington; men are held regionally at facilities in Rutland, St. Johnsbury, Newport, St. Albans, and Springfield — and about 150 sentenced men are currently housed out of state at a contract facility in Mississippi. The Offender Locator on the DOC homepage shows where someone is, and the department's Constituent Services Unit is the published hub for family questions.
