Wyoming's prison system is run by the Wyoming Department of Corrections (WDOC), which operates five adult facilities — the State Penitentiary in Rawlins, the Medium Correctional Institution in Torrington, the Women's Center in Lusk, the Honor Farm in Riverton, and the Honor Conservation Camp in Newcastle — plus contracted community corrections centers. Some Wyoming inmates are currently housed in county jails and out of state. The Wyoming Offender Locator shows who is in WDOC custody.
