## Overview

Long Unit, on US Highway 84 east of Ludowici in Long County, is a **medium-security state prison for men** in southeast Georgia. It has a capacity of about **212** and operates academic, vocational, and on-the-job training programs aimed at reentry preparation. The facility also houses a fire station. Long County is in the Hinesville and Savannah region of the state's southeastern coastal plain.

## What Makes Long Different

- **It is a smaller medium-security facility,** with a capacity of about 212 — well below the size of Georgia's larger men's prisons.
- **It runs academic, vocational, and on-the-job training programs,** part of GDC's reentry mission.
- **It houses a fire station.**
- **It is in southeast Georgia,** near Hinesville and about an hour from Savannah.

## Visiting

The statewide GDC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Long Unit. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays — confirm the current hours with the facility

**Scheduling:** By appointment through GDC's online visitation portal; the visitor must first be approved on the person's list, and requests for a given week are accepted Monday through Wednesday
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Georgia](/states/georgia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on US Highway 84 east of Ludowici, in Long County in southeast Georgia, near Hinesville.

:::key-info
**By car:** Long Unit is east of Ludowici on US Highway 84; Hinesville is about 15 miles east, and Savannah is about an hour northeast

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Ludowici and nearby Hinesville have the closest gas, food, and lodging; Savannah, about an hour northeast, has the fullest range. The nearest hospitals with 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Hinesville and Savannah area.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's full name and **GDC ID number**. Georgia is moving toward centralized mail handling, so confirm the current mailing address before sending. Books and magazines must come from a publisher or established retailer, and legal mail is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/georgia/guides/mail/).
