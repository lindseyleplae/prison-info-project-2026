## Overview

The Colorado State Penitentiary is Colorado's **highest-security prison for men** — a **Level V (maximum-security)** facility. It opened in **1993** as the state's dedicated **administrative-segregation (solitary-confinement)** prison. In **2017, CDOC formally ended extended restrictive housing** as a policy, replacing long-term isolation with more out-of-cell time and programming; housing today spans several unit types, and conditions remain highly restrictive. CSP sits in the **East Cañon Correctional Complex**, a cluster of prisons near Cañon City that also includes Centennial and several others.

## What Makes Colorado State Penitentiary Different

- **It is the state's highest-security men's prison** (Level V), with the system's most restrictive visiting controls.
- **The visit type depends on the housing unit.** CSP offers both contact and non-contact visits, and which one applies turns on where the person is housed; it is confirmed when scheduling.
- **It was built as a solitary-confinement facility,** but Colorado ended administrative segregation as a formal policy in 2017; older descriptions of permanent 23-hour isolation are out of date, though the prison remains restrictive.
- **It is in the Cañon City prison cluster,** about a two-and-a-half-hour drive from Denver and an hour from Colorado Springs.

## Visiting

The statewide CDOC rules above — the approved list, the no-green dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Colorado State Penitentiary. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Scheduling:** By appointment, at least seven days ahead — call the visiting office at (719) 269-5252 or email; an approved visiting application must be on file first

**Contact or non-contact:** Both exist, and which applies depends on the person's housing unit — confirm their eligibility when scheduling

**Confirm the schedule:** Because CDOC publishes limited public detail for CSP and rules can change, confirm the current days, hours, and visit type with the visiting office before traveling
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Colorado](/states/colorado/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is in the East Cañon complex just outside Cañon City, in south-central Colorado.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 115 miles from Denver — roughly a 2 to 2.5-hour drive — and about an hour from Colorado Springs

**By transit:** Cañon City has very limited public transit and no passenger rail; a car is the practical way to get there
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Cañon City has gas, food, and lodging, with a larger selection in Colorado Springs about an hour away. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **CommonSpirit St. Thomas More Hospital** at 1338 Phay Avenue in Cañon City.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's **commitment name and DOC number**. **Legal mail** is opened only to inspect for contraband, in the person's presence. Full rules — including which prisons photocopy incoming mail and destroy the original — are in [Mail & Packages](/states/colorado/guides/mail/).
