## Overview

Ware State Prison, on Harris Road in Waycross, Ware County, is a **close-security state prison for men** in southeast Georgia. It is one of the state's higher-security men's prisons, with a capacity of about **1,546**. Programs at the facility include vocational and counseling offerings, a canine unit, and a corrections emergency response team.

## What Makes Ware State Prison Different

- **It is a close-security prison,** one of the higher-security custody levels in the Georgia system, so visiting and movement may be more restricted.
- **It is in southeast Georgia,** in Waycross, Ware County, near the Okefenokee Swamp.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's full name and **GDC ID number**. Georgia is moving toward centralized mail handling, so confirm the current mailing address before sending. Books and magazines must come from a publisher or established retailer, and legal mail is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/georgia/guides/mail/).
