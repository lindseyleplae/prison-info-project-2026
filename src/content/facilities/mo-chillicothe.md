## Overview

The Chillicothe Correctional Center (CCC), on Litton Road in Chillicothe, is **Missouri's main general-population prison for women**. It spans minimum to maximum security and holds about **1,700 women**. The current prison opened in 2008. It is a **separate prison from WERDCC**, the women's reception center in Vandalia, where women entering the state prison system are first received; most women transfer to Chillicothe or another prison after intake. Confirm which prison holds the person on the offender search before traveling.

## What Makes Chillicothe Different

- **It is Missouri's main general-population prison for women,** holding about 1,700 women across minimum to maximum security.

- **The current facility opened in 2008.**
- **It is in Chillicothe, in north-central Missouri,** northeast of Kansas City.

## Nearby Services

Chillicothe has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Hedrick Medical Center**, in Chillicothe.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
