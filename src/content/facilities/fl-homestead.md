## Overview

Homestead Correctional Institution is a **women's state prison** in the Florida City area of Miami-Dade County, South Florida, south of Miami. It is operated by the Florida Department of Corrections and holds women across the **full custody range — minimum, medium, and close**. Its reported capacity is roughly 668.

Homestead Correctional Institution is in the same general area as, but is a **separate facility from**, the neighboring **Dade Correctional Institution**, which is a men's prison. They are different institutions with different populations; confirm which facility holds the person on the FDC Corrections Offender Network.

The address and phone number listed for Homestead Correctional Institution above are corroborated from FDC sources but are not separately itemized in the state's June 2025 facilities directory. **Confirm the current mailing address and phone number on the FDC website and the Corrections Offender Network before traveling or sending mail.**

## What Makes Homestead Different

- **It is a South Florida women's institution,** in the Florida City area of Miami-Dade County.
- **It holds women in minimum, medium, and close custody.**
- **It is a separate facility from the neighboring Dade Correctional Institution** (a men's prison) in the same general area.
- **The institution shown in the offender search can change** if the person is transferred.

## Mail

Florida does not deliver original personal mail to the prison. Routine personal letters and photos go to an off-site **Smart Communications** processing center, where they are **opened and scanned** and delivered electronically — the person reads them on a tablet or kiosk, and the mailroom may print copies. Address routine mail with the person's **full name and DC number**, to: **Smart Communications, P.O. Box 23608, Tampa, FL 33623**. Confirm the current address and format on FDC's website before sending. **Legal mail and privileged correspondence go directly to the institution**, not to the scanning center. **Publications** must come from an approved publisher or retailer. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/florida/guides/mail/).
