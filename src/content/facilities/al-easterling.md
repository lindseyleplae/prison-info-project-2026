## Overview

Easterling Correctional Facility, on Wallace Drive in Clio, Barbour County, is a **medium-security men's prison** in southeast Alabama, holding about **1,398 men**. Its programs center on **drug-treatment counseling**, alongside trades training through **Wallace Community College** and **Ingram State Technical College** (electrical, drafting and design, carpentry, plumbing, brick masonry, and cabinet making). It opened in **March 1990**.

## What Makes Easterling Different

- **It is a medium-security men's prison** whose programs center on drug-treatment counseling.
- **It offers trades training** through Wallace Community College and Ingram State Technical College.
- **It is in rural Barbour County,** southeast of Montgomery and west of Eufaula.
- **The nearest full-service town is Eufaula,** about 25 miles northeast.

## Visiting

The statewide ADOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the no-tan dress code, ID, and the $30 vending limit — apply at Easterling. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Schedule:** ADOC does not post Easterling's visiting days or hours online; under the 2025 visitation rules (AR 303), all visits are pre-scheduled and approved

**How to confirm:** Call the facility at (334) 397-4471 for the current days, hours, and process

**List:** The person must add the visitor to their approved list first (up to eight approved adults)
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Alabama](/states/alabama/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Wallace Drive in Clio, in rural Barbour County, southeast of Montgomery.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 70–80 miles southeast of Montgomery (U.S. 231 south toward Troy, then state routes east to Clio); about 25 miles southwest of Eufaula and about 45 miles north of Dothan

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Eufaula, about 25 miles northeast, has the nearest cluster of hotels, restaurants, and services; Dothan, about 45 miles south, is the largest nearby city. The nearest hospital emergency room is **Medical Center Barbour** in Eufaula, the only hospital in Barbour County.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since late 2025, ADOC routes personal letters, cards, and photos to an **off-site scanning center in San Antonio, Texas**, addressed with the person's name and **AIS number**; the person receives the mail as **images on a tablet**. **Legal mail and packages** still go to the facility. Full addressing details are in [Mail & Packages](/states/alabama/guides/mail/).
