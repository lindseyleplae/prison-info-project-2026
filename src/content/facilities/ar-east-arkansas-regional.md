## Overview

The East Arkansas Regional Unit, near Marianna in the Delta, is a large **regional prison** holding men in **minimum, medium, and maximum** custody, with a rated capacity of about 1,648 (established 1992). It runs field crops and an agricultural operation, a jail operation, and building-trades and education programs. Because it holds a maximum-security component, a person here may be in a higher-security setting than at a typical regional unit.

## What Makes East Arkansas Different

- **It holds the full custody range, including maximum security,** so visiting rules depend on the person's status.
- **It is remote,** in the Delta about 17 miles southeast of Forrest City and roughly 80 miles east of Pine Bluff — the drive is the defining logistical fact for visitors.
- **It runs agricultural and building-trades programs** and operates a jail.
- **There is no public transit** to the unit.

## Visiting

The statewide ADC rules above — the approved visitor list, the no-white dress code, ID, and pre-scheduled visits — apply at East Arkansas.

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturdays and Sundays for routine visits; how many visits a month depends on the inmate's class

**Scheduling:** Request a visit through the TeleGov online portal, or call the unit's Visitation Clerk

**Maximum security:** For people in the maximum-security area, visits may be restricted or non-contact — confirm with the unit

**Hours:** ADC does not post the unit's specific visiting hours online — call (870) 295-4700 to confirm
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Arkansas](/states/arkansas/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The unit is near Marianna in Lee County, reached through the Arkansas Delta.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 17 miles southeast of Forrest City (off Interstate 40) and about 80 miles east of Pine Bluff

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the unit; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Forrest City, about 17 miles northwest, has the nearest cluster of hotels, restaurants, and services. The nearest hospital emergency room is **Forrest City Medical Center** in Forrest City; Helena Regional Medical Center to the south is another 24-hour option.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the unit. Since September 2025, ADC routes non-legal personal letters and photos to an **off-site scanning center in Tampa, Florida**, addressed with the person's name and **ADC number**; the person receives the mail electronically. **Legal mail, books, and magazines** still go directly to the unit at the Marianna address. Full addressing details are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arkansas/guides/mail/).
