## Overview

MCI-Norfolk, on Clark Street in Norfolk, is the **largest prison in Massachusetts** — a medium-security facility for men, southwest of Boston near Walpole. It opened in 1927, originally as the Norfolk Prison Colony. The Massachusetts DOC does not publish per-facility population counts, but Norfolk is the largest of the state's prisons.

## What Makes MCI-Norfolk Different

- **It is the largest prison in Massachusetts,** a medium-security facility for men.
- **It opened in 1927** as the Norfolk Prison Colony.
- **It is southwest of Boston** in the town of Norfolk, near Walpole.
- **Visiting is scheduled by phone** with the facility, which sets its own days and hours.

## Visiting

The statewide MA DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at MCI-Norfolk. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Scheduling:** Visits are scheduled by phone with the facility — there is no statewide online scheduler; confirm the days, hours, and how far ahead to call

**Visit type:** Contact and video visits are available — confirm the current arrangements with the facility
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Massachusetts](/states/massachusetts/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Clark Street in Norfolk, southwest of Boston.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Norfolk, reached from Route 1A and Route 115, southwest of Boston near Walpole; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The Norfolk and Walpole areas have gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Sturdy Memorial Hospital** in Attleboro, about 13 miles away.

## Mail

Incoming personal mail goes to the prison — Massachusetts does not use an off-site mail vendor. Address it with the person's full name and commitment number and the facility's mailing address (above); all incoming mail is opened and inspected. **Legal mail** is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/massachusetts/guides/mail/).
