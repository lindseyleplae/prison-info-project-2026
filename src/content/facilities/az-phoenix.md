## Overview

ASPC-Phoenix, on East Van Buren Street in Phoenix, is the **statewide reception and classification complex for men**. Its **Alhambra unit** is where newly committed men are received, assessed (medical, mental health, and custody classification), and then assigned and transferred to a permanent unit elsewhere in the system. Because of that role, a man may be here only briefly, and the complex shown in the locator can change within the first weeks. The complex also operates **mental-health housing units**. Like all ADCRR prisons, it is organized into units, and visiting is arranged at the unit level.

## What Makes ASPC-Phoenix Different

- **Its Alhambra unit is the statewide men's reception and classification center,** so a newly committed man usually begins here before transferring.
- **Visiting is restricted during the reception period,** so confirm the person's status and unit before traveling.
- **It includes mental-health housing units,** where visiting arrangements may differ — confirm with the unit.
- **It is in central Phoenix,** more centrally located than many Arizona complexes.

## Visiting

The statewide ADCRR rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at ASPC-Phoenix. The complex's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Application to Visit an Inmate before visiting

**Reception status:** A man in the Alhambra reception unit has restricted visiting and will transfer to a permanent unit after classification

**By unit:** Visiting days, hours, and rules are set by the specific unit — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Arizona](/states/arizona/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The complex is on East Van Buren Street in Phoenix.

:::key-info
**By car:** In central Phoenix, reached from Interstate 10 and State Route 51 via Van Buren Street

**By transit:** A private vehicle is the most reliable way to reach the complex; confirm any local transit options
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Phoenix has the full range of gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in central Phoenix, including **Banner-University Medical Center Phoenix**.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since December 2025, Arizona routes incoming personal mail to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Dallas, Texas**, where it is scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address it to **[person's name and ADCRR number], P.O. Box 211309, Dallas, TX 75211**. **Legal mail, publications, and parcels** still go directly to the complex. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arizona/guides/mail/).
