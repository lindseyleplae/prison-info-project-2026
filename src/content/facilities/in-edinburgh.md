## Overview

Edinburgh Correctional Facility, on the Camp Atterbury grounds near Edinburgh, is a **minimum-security prison for men** in central Indiana. IDOC does not publish a current population count for the facility, which is described here by security level rather than a number.

## What Makes Edinburgh Different

- **It is a minimum-security facility,** which differs from the higher-security prisons elsewhere in the state.
- **It sits on the Camp Atterbury grounds,** a state military training site, so access routes pass through that area.
- **It holds only men.**

## Visiting

The statewide IDOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the Edinburgh Correctional Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Registration:** Visitors must register and be approved on the person's visitor list through ViaPath before scheduling

**Scheduling:** Each visit is scheduled at idoc.gtlvisitme.com, using Eastern time

**Right facility:** Confirm Edinburgh Correctional Facility holds the person before traveling
:::

The full registration process is in [Visiting in Indiana](/states/indiana/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on the Camp Atterbury grounds near Edinburgh, in central Indiana.

:::key-info
**By car:** Near Edinburgh, off Interstate 65 south of Indianapolis, on the Camp Atterbury grounds; Columbus and Franklin are the nearest larger cities

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The Columbus and Franklin areas have the full range of gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in **Columbus** and **Franklin**.

## Mail

Incoming personal mail goes to the prison, but the incarcerated person receives a **photocopy**, not the original. Indiana does not use an off-site mail vendor — each prison's mailroom opens and **photocopies** incoming letters and photos in black and white and delivers the copy. Address mail with the person's full name and IDOC number to the facility's mailing address (above). **Legal mail** is opened in the person's presence, and **books** must be new and shipped directly from a publisher or retailer. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/indiana/guides/mail/).
