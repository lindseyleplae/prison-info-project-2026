## Overview

Green Bay Correctional Institution (GBCI) is a maximum-security state prison for men in the village of Allouez, in the Green Bay area of Brown County, northeastern Wisconsin. It is operated by the Wisconsin Department of Corrections through its Division of Adult Institutions. The prison sits on Riverside Drive along the Fox River. Wisconsin has no death penalty, abolished in 1853, so there is no death row or execution chamber at any state institution.

## What Makes Green Bay Different

- **It is one of Wisconsin's older maximum-security prisons.** GBCI holds men at the maximum-security custody level.
- **It is slated for closure under a 2025 state plan.** State officials have proposed closing Green Bay around 2029 as part of a broader corrections realignment. As of mid-2026, only planning funds had been approved, and the prison remains open and operating. The timeline and details may change; the WI DOC offender locator reflects each person's current institution.

## Visiting

The statewide WI DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Green Bay Correctional Institution. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Schedule:** Days and hours are set by the institution; Wisconsin has no statewide online visit scheduler

**How to confirm:** Call the facility at (920) 432-4877 for the current visiting days, hours, and process

**List:** The person must add the visitor to their approved list first — the person mails a DOC-21AA Visitor Questionnaire to the prospective visitor, who completes it and returns it to the institution

**Video:** Video visits are scheduled separately through ICSolutions
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Wisconsin](/states/wisconsin/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is at 2833 Riverside Drive in the village of Allouez, just south of the city of Green Bay, in Brown County in northeastern Wisconsin.

:::key-info
**By car:** In the Green Bay metropolitan area along the Fox River; reached from Interstate 43 and U.S. Highway 41/141 through Green Bay and Allouez

**By transit:** A private vehicle is the most direct way to reach the prison; confirm any local transit options separately
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The Green Bay area has the nearest gas, food, and lodging. The nearest hospital emergency rooms are in Green Bay, a few miles north.

## Mail

Incoming personal mail does not go to the prison. Wisconsin sends personal mail to an off-site vendor, **TextBehind**, which opens and photocopies it and forwards copies to the institution; people with tablets may also receive electronic photos. Address personal mail with the person's full name, **DOC number**, and the full (unabbreviated) facility name to: TextBehind, P.O. Box 189, Phoenix, MD 21131. **Legal and privileged mail** is exempt and goes directly to the institution at its own legal-mail address. **Books and other publications** must come from an approved vendor, shipped directly from the retailer, not from individuals. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/wisconsin/guides/mail/).
