## Overview

Missouri Eastern Correctional Center, on US Highway 66 in Pacific, is a **medium-security prison for men** in Franklin County, about 35 miles west of St. Louis. Visiting is arranged through the statewide Missouri DOC online application, and the facility sets its own visiting days and hours.

## What Makes Missouri Eastern Different

- **It is a medium-security men's prison** west of St. Louis, in Franklin County.
- **It sits in the eastern St. Louis-area cluster of Missouri prisons,** so confirming the current facility on the offender search matters before traveling.
- **Visiting days, hours, and visit type are set by the facility** — confirm them before traveling.

## Visiting

The statewide Missouri DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the Missouri Eastern Correctional Center. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Missouri DOC online visiting application before visiting

**Right facility:** The Missouri DOC operates several prisons in eastern Missouri — confirm which one holds the person before traveling

**Visit type:** Both in-person and video visits may be available — confirm the current options with the facility

**Schedule:** The facility sets its own visiting days and hours — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Missouri](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on US Highway 66 in Pacific, in Franklin County, about 35 miles west of St. Louis.

:::key-info
**By car:** Pacific is about 35 miles west of St. Louis off Interstate 44 in Franklin County; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Pacific and the western St. Louis suburbs have gas, food, and lodging. Hospitals with emergency rooms operate in the St. Louis metropolitan area to the east.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
