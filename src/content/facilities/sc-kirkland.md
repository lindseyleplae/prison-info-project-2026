## Overview

Kirkland Correctional Institution, on Broad River Road in Columbia, is South Carolina's **statewide reception and evaluation center for men**. Most men entering SCDC custody are received here, where they are assessed (medical, mental health, and custody classification) and then assigned and transferred to a permanent institution. Because of that role, a man may be here only briefly, and the institution shown in the inmate search can change within the first weeks. The Kirkland campus also includes a **maximum-custody unit**, an infirmary, and the **Gilliam Psychiatric Hospital**, the state's inpatient psychiatric facility for incarcerated men. People in reception status have **limited privileges** until classification is complete.

## What Makes Kirkland Different

- **It is the statewide men's intake center,** so a newly committed man usually begins here before transferring.
- **Visiting is limited during the reception period,** so confirm the person's status and institution before traveling.
- **The campus includes the Gilliam Psychiatric Hospital** and a maximum-custody unit, where visiting arrangements may differ — confirm with the institution.
- **The institution shown in the inmate search can change** once the person is classified and transferred.

## Visiting

The statewide SCDC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Kirkland Correctional Institution. The institution's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** The incarcerated person requests visitors from inside using SCDC Form 19-127 before anyone can visit

**Reception status:** A man in reception has limited visiting and will transfer to a permanent institution after classification

**Scheduling:** Visits are scheduled through the SCDC/ViaPath scheduler (scdoc.gtlvisitme.com) — confirm the current days and hours before traveling
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in South Carolina](/states/south-carolina/guides/visiting/).

The campus is on Broad River Road in Columbia, northwest of downtown.

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Columbia has the full range of gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Columbia area.

## Mail

South Carolina does not use an off-site mail vendor — **incoming personal mail goes directly to the institution**, where it is opened and inspected before delivery. Address it with the person's **full committed name and SCDC number**, the living unit, and the institution's address. **Cash, stamps, and writing materials are not allowed** in the mail. **Legal mail** is opened in the person's presence. Since **October 1, 2025**, **publications (books and magazines) must be sent directly from an approved vendor**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/south-carolina/guides/mail/).
