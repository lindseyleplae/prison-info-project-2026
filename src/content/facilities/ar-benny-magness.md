## Overview

The Benny Magness Unit, near Calico Rock in north-central Arkansas, is a **minimum- and medium-security men's prison** with a rated capacity of about 800. It was known as the **North Central Unit** until the Board of Corrections renamed it for former board chairman Benny Magness (dedicated in 2026). It runs a large agricultural operation — a vegetable garden, an apple orchard, forage production, and an equine program — along with vocational and Paws in Prison programs.

## What Makes the Benny Magness Unit Different

- **It was recently renamed** from the North Central Unit, so records, mail, and navigation may still use the old name.
- **It is one of the most remote units in the system,** about 100 miles (roughly two hours) north of Little Rock.
- **It runs a large agricultural and equine operation,** including an orchard and field crops.
- In **2025, an incarcerated man escaped from the unit and was recaptured about two weeks later;** ADC attributed the escape to staff error.

## Visiting

The statewide ADC rules above — the approved visitor list, the no-white dress code, ID, and pre-scheduled visits — apply at the Benny Magness Unit.

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturdays and Sundays for routine visits; how many visits a month depends on the inmate's class

**Scheduling:** Request a visit through the TeleGov online portal, or call the unit's Visitation Clerk

**Hours:** ADC does not post the unit's specific visiting hours online — call (870) 297-4311 to confirm
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Arkansas](/states/arkansas/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The unit is near Calico Rock in Izard County, reached by state highways into north-central Arkansas.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 100 miles (roughly two hours) north of Little Rock via Highway 5; the final approach is on local roads near Calico Rock

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the unit; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Calico Rock and nearby Mountain Home have the nearest gas, food, and lodging. The nearest hospital emergency room is **Izard Regional Hospital** (Izard County Medical Center) in Calico Rock; higher-level care is in the Mountain Home or Batesville area.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the unit. Since September 2025, ADC routes non-legal personal letters and photos to an **off-site scanning center in Tampa, Florida**, addressed with the person's name and **ADC number**; the person receives the mail electronically. **Legal mail, books, and magazines** still go directly to the unit at the Calico Rock address. Full addressing details are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arkansas/guides/mail/).
