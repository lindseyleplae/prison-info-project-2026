## Overview

Arrowhead Correctional Center is a **Level II minimum-restricted men's facility** on the East Cañon Complex, about two miles east of Cañon City in Fremont County. It opened in **1989** and, with the neighboring Four Mile Correctional Center, makes up the **Cañon Minimum Centers**, which operate under one administration. Arrowhead houses an **accredited drug-and-alcohol therapeutic community** and supports Colorado Correctional Industries work programs, including canteen, service-station, transportation, and K-9 operations.

## What Makes Arrowhead Different

- **Routine visiting is by video or pre-approved special visit only** — there is no standard in-person contact visiting.
- **It houses an accredited drug-and-alcohol therapeutic community.**
- **With Four Mile, it forms the Cañon Minimum Centers** under one administration.
- **Visitors register first at the East Cañon Complex Visiting Center** before going to the facility.

## Visiting

The statewide CDOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Arrowhead. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Format:** Routine visits are by video or pre-approved special visit only — there is no standard in-person contact visiting

**Scheduling:** By appointment, scheduled at least a week in advance; an approved visiting application must be on file. Register through the East Cañon Complex Visiting Center — email DOC_ecc-visiting@state.co.us, or call (719) 269-5603 for the current process

**Hours:** By appointment, seven days a week; confirm the available times when scheduling
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Colorado](/states/colorado/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The facility is on the East Cañon Complex, off U.S. 50 east of Cañon City.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 45 miles southwest of Colorado Springs (Interstate 25 south to U.S. 50 west) and about 115 miles south of Denver; the East Cañon Complex Visiting Center is about two miles east of Cañon City, on the south side of U.S. 50 between mile markers 283 and 284

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the complex; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Cañon City has the nearest gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **St. Thomas More Hospital** in Cañon City.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's **commitment name and DOC number**. **Arrowhead is one of the CDOC facilities that photocopies incoming personal mail, delivers the copy, and destroys the original** — so do not send originals you want returned. **Legal mail** is opened only to inspect for contraband, in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/colorado/guides/mail/).
