## Overview

Roxbury Correctional Institution, in Hagerstown, is a **medium-security prison for men** in the Hagerstown/Roxbury corrections cluster in western Maryland, Washington County. The facility holds men in **medium custody**. Confirm the person's current facility before traveling.

## What Makes RCI Different

- **It is a medium-security men's prison** in western Maryland.
- **It is in the Hagerstown/Roxbury corrections cluster,** alongside other state facilities in Washington County.
- **Visits are arranged after the visitor is approved,** so confirm the person's status and the current schedule before traveling.

## Visiting

The statewide DPSCS rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Roxbury Correctional Institution. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Scheduling:** Visits are arranged with the facility (no statewide online scheduler); free video visits are available through Microsoft Teams — confirm the current days and hours
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in Maryland](/states/maryland/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

The Hagerstown area has the full range of gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Hagerstown area.

## Mail

Maryland does not use an off-site mail vendor — **incoming personal mail goes directly to the facility**. Address it with the person's **committed name and SID (State Identification) number** and the facility's inmate-mail address. **Books must be new paperbacks ordered from an approved vendor**; hardcovers are not accepted. **Cash, checks, and money orders cannot be mailed** to the person — money is sent through Access Corrections. **Legal mail** is opened in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/maryland/guides/mail/).
