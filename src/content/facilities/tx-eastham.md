## Overview

The Eastham Unit is a men's TDCJ prison near Lovelady in Houston County, set in the Trinity River bottomlands of East Texas north of Huntsville. It is one of the system's older agricultural units. TDCJ lists the unit at about 2,464 beds.

TDCJ has renamed the facility the J. Dale Wainwright Unit, and the official unit directory now lists it under that name; "Eastham" remains the long-used name for the same prison. The TDCJ inmate locator and unit directory show the current designation and the exact unit a person is assigned to.

## What Makes Eastham Unit Different

-   One of TDCJ's older agricultural units, with farm operations attached to the prison
-   Located in the Trinity River bottomlands of Houston County, reached by farm-to-market roads rather than a highway grid
-   Officially renamed the J. Dale Wainwright Unit; the TDCJ directory and locator list the current name, while "Eastham" remains in common use for the same facility
-   Houses a broad G1-G5 custody mix, so the visiting format follows the incarcerated person's classification rather than one unit-wide rule

## Visiting Hours and Procedures

The statewide rules above — approval, ID, prohibited items, and dress code — apply at Eastham Unit. What follows is specific to this unit.

Eastham uses TDCJ's standard weekend visitation, held on Saturdays and Sundays. Visits are scheduled through the TDCJ Online Visitation Portal, and the portal shows the unit's current time slots and any session limits.

The unit's custody mix is the main reason one posted schedule does not mean one visiting experience. General-population housing typically uses standard contact visiting, while more restrictive housing can mean different room assignments or non-contact procedures on the same weekend. The format follows the incarcerated person's classification, so two families arriving the same morning may have different experiences.

## Getting There and Parking

Eastham is in a rural section of Houston County. The drive finishes on farm-to-market roads, and services thin out over the last stretch.

:::key-info
**From Huntsville:** North on highways toward Trinity, then the FM-road approach toward Lovelady; often about 40 minutes

**From Lufkin:** West and southwest connectors toward the Lovelady area, then rural roads; often about 1 hour

**From Houston:** North on US 59/I-69 and connectors toward Trinity, then FM roads; often about 1.75 hours

**Parking:** Visitor parking is available at the unit.

**Entry:** Check-in is at the unit's visitor processing point, where ID review and standard TDCJ screening happen before movement into the visiting area.
:::

There is no fixed-route bus to the unit, and the rural roads into the area are not served by scheduled transit, so the trip is by car.

The nearest hospital emergency rooms are not adjacent to the unit. CHRISTUS Trinity Mother Frances Hospital in Crockett operates a 24-hour emergency room, and 24-hour emergency rooms also operate in Huntsville, including Huntsville Memorial Hospital.

## Phones, Mail, and Video

Contact has a sequence to it. A phone number must be on the incarcerated person's approved contact list before calls can connect, and after intake or a transfer that approval can take time to process. Calls are placed by the incarcerated person on TDCJ's Securus phone system; a prison cannot be called into. Account setup, rates, and video options are covered in [Phone & Video Calls](/states/texas/guides/phone-video/).

## Nearby Services

Lovelady is a small town with limited commercial services. Crockett, the Houston County seat to the north, and Huntsville to the south are the nearest service centers for gas, food, groceries, and lodging, and options thin out on the final farm-to-market approach to the unit. Hotel sites list chain properties in Huntsville and Crockett.

For support beyond the unit itself, the Texas Inmate Families Association is a statewide nonprofit focused on education, advocacy, and prison-system navigation resources for families with someone in TDCJ custody.
