## Overview

ASPC-Perryville, on North Citrus Road in Goodyear, is **Arizona's state prison complex for women** — it has held the state's female prison population since 2000. It is also the **women's reception center**, where newly committed women are received and classified, and it includes the **Lumley Unit**, which houses women under a death sentence. The complex is made up of several units across different custody levels, and visiting is arranged at the unit level.

## What Makes ASPC-Perryville Different

- **It is Arizona's only women's prison complex,** holding essentially the entire female population.
- **It is the women's reception center,** so a newly committed woman begins here before classification.
- **The Lumley Unit holds women under a death sentence,** where visiting arrangements may differ — confirm with the unit.
- **It is in the west Phoenix metro,** in Goodyear.

## Visiting

The statewide ADCRR rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at ASPC-Perryville. The complex's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Application to Visit an Inmate before visiting

**Reception status:** A woman in reception has restricted visiting until classification

**By unit:** Visiting days, hours, and rules are set by the specific unit — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Arizona](/states/arizona/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

Goodyear and the west Phoenix metro have the full range of gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Abrazo West Campus** in Goodyear.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since December 2025, Arizona routes incoming personal mail to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Dallas, Texas**, where it is scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address it to **[person's name and ADCRR number], P.O. Box 211309, Dallas, TX 75211**. **Legal mail, publications, and parcels** still go directly to the complex. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arizona/guides/mail/).
