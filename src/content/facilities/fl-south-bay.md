## Overview

South Bay Correctional and Rehabilitation Facility is a **men's prison** in South Bay, in Palm Beach County, in south Florida near the southwestern shore of Lake Okeechobee. It is privately operated by the GEO Group under contract with the Florida Department of Corrections, holds men in mixed custody, and has a capacity of about 1,948.

## What Makes South Bay Different

- **It is privately operated by the GEO Group,** under a contract monitored by the Florida Department of Management Services. Everyone held here remains in FDC custody and appears on the FDC Corrections Offender Network.
- **It offers academic and vocational programs,** including commercial driver's license (CDL) training and a PRIDE Industries print shop.
- **It runs a residential substance-abuse program,** a DCF-licensed therapeutic community, along with counseling.
- **Money is handled through the operator's path.** Phone accounts and deposits run through ViaPath and ConnectNetwork rather than the standard FDC vendors; confirm the current method in the [Sending Money](/states/florida/guides/money/) guide before sending funds.

## Mail

Florida does not deliver original personal mail to the prison. Routine personal letters and photos go to an off-site **Smart Communications** processing center, where they are **opened and scanned** and delivered electronically — the person reads them on a tablet or kiosk, and the mailroom may print copies. Address routine mail with the person's **full name and DC number**, to: **Smart Communications, P.O. Box 23608, Tampa, FL 33623**. Confirm the current address and format on FDC's website before sending. **Legal mail and privileged correspondence go directly to the institution**, not to the scanning center. **Publications** must come from an approved publisher or retailer. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/florida/guides/mail/).
