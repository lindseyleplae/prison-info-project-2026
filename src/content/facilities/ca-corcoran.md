**Location:** Corcoran in Kings County, in the central San Joaquin Valley.

**Capacity:** About 5,900 rated beds across general population and specialized housing units.

**Notable feature:** Multi-mission institution with Level I, III, and IV housing, a correctional treatment center, and several restricted-housing units.

## Overview

California State Prison, Corcoran is one of CDCR's largest and most complex institutions. CDCR describes it as a multi-mission site with Level I, Level III, and Level IV housing, a correctional treatment center, prison industry operations, and several specialized custody units.

The institution's scale and custody mix shape both movement and visiting. Standard mainline visiting runs on a general Friday-Sunday schedule, while several restricted or specialized units use separate appointment rules.

## Visiting Basics

Corcoran's current general schedule is Friday from 12:10 p.m. to 6:30 p.m. and Saturday-Sunday from 8:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. The visiting status page also lists Monday visits from 10:55 a.m. to 1:15 p.m. for appointment-only populations.

CDCR states that Facilities 3A, 3B, and 4B use appointment windows in the morning, while Facility 4A, the correctional treatment center, and other specialized units require advance scheduling. Corcoran also notes that all non-contact visits are one hour long. General statewide requirements remain in [Visiting in California](/states/california/guides/visiting/).

## Directions

From Fresno, the practical route is south on CA-99, then west toward the Hanford-Corcoran area using CA-198 and CA-43 before the final local approach to King Avenue. Most of the drive is on major valley highways, with the last segment on local agricultural roads.

## Parking and Entry

Parking is available on-site. Corcoran's visiting page states that walk-in visitors for eligible yards may drive onto institutional grounds during posted afternoon processing windows, while Facility 4A, the correctional treatment center, and other designated units do not accept walk-ins.

Visitors assigned to a specialized unit should confirm the appointment type before departure. Corcoran also directs travelers to check road conditions when weather or travel advisories affect the San Joaquin Valley.

## Nearby Services

Corcoran has basic fuel and food options in town, and larger selections are available in Hanford along the CA-43 and CA-198 corridor. Lodging is more limited in Corcoran than in larger valley cities, so many overnight visitors use hotels in Hanford or Fresno.
