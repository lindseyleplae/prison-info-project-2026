## Overview

Skyline Correctional Center is the **only Level I minimum facility in the Cañon City area**, on the East Cañon Complex in Fremont County. It began operating in **1957**, sat vacant from December 2020 to October 2022, and then reopened as **"The Beacon at Skyline"** — a redesigned, single-cell facility built around a "human-centered" program model with a new organizational structure. Among its features, a group of residents work as **"Community Advocates"** who guide their peers and work alongside staff.

## What Makes Skyline Different

- **It is the only Level I (minimum) facility in the Cañon City area.**
- **It reopened in 2022 as "The Beacon at Skyline,"** a single-cell, program-focused model.
- **A group of residents serve as "Community Advocates,"** working alongside staff.
- **CDOC does not publish a general switchboard for Skyline** — the visiting center is the main contact.

## Visiting

The statewide CDOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Skyline. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Friday, Saturday, and Sunday, 9:30 a.m.–3:30 p.m.; a person may be visited Friday + Saturday or Friday + Sunday, but not Saturday + Sunday

**Scheduling:** By appointment, a week ahead — call the Skyline Visiting Center at (719) 269-5405 (Mon–Fri 8:00 a.m.–4:30 p.m.) or email DOC_SCC_visiting@state.co.us; an approved visiting application must be on file

**Limit:** Up to five visitors (adults and children), because of visiting-room capacity
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Colorado](/states/colorado/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The facility is on the East Cañon Complex, off U.S. 50 east of Cañon City.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 45 miles southwest of Colorado Springs (Interstate 25 south to U.S. 50 west) and about 115 miles south of Denver; the East Cañon Complex is about two miles east of Cañon City on U.S. 50

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the complex; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Cañon City has the nearest gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **St. Thomas More Hospital** in Cañon City.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's **commitment name and DOC number**. **Legal mail** is opened only to inspect for contraband, in the person's presence. Some CDOC facilities photocopy incoming personal mail and destroy the original; the current list is in [Mail & Packages](/states/colorado/guides/mail/).
