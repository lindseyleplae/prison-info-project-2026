## Overview

The Fulton Reception and Diagnostic Center, on State Route O in Fulton, is a **reception and diagnostic center for men** in central Missouri. It is one of three regional intake points — with Bonne Terre and St. Joseph — where newly committed men are received, screened for medical and mental health, classified, and then transferred to a permanent prison. It is a minimum-security/diagnostic facility and holds about **1,500 men**. Because of its reception role, a man may be here only briefly, and the prison shown in the offender search can change after classification.

## What Makes Fulton Different

- **It is a men's reception and diagnostic center** for central Missouri, so a newly committed man may begin here before transferring.
- **It is one of three regional intake points** in the state, along with Bonne Terre and St. Joseph.
- **A man may be here only briefly,** and the prison shown in the offender search can change after classification.
- **It is in Fulton, in central Missouri,** between Columbia and Jefferson City.

## Visiting

The statewide Missouri DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the Fulton Reception and Diagnostic Center. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Missouri DOC online visiting application before visiting

**Reception status:** A man in reception has limited visiting and may transfer to a permanent prison after classification

**Schedule:** The facility sets its own visiting days and hours — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Missouri](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on State Route O in Fulton, in central Missouri.

:::key-info
**By car:** Fulton is reached from Interstate 70 in central Missouri, between Columbia and Jefferson City; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Fulton has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest full-service emergency rooms are in Columbia and Jefferson City, each about 25 miles away.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
