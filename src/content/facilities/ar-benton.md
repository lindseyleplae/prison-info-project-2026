## Overview

The Benton Unit, in Benton in Saline County, is a small **minimum-custody work-release unit** for men, with a rated capacity of about 325. Many people here leave during the day for **community jobs** and return at night, so a person may not be at the unit during weekday business hours. It runs vocational training, including welding and mechanics, and credential programs.

## What Makes the Benton Unit Different

- **It is a work-release unit,** so a resident may be at a job in the community on weekdays.
- **It is small** (about 325 beds) and close to Little Rock, in Benton.
- **It runs vocational training** in welding, mechanics, and food-safety credentials.

## Visiting

The statewide ADC rules above — the approved visitor list, the no-white dress code, ID, and pre-scheduled visits — apply at the Benton Unit.

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturdays and Sundays for routine visits; how many visits a month depends on the inmate's class

**Scheduling:** Request a visit through the TeleGov online portal, or call the unit's Visitation Clerk

**Hours:** ADC does not post the unit's specific visiting hours online — call (501) 315-2252 to confirm
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Arkansas](/states/arkansas/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The unit is on Highway 67 in Benton, southwest of Little Rock.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 25 miles southwest of Little Rock via Interstate 30 and U.S. 67

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the unit; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Benton and nearby Bryant have gas, food, and lodging, with more in the Little Rock area. The nearest hospital emergency room is **Saline Memorial Hospital** in Benton.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the unit. Since September 2025, ADC routes non-legal personal letters and photos to an **off-site scanning center in Tampa, Florida**, addressed with the person's name and **ADC number**; the person receives the mail electronically. **Legal mail, books, and magazines** still go directly to the unit at the Benton address. Full addressing details are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arkansas/guides/mail/).
