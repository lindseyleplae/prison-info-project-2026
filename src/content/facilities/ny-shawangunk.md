## Overview

Shawangunk Correctional Facility, in the hamlet of Wallkill, is a **maximum-security prison** for men in Ulster County, in New York's mid-Hudson Valley. Like all maximum-security facilities, visiting and movement are more controlled than at lower-security prisons.

Shawangunk Correctional Facility (maximum security) is a **separate prison** from the nearby **Wallkill Correctional Facility** (medium security), which is also in the hamlet of Wallkill. Confirm the exact facility on the DOCCS Incarcerated Lookup before traveling.

## What Makes Shawangunk Different

- **It is a maximum-security prison,** so visiting, movement, and property are more restricted.
- **It has unusual visiting days,** with the profile noting Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday rather than weekends only.
- **It is in the mid-Hudson Valley,** in Ulster County.
- **It is distinct from Wallkill Correctional Facility,** a separate, medium-security prison in the same hamlet.

## Visiting

The statewide DOCCS rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Shawangunk Correctional Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Days:** The profile notes visiting on Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday — confirm the current schedule

**Hours:** Visits are within the facility's posted hours — confirm the current schedule

**Screening:** A body scanner is required for a full-contact visit (a medical exemption can be requested)
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in New York](/states/new-york/guides/visiting/).

## Mail

New York does not use an off-site mail vendor — incoming personal mail is **opened and photocopied at the facility**, and the incarcerated person receives the **photocopies** (images may also reach their JPay tablet) rather than the original letters. Address it to the incarcerated person at **P.O. Box 700, Wallkill, NY 12589-0750**, with the person's **name and DIN (Department Identification Number)**. **Legal mail** goes to the facility and is handled under privileged-mail rules. **Books, publications, and packages** must be ordered from **approved vendors**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/new-york/guides/mail/).
