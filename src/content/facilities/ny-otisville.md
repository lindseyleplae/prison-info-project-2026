## Overview

Otisville Correctional Facility, in Otisville, is a **medium-security prison** for men in Orange County, in New York's mid-Hudson/Catskills region. It is a separate facility from the federal **FCI Otisville**, which is in the same area — this page covers the New York State (DOCCS) facility.

## What Makes Otisville Different

- **It is a medium-security prison,** with general-population housing and programming.
- **It is in the mid-Hudson/Catskills region,** in Orange County.
- **It shares a town with a federal prison,** FCI Otisville, which is run by a different agency.
- **The facility prioritizes visitors within 100 miles,** with extended-visit consideration for those traveling more than 100 miles — confirm the current policy before traveling.

## Visiting

The statewide DOCCS rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Otisville Correctional Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Hours:** Visits are within the facility's posted hours; some visits require advance scheduling — confirm the current schedule

**Screening:** A body scanner is required for a full-contact visit (a medical exemption can be requested)
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in New York](/states/new-york/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

Otisville and the surrounding Orange County area have gas, food, and lodging, with more in the Middletown area to the south. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Middletown area.

## Mail

New York does not use an off-site mail vendor — incoming personal mail is **opened and photocopied at the facility**, and the incarcerated person receives the **photocopies** (images may also reach their JPay tablet) rather than the original letters. Address it with the person's **name and DIN (Department Identification Number)** and the facility's address. **Legal mail** goes to the facility and is handled under privileged-mail rules. **Books, publications, and packages** must be ordered from **approved vendors**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/new-york/guides/mail/).
