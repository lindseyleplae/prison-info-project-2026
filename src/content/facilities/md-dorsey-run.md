## Overview

Dorsey Run Correctional Facility, in Jessup, is a **minimum-security and pre-release prison for men** in the central Maryland (Jessup) corrections cluster, Anne Arundel County. The facility holds men in **minimum custody** under a pre-release mission, which means some people work or participate in programs outside the facility. Confirm the person's current facility before traveling.

## What Makes Dorsey Run Different

- **It is a minimum-security and pre-release men's prison** in central Maryland.
- **It is in the central Maryland (Jessup) corrections cluster,** alongside other state facilities in the Jessup area.
- **A pre-release mission means some people work or participate in programs outside the facility,** so confirm the person's status and the current schedule before traveling.

## Visiting

The statewide DPSCS rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Dorsey Run Correctional Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Scheduling:** Visits are arranged with the facility (no statewide online scheduler); free video visits are available through Microsoft Teams — confirm the current days and hours
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in Maryland](/states/maryland/guides/visiting/).

## Mail

Maryland does not use an off-site mail vendor — **incoming personal mail goes directly to the facility**. Address it with the person's **committed name and SID (State Identification) number** and the facility's inmate-mail address. **Books must be new paperbacks ordered from an approved vendor**; hardcovers are not accepted. **Cash, checks, and money orders cannot be mailed** to the person — money is sent through Access Corrections. **Legal mail** is opened in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/maryland/guides/mail/).
