Apply online early (clearance takes weeks), and confirm where the person is held and the visiting schedule by calling **(434) 767-5543** before traveling.

## Overview

Nottoway Correctional Center, in Burkeville, is a **medium-security prison for men** that also serves as a **reception and classification center**. Many men entering a Virginia state sentence are received here first — given intake assessments, a medical screening, and a security-level classification — before being assigned (often transferred) to a permanent facility, chosen mainly by security level and bed space rather than nearness to home. Beyond intake, Nottoway houses a general-population men's population, with vocational training (HVAC, horticulture), education, and re-entry programs. A separate **Nottoway Work Center** operates on the same complex.

## What Makes Nottoway Different

- **It is a reception/intake center.** A newly sentenced man may begin his incarceration here, then be transferred elsewhere — so the first facility is often not the long-term one.
- **The 60-day no-visit window.** VADOC does not allow visits during the first 60 days of a person's assignment to a reception center — an important expectation right after sentencing.
- **A co-located work center.** Confirm whether a person is at Nottoway Correctional Center or the adjacent Nottoway Work Center; they are separate facilities at the same address.

## Visiting

The statewide VADOC rules above — the online application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Nottoway. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays — confirm the current hours with the facility

**New arrivals:** No visits during the first 60 days after a person is received at the reception center

**Video:** Video visits are offered, including from home on a computer or Android device
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Virginia](/states/virginia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Schutt Road in Burkeville, in Nottoway County.

:::key-info
**From Richmond:** About 55 miles — roughly 1 hour 10 minutes, generally via US-360

**From Farmville:** About 15 miles — roughly 20 minutes

**Transit:** No fixed-route public transit serves Burkeville; a vehicle is the practical way to reach the prison
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Burkeville is small; nearby Crewe and Blackstone (to the east) and **Farmville** (about 15 miles west) have gas, food, and lodging, with Farmville the largest hub. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Centra Southside Community Hospital** at 800 Oak Street in Farmville.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the facility at **P.O. Box 488, Burkeville, VA 23922**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go instead to the Central Mail Distribution Center in State Farm. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
