## Overview

North Central Correctional Facility (NCCF) is a minimum-security state prison for men operated by the Iowa Department of Corrections. It is in Rockwell City, the seat of Calhoun County, in north-central Iowa. The facility holds mainly low-risk men and opened in 1982.

## What Makes NCCF Different

NCCF is a general minimum-security facility with a strong vocational and educational emphasis, delivered in partnership with Iowa Central Community College. Programs include culinary arts, welding, woodwork, plumbing, master gardening, and GED/HiSET coursework. It is not a reception or medical center; newly committed men are received and classified at the Iowa Medical and Classification Center in Coralville and are assigned to NCCF or another prison after intake.

## Visiting

The statewide Iowa DOC rules — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID requirements, and item limits — apply at NCCF. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
- **Approved list first:** Before any visit, each visitor must be approved. Iowa establishes one approved visiting list per incarcerated individual through a Centralized Visiting Authority, and the list stays valid across transfers.
- **Scheduling:** After approval, visits are scheduled through the Ameelio Connect app. Confirm the current process before a first visit.
- **Days and hours:** Set by the facility — confirm the current visiting schedule directly before traveling.
- **Video visits:** Available through Ameelio, in addition to in-person visits.
- **Main line:** (712) 297-7521
:::

See [Visiting in Iowa](/states/iowa/guides/visiting/) for the approval process and the full statewide rules.

## Mail

Iowa does not deliver friends-and-family mail to the prison. Personal mail goes off-site to a vendor, Pigeonly, at a processing center in Las Vegas, Nevada, which opens, scans, and prints each item; the copy is delivered to the facility. Mail sent directly to the prison is returned to the sender. Each facility has its own numeric facility ID code that must appear on the envelope. The [Mail & Packages](/states/iowa/guides/mail/) guide has the exact Pigeonly address and the facility ID code to use.

Legal and privileged mail goes directly to the facility, not to Pigeonly. Verify the current mailing rules before sending anything.
