## Overview

West Tennessee State Penitentiary, on Green Chapel Road in Henning, is a **prison for men** in West Tennessee, across **minimum to maximum security**. It holds about **980 men** and opened in 1999, when it was the West Tennessee High Security Facility. The prison shares its grounds, street address, and main phone number with the **Women's Therapeutic Residential Center**, a separate women's prison, so the person's specific prison should be confirmed on the locator before traveling.

## What Makes West Tennessee State Penitentiary Different

- **It shares its grounds, street address, and main phone number with the Women's Therapeutic Residential Center,** a separate women's prison — confirm which of the two holds the person before traveling.
- **It spans minimum to maximum security** for men.
- **It opened in 1999,** formerly named the West Tennessee High Security Facility.
- **It is in Henning, in Lauderdale County,** north of Memphis in West Tennessee.

## Visiting

The statewide TDOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at West Tennessee State Penitentiary. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting; applications take about 30 days

**Right facility:** West Tennessee State Penitentiary and the Women's Therapeutic Residential Center are separate prisons on the same grounds — confirm which one holds the person before traveling

**Schedule:** The facility sets its own visiting days and hours, and Tennessee has no statewide online scheduler — confirm the schedule with the facility before traveling
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Tennessee](/states/tennessee/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Green Chapel Road in Henning, north of Memphis.

:::key-info
**By car:** Henning is reached via U.S. 51 north of Memphis

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Lauderdale Community Hospital** in Ripley, about 10 miles away; Memphis hospitals are about 50 miles away.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since November 2025, Tennessee routes incoming letters, cards, and photos to an **off-site digital-mail center**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[facility name], [person's name], TDOC ID# [number], P.O. Box 247, Phoenix, MD 21131**, with "TDOC ID#" immediately before the number. Mail sent directly to the prison is returned. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the institution. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/tennessee/guides/mail/).
