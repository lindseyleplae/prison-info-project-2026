## Overview

Broad River Correctional Institution, on Broad River Road in Columbia, is a **men's institution** that holds **South Carolina's men's death row and the state's execution chamber**, alongside general-population and special-management housing. It is a **close/maximum-custody** facility, and visiting arrangements differ depending on the person's unit. Men held in death-row or other high-custody housing may have **non-contact visits** and other restrictions that do not apply in general population.

## What Makes Broad River Different

- **It holds the men's death row and the state execution chamber,** so part of the institution operates under high-custody rules.
- **Visiting differs by unit,** and some housing is non-contact — confirm the person's unit before traveling.
- **It also houses general-population and special-management men,** so conditions and visiting can vary across the institution.
- **The institution shown in the inmate search can change** if the person is transferred.

## Visiting

The statewide SCDC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Broad River Correctional Institution. The institution's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** The incarcerated person requests visitors from inside using SCDC Form 19-127 before anyone can visit

**By unit:** Visiting for men in death-row or high-custody housing may be non-contact and differs by unit — confirm the person's unit and the current rules

**Scheduling:** Visits are scheduled through the SCDC/ViaPath scheduler (scdoc.gtlvisitme.com) — confirm the current days and hours before traveling
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in South Carolina](/states/south-carolina/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The campus is on Broad River Road in Columbia, northwest of downtown, in the Broad River Road prison cluster.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Columbia, Richland County, reached from Interstate 20 and Interstate 26 via Broad River Road; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** A private vehicle is the most reliable way to reach the institution; confirm any local transit options
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Columbia has the full range of gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Columbia area.

## Mail

South Carolina does not use an off-site mail vendor — **incoming personal mail goes directly to the institution**, where it is opened and inspected before delivery. Address it with the person's **full committed name and SCDC number**, the living unit, and the institution's address. **Cash, stamps, and writing materials are not allowed** in the mail. **Legal mail** is opened in the person's presence. Since **October 1, 2025**, **publications (books and magazines) must be sent directly from an approved vendor**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/south-carolina/guides/mail/).
