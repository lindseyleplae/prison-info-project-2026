## Overview

Dodge Correctional Institution, on West Lincoln Street in Waupun, is **Wisconsin's statewide reception and intake center for men** — the point where most newly sentenced men in the state are received, assessed (medical, mental health, and custody classification), and then assigned and transferred to a permanent prison. Because of that role, a man is usually here only briefly, and the facility shown in the locator changes once he is classified and transferred. The prison operates at **maximum security**.

Dodge also houses the Wisconsin prison system's **central medical infirmary** — an inpatient unit of about **60 beds** that provides infirmary-level care for men from across the state's institutions.

## What Makes Dodge Different

- **It is the men's statewide reception and intake center,** so most newly sentenced men in Wisconsin begin here before transferring to a permanent prison.
- **Security level is assigned during intake,** and the facility shown in the locator changes after classification and transfer.
- **It houses the central 60-bed men's infirmary,** providing inpatient medical care for men from institutions across the state.
- **It is in Waupun, Dodge County,** between Madison and Fond du Lac.

## Visiting

The statewide WI DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Dodge Correctional Institution. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approval first:** Every visitor must be on the person's approved visiting list — the person mails a DOC-21AA Visitor Questionnaire to the prospective visitor, who completes it and returns it to the institution

**Intake status:** Reception ("A&E") status may limit visits early on and caps visiting at up to **4 adult close-family** members — confirm the current rules with the institution

**Scheduling:** Wisconsin has no statewide online visit scheduler; visits are arranged directly with the institution. Video visits are scheduled separately through ICSolutions
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Wisconsin](/states/wisconsin/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

Dodge Correctional Institution is in Waupun, Dodge County, between Madison and Fond du Lac.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Waupun, reached by state highways from Madison to the southwest and Fond du Lac to the northeast

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the facility; a private vehicle is required

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Waupun and the surrounding Dodge County area have gas, food, and lodging, with more options in nearby Beaver Dam and Fond du Lac. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the **Fond du Lac and Beaver Dam** area.

## Mail

Incoming personal mail does not go to the prison. Wisconsin sends personal mail to an off-site vendor, **TextBehind**, which opens and photocopies it and forwards copies to the institution; people with tablets may also receive electronic photos. Address personal mail with the person's full name, **DOC number**, and the full (unabbreviated) facility name to: TextBehind, P.O. Box 189, Phoenix, MD 21131. **Legal and privileged mail** is exempt and goes directly to the institution at its own legal-mail address. **Books and other publications** must come from an approved vendor, shipped directly from the retailer, not from individuals. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/wisconsin/guides/mail/).
