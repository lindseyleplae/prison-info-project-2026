## Overview

Marion Correctional Treatment Center is VADOC's facility providing **acute psychiatric and residential mental-health treatment** for men in state custody, rather than general-population housing. It opened in 1980 in Marion, alongside the long-standing state psychiatric hospital (the Southwestern Virginia Mental Health Institute). In 2025 it received a three-year license from the **Virginia Department of Behavioral Health and Developmental Services**, and it is described as the first VADOC facility **accredited by the Joint Commission** — the same kinds of oversight that apply to civilian behavioral-health hospitals.

## What Makes Marion Different

- **It is a treatment facility.** Men are placed here for mental-health treatment and stabilization, so day-to-day life and visiting can differ from a standard prison.
- **State behavioral-health oversight.** It is licensed by the state behavioral-health authority and accredited by the Joint Commission.
- **Co-located with the state psychiatric hospital.** Marion has had a mental-health-care presence for well over a century.

## Visiting

The statewide VADOC rules above — the online application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Marion. Because this is a treatment facility, the visiting arrangement can differ:

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays — but visiting may depend on a person's treatment status, so confirm the current arrangement with the facility

**Video:** Video visits are offered, including from home on a computer or Android device
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Virginia](/states/virginia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The facility is on Wright Street in Marion, off I-81 in Smyth County.

:::key-info
**From Richmond:** About 280 miles — roughly 4 hours 15 minutes

**From the Abingdon/Bristol area:** About 30 miles — roughly 30 minutes

**Transit:** Mountain Lynx Transit runs local bus service in Marion, but it may not reach the facility; a vehicle is the practical way to get there
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Marion, on I-81, has gas, food, and lodging, including hotels near the interstate. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Smyth County Community Hospital** at 245 Medical Park Drive in Marion.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the facility at **110 Wright Street, Marion, VA 24354**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go instead to the Central Mail Distribution Center in State Farm. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
