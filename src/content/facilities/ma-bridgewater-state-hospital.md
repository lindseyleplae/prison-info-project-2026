## Overview

Bridgewater State Hospital, on Administration Road in Bridgewater, is a **secure forensic mental-health facility for men, operated by the Department of Correction**, with clinical care provided by a contracted health provider. It holds men committed for **psychiatric evaluation or treatment** — including people civilly committed and people sent for court-ordered competency or criminal-responsibility evaluations. Because it is a hospital rather than a general-population prison, its day-to-day operations and **visiting process differ** from other DOC facilities. It is one of several DOC facilities clustered at the Bridgewater complex.

## What Makes Bridgewater State Hospital Different

- **It is a secure forensic mental-health facility,** not a general-population prison; it holds men for psychiatric evaluation or treatment.
- **Its visiting process differs from a standard prison** — confirm the hospital's specific procedure before traveling.
- **It is one of several DOC facilities at the Bridgewater complex** (with the Old Colony Correctional Center and the Massachusetts Treatment Center) — confirm which one holds the person.
- **Clinical care is provided by a contracted health provider** working with the Department of Correction.

## Nearby Services

Bridgewater and nearby Brockton have gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Signature Healthcare Brockton Hospital** in Brockton.

## Mail

Incoming personal mail goes to the facility — Massachusetts does not use an off-site mail vendor. Address it with the person's full name and commitment number and the facility's mailing address (above); all incoming mail is opened and inspected. **Legal mail** is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/massachusetts/guides/mail/).
