## Overview

Pocatello Women's Correctional Center (PWCC), at 1451 Fore Road in Pocatello, holds women at all custody levels and operates the statewide women's **Reception and Diagnostic Center** — the intake point for women entering Idaho's prison system. It was the first Idaho facility designed specifically for women, opening in 1994.

Women enter the Idaho prison system through the Reception and Diagnostic Center here. Programs at the facility include vocational work, education, and pre-release and work-release programs.

PWCC is the designated facility for the one woman in Idaho under a death sentence; the state's execution chamber is at the Idaho Maximum Security Institution in Kuna, not here.

## Visiting

The statewide Idaho DOC visiting rules — the approved visitor list, the application and approval process, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Pocatello Women's Correctional Center. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Application first:** An approved visiting application is required before a visit. The resident requests and mails application forms to prospective visitors

**Schedule:** Visiting days and hours are set by the facility — confirm the current schedule with PWCC before traveling

**Before traveling:** Confirm the resident's current location on the Idaho DOC [Resident/Client Search](https://www.idoc.idaho.gov/content/prisons/resident-client-search)
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Idaho](/states/idaho/guides/visiting/).

## Communicating

Idaho DOC does not use an off-site mail vendor. Incoming personal mail goes directly to the facility, where it is inspected before delivery. Address personal mail with the resident's committed name and IDOC number, the facility name, and the facility address:

> [Resident's full name and IDOC number]
> Pocatello Women's Correctional Center
> 1451 Fore Road
> Pocatello, ID 83204

Phones run through ICSolutions, and residents use ViaPath tablets for tablet services and messaging. Details on setting up accounts and call costs are in [Phone & Video Calls](/states/idaho/guides/phone-video/), what you can send by mail is in [Mail & Packages](/states/idaho/guides/mail/), and how to add funds is in [Sending Money](/states/idaho/guides/money/).
