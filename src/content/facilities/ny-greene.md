## Overview

Greene Correctional Facility, in the town of Coxsackie, is a **medium-security prison** for men in Greene County, in the Hudson Valley. It has a Special Housing Unit (SHU) and a Residential Rehabilitation Unit (RRU), which have their own evening visiting window separate from the daytime general-population hours.

Greene Correctional Facility is a different prison from the nearby **Coxsackie Correctional Facility**, a maximum-security prison that is also in the town of Coxsackie. The two share the town and ZIP code but sit on different roads and use different incarcerated-mail boxes. Greene is also distinct from the unrelated "Green Haven" prison.

## What Makes Greene Different

- **It is a medium-security prison,** with less restricted visiting and movement than a maximum-security facility.
- **It has a Special Housing Unit (SHU) and a Residential Rehabilitation Unit (RRU),** which have a separate evening visiting window.
- **It is in the town of Coxsackie, Greene County,** in the Hudson Valley.
- **It is a separate prison from Coxsackie Correctional Facility,** which is maximum security and in the same town — confirm the exact facility before traveling.

## Visiting

The statewide DOCCS rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Greene Correctional Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Hours:** Weekend general-population visiting is divided by the **last digit of the person's DIN**, so the day a person may receive visitors depends on their DIN — confirm the current schedule

**Evening window:** SHU and RRU housing have a separate **evening visiting window** that is different from the daytime general-population hours; SHU/RRU visits are limited — confirm the current schedule

**Screening:** A body scanner is required for a full-contact visit (a medical exemption can be requested)
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in New York](/states/new-york/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

Coxsackie and the surrounding Greene County area have gas, food, and lodging, with more in the Catskill and Hudson areas nearby and additional options toward Albany to the north. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Hudson/Catskill and greater Albany areas.

## Mail

New York does not use an off-site mail vendor — incoming personal mail is **opened and photocopied at the facility**, and the incarcerated person receives the **photocopies** (images may also reach their JPay tablet) rather than the original letters. Address it with the person's **name and DIN (Department Identification Number)** and the facility's address. **Legal mail** goes to the facility and is handled under privileged-mail rules. **Books, publications, and packages** must be ordered from **approved vendors**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/new-york/guides/mail/).
