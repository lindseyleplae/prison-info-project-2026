## Overview

Woodbourne Correctional Facility, in Woodbourne, is a **medium-security prison** for men in Sullivan County, in the Catskills. It also operates a Regional Medical Unit (RMU), so some people are housed there for medical reasons rather than by security classification.

## What Makes Woodbourne Different

- **It operates a Regional Medical Unit (RMU),** which provides skilled and long-term medical care.
- **A person may be housed there for medical reasons,** so their unit can differ from a general-population assignment.
- **The RMU has its own visiting arrangements,** so confirm the person's unit and visit type before traveling.
- **It is in the Catskills,** in Sullivan County.

## Visiting

The statewide DOCCS rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Woodbourne Correctional Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Hours:** Visits are within the facility's posted hours; some visits require advance scheduling — confirm the current schedule

**Medical unit:** The Regional Medical Unit (RMU) has its own visiting arrangements — confirm them with the facility if the person is housed there

**Screening:** A body scanner is required for a full-contact visit (a medical exemption can be requested)
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in New York](/states/new-york/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

Woodbourne and the surrounding Sullivan County area have gas, food, and lodging, with more in the Monticello and Middletown areas. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Sullivan County / Middletown area.

## Mail

New York does not use an off-site mail vendor — incoming personal mail is **opened and photocopied at the facility**, and the incarcerated person receives the **photocopies** (images may also reach their JPay tablet) rather than the original letters. Address it with the person's **name and DIN (Department Identification Number)** and the facility's address. **Legal mail** goes to the facility and is handled under privileged-mail rules. **Books, publications, and packages** must be ordered from **approved vendors**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/new-york/guides/mail/).
