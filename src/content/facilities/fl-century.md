## Overview

Century Correctional Institution is a **general-population men's prison** at Century in Escambia County, in the far-northwest corner of the Florida Panhandle near the Alabama line. It is operated by the Florida Department of Corrections, holds men in minimum, medium, and close custody, and has a capacity of about 1,345. Because it is in the western Panhandle, the institution is on **Central Time**.

## What Makes Century Different

- **It is in the far-northwest corner of Florida,** near the Alabama line — one of the most distant FDC prisons from any metro area, and families often travel through or from Alabama.
- **It is on Central Time,** unlike most of the state — confirm whether a posted visiting time is Central or Eastern.
- **Its satellite units include the Century Work Camp and the Pensacola Community Release Center** — confirm which housing unit the person is in before traveling.
- **It offers academic, vocational, substance-abuse, and faith-and-character programs,** including residential carpentry and cabinetmaking, a PRIDE "Tiny Homes" build program, and small-business certification.

## Getting There and Parking

The institution is at Century in Escambia County, in the far-northwest Florida Panhandle near the Alabama line, on Central Time. From the Pensacola area it is reached by traveling north on US-29 and then to Tedder Road.

:::key-info
**By car:** At Century in Escambia County, far-northwest Florida, north of Pensacola via US-29 to Tedder Road; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the institution; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Mail

Florida does not deliver original personal mail to the prison. Routine personal letters and photos go to an off-site **Smart Communications** processing center, where they are **opened and scanned** and delivered electronically — the person reads them on a tablet or kiosk, and the mailroom may print copies. Address routine mail with the person's **full name and DC number**, to: **Smart Communications, P.O. Box 23608, Tampa, FL 33623**. Confirm the current address and format on FDC's website before sending. **Legal mail and privileged correspondence go directly to the institution**, not to the scanning center. **Publications** must come from an approved publisher or retailer. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/florida/guides/mail/).
