## Overview

Racine Correctional Institution (RCI) is a medium-security state prison for men in Sturtevant, Wisconsin, operated by the Wisconsin Department of Corrections (WI DOC) through its Division of Adult Institutions.

Wisconsin abolished the death penalty in 1853 — the first U.S. state to do so — and has no death row and no execution chamber.

Men are assigned a security level at intake, which for the Wisconsin system takes place at Dodge Correctional Institution in Waupun. People are placed at Racine by the department; families do not choose the facility, and the department does not automatically notify families of a transfer. Re-check the [offender locator](https://appsdoc.wi.gov/lop/welcome) before traveling.

## What Makes Racine Different

Racine Correctional Institution is a medium-security general-population institution for men in southeastern Wisconsin, between Milwaukee and the Illinois state line. It shares the village of Sturtevant with two other WI DOC facilities — the Racine Youthful Offender Correctional Facility and the Sturtevant Transitional Facility — which are separate institutions with their own operations. Because the three are in the same community and have similar names, confirm on the [offender locator](https://appsdoc.wi.gov/lop/welcome) which facility holds a specific person before traveling or sending mail.

## Visiting

The statewide WI DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Racine Correctional Institution. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
- **Approved list first:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visiting list before visiting. The person mails a DOC-21AA Visitor Questionnaire to the prospective visitor, who completes it and returns it to the institution; the person is notified when a visitor is approved.
- **Scheduling:** Wisconsin has no statewide online visit scheduler. In-person visits are arranged with Racine Correctional Institution directly — call the facility at (262) 886-3214 to confirm the current visiting schedule and procedures.
- **Days and hours:** Set by the facility and subject to change. Confirm the current schedule before traveling, and arrive no more than 15 minutes before a visit.
- **Video visits:** Scheduled separately through ICSolutions, not through the facility.
:::

## Getting There and Parking

Racine Correctional Institution is at 2019 Wisconsin St. in Sturtevant, Racine County, in southeastern Wisconsin between Milwaukee and the Illinois state line, near the Interstate 94 corridor. The facility is reached by private vehicle; confirm visitor parking and entrance procedures with the institution. Public transit options to the site are limited.

## Nearby Services

Sturtevant is in Racine County in southeastern Wisconsin. Hospitals with emergency rooms operate in the Racine and Milwaukee area. Lodging, fuel, and food are available in the Racine and Milwaukee area along the Interstate 94 corridor.

## Mail

Incoming personal mail does not go to the prison. Wisconsin sends personal mail to an off-site vendor, **TextBehind**, which opens and photocopies it and forwards copies to the institution; people with tablets may also receive electronic photos. Address personal mail with the person's full name, **DOC number**, and the full (unabbreviated) facility name to: TextBehind, P.O. Box 189, Phoenix, MD 21131. **Legal and privileged mail** is exempt and goes directly to the institution at its own legal-mail address. **Books and other publications** must come from an approved vendor, shipped directly from the retailer, not from individuals. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/wisconsin/guides/mail/).
