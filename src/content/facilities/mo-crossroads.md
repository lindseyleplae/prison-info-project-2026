## Overview

Crossroads Correctional Center, on East Pence Road in Cameron, is a **maximum-security prison for men** in northwestern Missouri. Because of its security level, visiting and movement can be more controlled than at lower-security prisons, and arrangements vary by housing unit.

## What Makes Crossroads Different

- **It is a maximum-security prison** for men in northwestern Missouri.
- **Because it is maximum security,** visiting, movement, and property are more restricted than at lower-security prisons.
- **Cameron also has other state facilities,** so confirm which one holds the person before traveling.

## Visiting

The statewide Missouri DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the Crossroads Correctional Center. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Missouri DOC online visiting application before visiting

**Right facility:** Cameron also has other state facilities — confirm which one holds the person before traveling

**Visit type:** Visiting for men in maximum-security or restrictive housing may differ from general population — confirm the housing and visit type

**Schedule:** The facility sets its own visiting days and hours — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Missouri](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on East Pence Road in Cameron, in northwestern Missouri.

:::key-info
**By car:** Cameron is in northwestern Missouri, on Interstate 35 north of Kansas City; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Cameron has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Cameron and St. Joseph area, to the west.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
