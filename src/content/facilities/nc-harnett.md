## Overview

Harnett Correctional Institution is a medium-security state prison for men in Lillington, North Carolina, operated by the North Carolina Department of Adult Correction (NCDAC). The facility is in Harnett County in the central part of the state, between Raleigh and Fayetteville.

People are assigned a custody level by the department and placed at a facility accordingly; families do not choose the facility, and the department does not automatically notify families of a routine transfer. Re-check the [offender locator](https://webapps.doc.state.nc.us/opi/offendersearch.do?method=view) before traveling.

## What Makes Harnett Different

Harnett is a medium-custody general-population institution for men, with an offender capacity of about 988. It operates within the central region of the North Carolina prison system.

## Visiting

The statewide NCDAC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Harnett Correctional Institution. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
- **Approved list first:** Every visitor must be on the offender's approved visiting list before visiting. The incarcerated person mails an application to each prospective visitor; applicants 16 and older include a copy of a government photo ID, and minors under 16 a copy of a birth certificate. The completed form goes to the prison where the person is housed.
- **Scheduling:** Visits are by appointment only. North Carolina has no statewide online visit scheduler; appointments are arranged with Harnett Correctional Institution directly — call the facility at 910-893-2751 to confirm the current visiting schedule and procedures.
- **Days and hours:** Set by the facility and subject to change. Confirm visitation status the day before a visit.
- **Limits:** Generally one visit per week, up to two hours, with up to three approved visitors. Whether a visit is contact or non-contact depends on custody class and disciplinary status.
:::

## Getting There and Parking

Harnett Correctional Institution is at 1210 E. McNeill Street in Lillington, Harnett County, in central North Carolina between Raleigh and Fayetteville. The facility is reached by private vehicle; confirm visitor parking and entrance procedures with the institution. Public transit options to the site are limited.

## Nearby Services

Lillington is in Harnett County in central North Carolina. Hospitals with emergency rooms operate in the Lillington and Fayetteville area. Lodging, fuel, and food are available in the Lillington area and along the U.S. Highway 401 corridor toward Fayetteville and Raleigh.

## Mail

Incoming personal mail does not go to the prison. North Carolina sends personal mail to an off-site vendor, **TextBehind**, which opens and scans it and delivers a digital copy to the incarcerated person's tablet; original letters, cards, and packages are returned to the sender. Address personal mail with the person's full name and **OPUS number** and the full (unabbreviated) prison name to: TextBehind, P.O. Box 247, Phoenix, MD 21131 — and include the sender's full first and last name in the return address. **Legal mail** is exempt and goes directly to the facility. **Books and publications** must come from a publisher or online retailer, shipped to the prison's street address, not the TextBehind box. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/north-carolina/guides/mail/).
