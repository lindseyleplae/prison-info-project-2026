## Overview

The White River Correctional Center, in Batesville, is a **supervision-sanction center** — part of Arkansas's community-corrections system rather than a traditional prison. It holds men who are serving **short sanctions** (often about 90 days, up to 180) for **technical violations** of probation, parole, or post-release supervision — for example, a missed check-in or a failed drug test — as a diversion from a longer prison term. It opened in **2024** in a former juvenile-detention facility and has about 162 beds. Because stays are short, a person may be here only briefly before returning to supervision.

## What Makes White River Different

- **It is a short-term sanction center,** not a standard prison — most people are serving brief sanctions and may be released back to supervision soon.
- **It holds technical violators,** not people serving new-crime convictions, with a rehabilitation and reentry focus.
- **It is new (2024),** in a converted juvenile facility, so ADC has published little about visiting — confirm directly.

## Visiting

The statewide ADC rules — the approved visitor list, the no-white dress code, ID, and pre-scheduled visits — are the baseline, but as a community-corrections sanction center, White River's visiting may differ.

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** ADC has not published White River's visiting days or hours — call (870) 793-7970 to confirm whether and when you can visit

**Scheduling:** Confirm the process with the facility; the statewide TeleGov scheduling and approved-list rules generally apply

**Stays are short:** Because sanctions are often about 90 days, confirm the person is still held here before traveling
:::

The full statewide approval process is in [Visiting in Arkansas](/states/arkansas/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The center is on County Yard Road in Batesville, in north-central Arkansas.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Batesville, about 90 miles northeast of Little Rock via U.S. 167 and Highway 25

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the facility; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Batesville has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **White River Medical Center** in Batesville.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the facility. Since September 2025, ADC routes non-legal personal letters and photos to an **off-site scanning center in Tampa, Florida**, addressed with the person's name and **ADC number**; the person receives the mail electronically. **Legal mail, books, and magazines** still go directly to the facility at the Batesville address. Full addressing details are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arkansas/guides/mail/).
