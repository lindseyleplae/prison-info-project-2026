## Overview

Montgomery State Prison, on Mt. Vernon/Alston Road in Mount Vernon in Montgomery County, is a **medium-security state prison for men** in southeast Georgia. It houses adult male felons and has a capacity of about **900**, in six dormitories of 64 beds each. The prison offers academic, vocational, and counseling programs, and it operates a fire department and an egg-producing plant.

## What Makes Montgomery Different

- **It is a medium-security men's prison,** holding adult male felons in general population.
- **It offers academic, vocational, and counseling programs** alongside work assignments.
- **It operates a fire department and an egg-producing plant** as part of its work programs.
- **It is in Mount Vernon, in southeast Georgia,** near Vidalia.

## Visiting

The statewide GDC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Montgomery State Prison. The facility's own arrangements:

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is in Mount Vernon, in Montgomery County in southeast Georgia, near Vidalia.

:::key-info
**By car:** Mount Vernon is in southeast Georgia, near Vidalia and southeast of Dublin; the prison is on Mt. Vernon/Alston Road

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Vidalia, a few miles east, and Dublin, to the northwest, have the closest gas, food, and lodging. The nearest hospital emergency rooms are in **Vidalia** (Memorial Health Meadows Hospital) and **Dublin** (Fairview Park Hospital and the Carl Vinson VA Medical Center).

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's full name and **GDC ID number**. Georgia is moving toward centralized mail handling, so confirm the current mailing address before sending. Books and magazines must come from a publisher or established retailer, and legal mail is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/georgia/guides/mail/).
