## Overview

Moore Haven Correctional and Rehabilitation Facility is a **medium-custody men's prison** near Moore Haven in Glades County, in south-central Florida near Lake Okeechobee. It is privately operated by the GEO Group under contract for the Florida Department of Corrections, with the contract monitored by the Florida Department of Management Services, and has a capacity of about 985.

## What Makes Moore Haven Different

- **It is privately operated by the GEO Group** under contract for the FDC; everyone held here remains in FDC custody and appears on the FDC Corrections Offender Network.
- **The contract is monitored by the Florida Department of Management Services,** the state's private-prison oversight agency.
- **It runs a reentry mission,** with academic instruction, vocational training, substance-abuse education, life skills, and transition planning.
- **Account deposits may follow the operator's process** (a ViaPath/ConnectNetwork path) rather than the FDC-standard vendor — confirm the current method in [Sending Money](/states/florida/guides/money/) and with the facility.

## Mail

Florida does not deliver original personal mail to the prison. Routine personal letters and photos go to an off-site **Smart Communications** processing center, where they are **opened and scanned** and delivered electronically — the person reads them on a tablet or kiosk, and the mailroom may print copies. Address routine mail with the person's **full name and DC number**, to: **Smart Communications, P.O. Box 23608, Tampa, FL 33623**. Confirm the current address and format on FDC's website before sending. **Legal mail and privileged correspondence go directly to the institution**, not to the scanning center. **Publications** must come from an approved publisher or retailer. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/florida/guides/mail/).
