## Overview

The North Central Correctional Institution, commonly known as **NCCI-Gardner**, is a men's prison in Gardner, in north-central Massachusetts. It is **primarily medium security**, with some minimum and pre-release housing. It opened in the early 1980s.

## What Makes NCCI-Gardner Different

- **It is a medium-security men's prison,** with some minimum and pre-release housing.
- **It is in Gardner, in north-central Massachusetts** (Worcester County), northwest of Worcester.
- **Visiting is scheduled by phone with the facility,** which sets its own days and hours — there is no statewide online scheduler.

## Visiting

The statewide MA DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at North Central Correctional Institution. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Scheduling:** Visits are scheduled by phone with the facility — there is no statewide online scheduler; confirm the days, hours, and how far ahead to call

**Visit type:** Contact and video visits are available; confirm the current arrangements with the facility
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Massachusetts](/states/massachusetts/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Colony Road in Gardner, in north-central Massachusetts.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Gardner, reached from Route 2 and Route 68 in north-central Massachusetts (Worcester County), northwest of Worcester

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The Gardner area has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Heywood Hospital** in Gardner, about 2 miles away.

## Mail

Incoming personal mail goes to the prison — Massachusetts does not use an off-site mail vendor. Address it with the person's full name and commitment number and the facility's mailing address (above); all incoming mail is opened and inspected. **Legal mail** is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/massachusetts/guides/mail/).
