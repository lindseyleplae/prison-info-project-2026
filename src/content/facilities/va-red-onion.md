## Overview

Red Onion State Prison, near Pound in far southwest Virginia, is the state's **highest-security prison**, holding men at **Security Level S**. It opened in 1998 for the long-term segregation of people with the most serious security histories. With Wallens Ridge, it runs VADOC's **Step-Down Program**, which uses structured programming to move people out of restrictive housing toward a general-population setting; the department's restrictive-housing reforms have been recognized nationally.

Conditions in restrictive housing at Red Onion have been the subject of **state oversight** — including review by Virginia's Corrections Ombudsman, created in 2024 — and of litigation. Families with a concern can reach the Ombudsman through the channels in [Medical & Mental Health](/states/virginia/guides/medical/).

## What Makes Red Onion Different

- **It is the highest-security prison in Virginia.** Because it holds Security Level S men, **visits are generally non-contact** — contact visits are limited to attorneys.
- **It is extremely remote.** Far southwest Virginia, roughly seven hours from Richmond and reached only by car.
- **Restrictive housing is its focus.** It was built for long-term segregation and runs the Step-Down program; its conditions have drawn oversight and litigation.

## Visiting

The statewide VADOC rules above — the online application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Red Onion. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Contact vs. non-contact:** Because Red Onion holds Security Level S men, visits are **non-contact**, except contact visits with an attorney who has a current attorney-client relationship

**Days:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays; the schedule for high-security and restorative-housing units is set per facility, so confirm with the prison

**Video:** Video visits are offered, including from home on a computer or Android device
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Virginia](/states/virginia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on H. Jack Rose Highway near Pound, in Wise County.

:::key-info
**From Richmond:** About 350 miles — roughly 7 hours by car

**From the Bristol/Abingdon area:** Roughly 1 hour 30 minutes

**By air:** The nearest commercial airport is Tri-Cities Regional (Tennessee/Virginia), about 78 miles away; there is no public transit to the facility
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The nearest concentration of gas, food, and lodging is in the **Norton and Wise** area, the regional hub about 15 to 20 minutes away. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Norton Community Hospital** in Norton.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the facility at **P.O. Box 970, Pound, VA 24279**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go instead to the Central Mail Distribution Center in State Farm. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
