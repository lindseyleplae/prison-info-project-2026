## Overview

River North Correctional Center, near Independence in Grayson County, is a **close-security prison for men** (Security Level 4). It opened in 2013, making it one of VADOC's newer prisons, with a capacity of about 1,021 and pod-style housing. River North offers education, vocational training, and re-entry programming.

## What Makes River North Different

- **A close-security facility.** River North holds men in close custody (Security Level 4).
- **One of VADOC's newer prisons.** River North opened in 2013, with a capacity of about 1,021.
- **Pod-style housing.** The facility uses a pod-style housing design.

## Visiting

The statewide VADOC rules above — the online application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at River North. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays — confirm the current in-person hours with the facility

**Scheduling:** Apply online to the Central Visitation Unit, then book through the statewide scheduler (the ViaPath visitation scheduler) up to 14 days ahead

**Video:** Video visits are offered, including from home on a computer or Android device
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Virginia](/states/virginia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Dellbrook Lane near Independence, in Grayson County.

:::key-info
**From Richmond:** Well over 250 miles — several hours, generally via Interstate 81 and Interstate 77

**Interstate access:** The nearest interstate access is at Wytheville, about 23 miles away

**Transit:** No fixed-route public transit serves Independence; a vehicle is the practical way to reach the prison
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Independence is small. **Galax**, about 16 miles away, is the nearest larger town with gas, food, and lodging; **Wytheville**, about 23 miles away, has the nearest interstate access. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Twin County Regional Hospital** in Galax.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the facility at **329 Dellbrook Lane, Independence, VA 24348**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go instead to the Central Mail Distribution Center in State Farm. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
