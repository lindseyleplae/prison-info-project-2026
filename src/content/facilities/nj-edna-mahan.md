## Overview

The Edna Mahan Correctional Facility for Women, in Hunterdon County, is **New Jersey's only women's prison** and the point where women enter the state system. It has operated since **1913** (originally as Clinton Farms) and is named for a longtime superintendent.

In **2020, the U.S. Department of Justice found reasonable cause to believe** that conditions at the facility violated the Eighth Amendment because of sexual abuse of women by staff. In **2021, the state announced it would close Edna Mahan** and build a new women's prison. Since then, women have been moved in phases: the **main grounds** now hold only **medium- and maximum-custody** women, while **minimum- and medium-custody** women are held at an **off-site satellite unit** — the former William H. Fauver youth facility in **Annandale**, about four miles away. A **new women's prison is under construction** in Burlington County and is **not yet open**. Because of this, confirming where a specific woman is held is an important first step.

## What Makes Edna Mahan Different

- **It is the state's only women's prison** and the women's intake point.
- **Its population is split across two sites** during the replacement — the main grounds and the Annandale satellite unit — so visiting and mail depend on which one holds the person.
- **A new women's prison is being built** to replace it, but has not yet opened.

## Visiting

The statewide NJDOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Edna Mahan. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Scheduling:** Call (908) 735-7111 ext. 3318, Monday through Thursday, 8 a.m. to 4 p.m., at least 48 hours ahead

**Where the visit happens:** At the main grounds or the satellite unit, depending on the person's housing — confirm when scheduling

**Video:** Video visits are available; see the phone and video guide
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in New Jersey](/states/new-jersey/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

Both sites are in Hunterdon County, off the I-78 corridor near Clinton and Annandale.

:::key-info
**Main grounds:** On Route 513 near Clinton, off I-78

**Satellite unit:** In Annandale, about four miles from the main grounds

**Transit:** This is a rural area off I-78; there is no direct public transit to either site, so a vehicle is the practical way to get there
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site at both locations.

## Nearby Services

The Clinton and Annandale area, along I-78, has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Hunterdon Medical Center** at 2100 Wescott Drive in Flemington, about 15 to 20 minutes away.

## Mail

Personal **letters** do not go to the prison — they go to the state's **off-site scanning center**, addressed with the person's name, **SBI number**, and the facility's code (**1405**), to **P.O. Box 96777, Las Vegas, NV 89193** (confirm the code and current format on NJDOC's Staying Connected page). **Legal mail and publications** go directly to the facility at **30 Route 513, Clinton, NJ 08809**; for a person at the satellite unit, confirm the correct legal-mail address with the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/new-jersey/guides/mail/).
