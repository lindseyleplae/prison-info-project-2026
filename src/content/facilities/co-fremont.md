## Overview

Fremont Correctional Facility, on the East Cañon Complex about two miles east of Cañon City, is a **Level III men's prison** holding a mix of custody levels from minimum to close. With a bed capacity of about **1,601**, it is the **second-largest facility in the CDOC system**; CDOC's early-2026 report listed about 1,520 men there. Fremont hosts **Tracks 1, 2, and 3 of the statewide Sex Offender Treatment and Monitoring Program (SOTMP)**, so many men here are completing that treatment, and it runs the **Juveniles and Young Adults Convicted as Adults Program (JYACAP)** along with Colorado Correctional Industries shops and college access. It opened in **1962**.

## What Makes Fremont Different

- **It is the second-largest prison in Colorado** by capacity.
- **It hosts the statewide Sex Offender Treatment and Monitoring Program (SOTMP),** so many men here are completing that treatment.
- **It runs a program for juveniles and young adults convicted as adults (JYACAP).**
- **Visits are scheduled by email only** — CDOC publishes no separate visiting phone line for Fremont.

## Visiting

The statewide CDOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Fremont. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Thursday through Sunday, 9:00 a.m. to 3:45 p.m., by appointment

**Scheduling:** By email to DOC_fcf_visiting@state.co.us; an approved visiting application must be on file. CDOC does not publish a separate visiting phone line for Fremont

**Video:** Video visits are available through Securus
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Colorado](/states/colorado/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on the East Cañon Complex, off U.S. 50 east of Cañon City.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 45 miles southwest of Colorado Springs (Interstate 25 south to U.S. 50 west) and about 115 miles south of Denver; the East Cañon Complex is about two miles east of Cañon City on U.S. 50

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the complex; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Cañon City has the nearest gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **St. Thomas More Hospital** in Cañon City.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's **commitment name and DOC number**. **Fremont is one of the CDOC facilities that photocopies incoming personal mail, delivers the copy, and destroys the original** — so do not send originals you want returned. **Legal mail** is opened only to inspect for contraband, in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/colorado/guides/mail/).
