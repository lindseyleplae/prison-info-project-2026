## Overview

ASPC-Winslow is a **state prison complex for men** in Winslow, in **Navajo County** in northeastern Arizona, along the **Interstate 40 corridor**. Like other ADCRR complexes, it is made up of **units that span different custody levels**, including a unit at **Apache** in the Winslow area. Visiting is arranged at the unit level, so the days, hours, and rules depend on the specific unit where the person is housed.

## What Makes ASPC-Winslow Different

- **It is in northeastern Arizona, on the I-40 corridor,** in the high desert near Winslow.
- **It is a multi-unit complex,** including a unit at Apache in the Winslow area.
- **Visiting is organized by unit,** so confirm the person's unit and that unit's schedule before traveling.

## Visiting

The statewide ADCRR rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at ASPC-Winslow. The complex's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Application to Visit an Inmate before visiting

**By unit:** Visiting days, hours, and rules are set by the specific unit — confirm them before traveling

**Confirm the unit:** The complex includes units in the Winslow area, including a unit at Apache — confirm where the person is housed on the locator
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Arizona](/states/arizona/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The complex is in Winslow, in northeastern Arizona along Interstate 40.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Winslow, reached from Interstate 40; Flagstaff is about 1 hour west and Phoenix about 3 hours southwest

**By transit:** A private vehicle is the most reliable way to reach the complex; confirm any local transit options
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Winslow has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is in Winslow; Flagstaff, about an hour west, has additional hospitals.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since December 2025, Arizona routes incoming personal mail to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Dallas, Texas**, where it is scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address it to **[person's name and ADCRR number], P.O. Box 211309, Dallas, TX 75211**. **Legal mail, publications, and parcels** still go directly to the complex. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arizona/guides/mail/).
