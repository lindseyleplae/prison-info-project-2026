## Overview

MCI-Shirley (the Massachusetts Correctional Institution - Shirley) is a **medium-security prison for men** in Shirley, in central Massachusetts. It is a **separate prison from the nearby Souza-Baranowski Correctional Center**, which is also in Shirley — confirm which one holds the person before traveling or sending mail.

## What Makes MCI-Shirley Different

- **It is a medium-security prison for men,** distinct from the maximum-security Souza-Baranowski Correctional Center.
- **It is in Shirley, the same town as the Souza-Baranowski Correctional Center** — the two are separate prisons, so confirm which one holds the person.
- **Visiting is scheduled by phone** with the facility, which sets its own days and hours.

## Visiting

The statewide MA DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at MCI-Shirley. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Scheduling:** Visits are scheduled by phone with the facility — there is no statewide online scheduler; confirm the days, hours, and how far ahead to call

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Massachusetts](/states/massachusetts/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Harvard Road in Shirley, in central Massachusetts.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Shirley, reached from Route 2 and local roads in central Massachusetts, near Ayer; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The Leominster and Shirley/Ayer areas have gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **UMass Memorial HealthAlliance-Clinton Hospital** in Leominster, about 15 miles away.

## Mail

Incoming personal mail goes to the prison — Massachusetts does not use an off-site mail vendor. Address it with the person's full name and commitment number and the facility's mailing address (above); all incoming mail is opened and inspected. **Legal mail** is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/massachusetts/guides/mail/).
