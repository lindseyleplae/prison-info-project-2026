## Overview

Green Rock Correctional Center, near Chatham, is a **medium-security prison for men** (Security Level 3) with a capacity of roughly 1,000. It is one of VADOC's newer prisons, built in the late 2000s. Green Rock is in Pittsylvania County, in the Southside region near Danville. Chatham is the Pittsylvania County seat; Danville, about 18 miles south, is the nearest city.

## What Makes Green Rock Different

- **One of VADOC's newer prisons.** Green Rock was built in the late 2000s.
- **Southside location.** It is in Pittsylvania County, near Danville.
- **Level 3 custody.** It holds men in medium custody (Security Level 3).
- **A mid-sized facility.** Capacity is roughly 1,000.

## Nearby Services

Chatham, the Pittsylvania County seat, has gas and food; **Danville** (about 18 miles south) is the nearest city, with more food and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Sovah Health – Danville**.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the facility at **475 Green Rock Lane, Chatham, VA 24531**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go instead to the Central Mail Distribution Center in State Farm. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
