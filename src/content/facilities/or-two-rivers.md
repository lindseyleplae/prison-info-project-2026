## Overview

Two Rivers Correctional Institution, in Umatilla on the Columbia River, is a **medium- and minimum-security** men's prison, budgeted for about **1,768** people (1,641 medium and 127 minimum). Construction began in 1997 and it became fully operational in **2000**, built to meet Oregon's requirement that adults in custody be engaged in productive work. It runs a range of **Oregon Corrections Enterprises** operations — an industrial laundry, an upholstery shop, and wood and custom-furniture manufacturing — along with a **dialysis unit**, an infirmary, behavioral-health services, and education and cognitive programs. Outside work crews serve nearby schools, the city of Hermiston, and state agencies.

## What Makes Two Rivers Different

- **Visits are by advance appointment,** booked through the ICSolutions visitor system — unlike Oregon prisons that take walk-in visits.
- **It runs a dialysis unit,** so it holds some men partly for their medical needs.
- **It is on Pacific time,** despite being in eastern Oregon — only the far-southeast corner near Ontario is on Mountain time.
- **It is on the Columbia River,** just off Interstate 84, about 30 minutes from the Tri-Cities in Washington.

## Visiting

The statewide ODOC rules above — the approved list, the no-blue dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Two Rivers.

:::key-info
**By appointment:** All visits must be scheduled in advance through the ICSolutions visitor system; the schedule opens about two weeks ahead and closes about three days before the visit

**Days:** General-population visits are generally Friday through Sunday plus some Thursdays; the minimum-security side visits on weekends; a separate housing unit visits on certain Wednesdays

**Sessions:** Two a day — about 8:00 to 10:30 a.m. and 1:00 to 3:30 p.m.

**Confirm first:** ODOC adjusted visiting hours statewide in 2026 — check the current schedule before booking
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Oregon](/states/oregon/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Beach Access Road in Umatilla, off U.S. 730 near Interstate 84 on the Columbia River.

:::key-info
**From Portland:** About 181 miles east on Interstate 84 — roughly 2 hours 50 minutes

**From Pendleton:** About 35 miles southeast

**Time zone:** Pacific time
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Hermiston, about 7 miles south, and the Tri-Cities in Washington, about 30 minutes north, have the nearest clusters of food, gas, and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Good Shepherd Medical Center** at 610 NW 11th Street in Hermiston.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's name **as ODOC lists it** and their **SID number**. Oregon is moving toward scanning personal mail to tablets, so confirm the current method before sending. **Legal mail** marked "LEGAL MAIL" goes to the facility and is opened in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/oregon/guides/mail/).
