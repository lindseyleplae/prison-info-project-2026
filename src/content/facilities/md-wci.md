## Overview

Western Correctional Institution, in Cumberland, is a **maximum-security prison for men** in Allegany County, in far western Maryland. It is located near North Branch Correctional Institution in the same far-western area. As a maximum-security facility in a remote location, visiting may be more restricted and some housing may be non-contact; confirm the person's unit and the facility's current rules before traveling.

## What Makes WCI Different

- **It is a maximum-security men's prison,** so visiting may be more restricted and some housing may be non-contact.
- **It is in far western Maryland,** a long drive from the Baltimore–Washington area.
- **It is located near North Branch Correctional Institution,** in the same far-western area.
- **A private vehicle is required** to reach the remote Cumberland location.

## Visiting

The statewide DPSCS rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Western Correctional Institution. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Maximum security:** Visiting may be more restricted and some housing may be non-contact — confirm the person's unit and the facility's current rules

**Scheduling:** Visits are arranged with the facility (no statewide online scheduler); free video visits are available through Microsoft Teams — confirm the current schedule
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in Maryland](/states/maryland/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The facility is in Cumberland (the Cresaptown area), in far western Maryland.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Cumberland, Allegany County, reached via Interstate 68 and McMullen Highway in far western Maryland — a long drive from the Baltimore–Washington area

**By transit:** A private vehicle is required to reach the facility; confirm any local transit options
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The Cumberland area has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Cumberland area.

## Mail

Maryland does not use an off-site mail vendor — **incoming personal mail goes directly to the facility**. Address it with the person's **committed name and SID (State Identification) number** and the facility's inmate-mail address. **Books must be new paperbacks ordered from an approved vendor**; hardcovers are not accepted. **Cash, checks, and money orders cannot be mailed** to the person — money is sent through Access Corrections. **Legal mail** is opened in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/maryland/guides/mail/).
