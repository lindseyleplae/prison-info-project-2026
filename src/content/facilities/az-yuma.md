## Overview

ASPC-Yuma, on East Juan Sanchez Boulevard in San Luis, is a **men's prison complex** in the far southwest corner of Arizona, near the U.S.–Mexico border. It is made up of **several units across custody levels**. Like all ADCRR prisons, it is organized into units, and visiting is arranged at the unit level.

## What Makes ASPC-Yuma Different

- **It is in the far southwest corner of Arizona, near the U.S.–Mexico border,** a long drive from most of the state.
- **It is made up of several units across custody levels,** and visiting is arranged at the unit level.
- **Visiting days, hours, and rules are set by the specific unit,** so confirm the person's unit before traveling.

## Visiting

The statewide ADCRR rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at ASPC-Yuma. The complex's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Application to Visit an Inmate before visiting

**By unit:** Visiting days, hours, and rules are set by the specific unit — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Arizona](/states/arizona/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The complex is in San Luis, in the far southwest corner of Arizona.

:::key-info
**By car:** In San Luis, reached via U.S. 95 south of the city of Yuma, near the border; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the complex; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Yuma, about 25 miles north of San Luis, has the range of gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Yuma Regional Medical Center**, in Yuma.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since December 2025, Arizona routes incoming personal mail to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Dallas, Texas**, where it is scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address it to **[person's name and ADCRR number], P.O. Box 211309, Dallas, TX 75211**. **Legal mail, publications, and parcels** still go directly to the complex. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arizona/guides/mail/).
