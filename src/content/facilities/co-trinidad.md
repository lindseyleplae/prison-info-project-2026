## Overview

Trinidad Correctional Facility is a **Level II minimum-restricted men's prison** in far southern Colorado. Although named for the city of Trinidad, it is **physically located in Model, about 15 miles east of Trinidad** on State Highway 350. It has been operational since **December 2001** and spans 544 acres. The facility runs a **RESTORE** reentry program and an **Honor House** unit that rewards program participation with enhanced-privilege housing, with college access through Trinidad State College and Second Chance Pell.

## What Makes Trinidad Different

- **It is physically in Model, not the city of Trinidad** — map to the Model address, about 15 miles east.
- **It is a Level II minimum-restricted men's prison** on a 544-acre site.
- **It runs RESTORE reentry and Honor House program units.**
- **It is in far southern Colorado,** near the New Mexico line.

## Visiting

The statewide CDOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Trinidad. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Friday through Tuesday, 10:00 a.m.–3:30 p.m.

**Scheduling:** By appointment — scheduled by 3:30 p.m. the Wednesday before the visiting weekend, through the visiting office (email doc_tcf_visiting@state.co.us); an approved visiting application must be on file
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Colorado](/states/colorado/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is at 21000 Highway 350 East in Model, about 15 miles east of Trinidad.

:::key-info
**By car:** Far southern Colorado on Interstate 25 to Trinidad (about 195 miles south of Denver), then about 15 miles east on State Highway 350 to Model

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The city of Trinidad, about 15 miles west and just off I-25, has the nearest gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Mt. San Rafael Hospital** in Trinidad.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's **commitment name and DOC number**. **Trinidad is one of the CDOC facilities that photocopies incoming personal mail, delivers the copy, and destroys the original** — so do not send originals you want returned. **Legal mail** is opened only to inspect for contraband, in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/colorado/guides/mail/).
