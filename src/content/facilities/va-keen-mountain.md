## Overview

Keen Mountain Correctional Center, in Buchanan County, is a **prison for men** in the far-southwest coalfields of Virginia. Opened in 1990, the facility's main compound holds **medium and close custody** (Security Levels 3 and 4), with a lower-level dorm. Keen Mountain offers education, vocational training, and re-entry programming.

## What Makes Keen Mountain Different

- **A coalfields location.** Keen Mountain is in Buchanan County, in the far-southwest corner of the state — one of the most remote VADOC prisons.
- **A mixed-custody compound.** The main compound holds medium and close custody, with a lower-level dorm.
- **Expanded video visitation.** On April 30, 2024, VADOC expanded video visitation at Keen Mountain, equipping every living area so that visits can be taken from the housing units.

## Visiting

The statewide VADOC rules above — the online application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Keen Mountain. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays — confirm the current in-person hours with the facility

**Scheduling:** Apply online to the Central Visitation Unit, then book through the statewide scheduler (the ViaPath visitation scheduler) up to 14 days ahead

**Video:** Video visitation was expanded in 2024, with every living area equipped, so visits can be taken from the housing units
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Virginia](/states/virginia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Kennel Gap Road in Oakwood, in Buchanan County.

:::key-info
**From Richmond:** Roughly 350 miles — about six hours, generally via Interstate 81 and US-460

**Nearest hubs:** Grundy, the Buchanan County seat, and Tazewell are the closest regional towns

**Transit:** No fixed-route public transit serves Oakwood; a vehicle is the practical way to reach the prison
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Oakwood is small. **Grundy**, the Buchanan County seat, and **Tazewell** are the nearest regional towns with gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Buchanan General Hospital** in Grundy.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the facility at **P.O. Box 860, Oakwood, VA 24631**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go instead to the Central Mail Distribution Center in State Farm. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
