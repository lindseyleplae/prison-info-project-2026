## Overview

The Choice Moore Unit is a TDCJ transfer facility for men in Bonham, Fannin County, in northeast Texas near the Oklahoma line. TDCJ lists it at about 1,224 beds, and it opened in May 1995.

As a transfer facility, the unit's role is the main thing that shapes visiting: people are often held here for shorter periods while awaiting movement to a permanent unit, so an assignment here can change.

## What Makes the Choice Moore Unit Different

-   A men's transfer facility in Bonham, Fannin County, in the northeast corner of Texas near the Oklahoma line
-   Transfer units tend to have shorter stays than permanent prisons, so an assignment here can change quickly; the TDCJ inmate locator shows the unit a person is currently assigned to
-   Separate from the nearby Buster Cole State Jail, also in Bonham; the two share a complex but have different addresses and visitation lists, and the locator shows which one a person is assigned to

## Visiting Hours and Procedures

The statewide rules above — approval, ID, prohibited items, and dress code — apply at the Choice Moore Unit. What follows is specific to this unit.

TDCJ does not publish per-unit visiting hours for the Choice Moore Unit. Visits are scheduled through the TDCJ Online Visitation Portal, and every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list. Weekend visiting is the standard pattern across TDCJ, but the current days, hours, and visit type for this unit should be confirmed before traveling.

Because this is a transfer facility with shorter stays, a person may be moved to another unit before a scheduled visit. Confirming the current unit assignment on the TDCJ inmate locator before a trip avoids arriving at a unit the person has already left.

## Getting There and Parking

The Choice Moore Unit is in Bonham, in Fannin County, northeast of Dallas and near the Oklahoma line. The address is 1700 North FM 87, Bonham, TX 75418.

:::key-info
**From Dallas:** Northeast on US-75 and connecting state highways toward Bonham, then the local approach to the unit; often about 1.5 hours

**From Sherman:** East on US-82 toward Bonham, then the local approach; often about 30-45 minutes

**Parking:** Visitor parking is available at the unit.

**Entry:** Check-in is at the unit's visitor processing point, where ID review and standard TDCJ screening happen before movement into the visiting area.
:::

There is no fixed-route bus to the unit, so the trip is by private vehicle.

## Phones, Mail, and Video

Contact has a sequence to it. A phone number must be on the incarcerated person's approved contact list before calls can connect, and after intake or a transfer that approval can take time to process. Calls are placed by the incarcerated person on TDCJ's Securus phone system; a prison cannot be called into. Account setup, rates, and video options are covered in [Phone & Video Calls](/states/texas/guides/phone-video/).

## Nearby Services

Bonham is a small city with limited commercial services. Sherman, west of Bonham, is the nearest larger service center for gas, food, groceries, and lodging.

For support beyond the unit itself, the Texas Inmate Families Association is a statewide nonprofit focused on education, advocacy, and prison-system navigation resources for families with someone in TDCJ custody.
