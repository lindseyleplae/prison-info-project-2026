## Overview

Milwaukee Secure Detention Facility (MSDF) is a **medium-security** detention facility in **downtown Milwaukee**, in **Milwaukee County**, run by the Department of Corrections' Division of Adult Institutions. It is a **high-rise urban facility** that opened in **2001** and functions much like a large jail, accepting people 24 hours a day. Unlike Wisconsin's general-population prisons, MSDF primarily holds **men on probation, parole, or extended-supervision holds and short revocations**, so a person's stay can be brief and the facility shown in the locator may change.

According to WI DOC, the facility has a capacity of about **1,040**, with most beds designated for men and **40 for women** in a separate unit.

Wisconsin **abolished the death penalty in 1853** — the first U.S. state to do so — so there is **no death row and no execution chamber** at any Wisconsin facility.

To confirm where a specific person is held, use the WI DOC offender locator.

## What Makes the Milwaukee Secure Detention Facility Different

- **It is an urban detention facility, not a general-population prison,** primarily holding people on probation, parole, or extended-supervision holds and short revocations.
- **Stays can be brief and a person may be moved,** so the facility shown in the locator can change between visits.
- **It is a high-rise building in downtown Milwaukee,** functioning much like a large jail and accepting people 24 hours a day.
- **It holds mainly men but also has a small women's unit** (about 40 beds), so both populations may be present.

## Visiting

The statewide WI DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the Milwaukee Secure Detention Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**List first:** Every visitor must be on the person's approved visiting list before visiting; the person mails a **DOC-21AA Visitor Questionnaire** to the prospective visitor, who completes and returns it to the institution

**More limited:** Visiting at a secure detention facility may be more limited than at a general-population prison — confirm the current days, hours, and process with the facility

**Schedule:** Wisconsin has no statewide online visit scheduler; visits are arranged with the institution. Video visits are scheduled separately through ICSolutions

**How to confirm:** Call the facility at (414) 212-3535 for the current visiting arrangements
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Wisconsin](/states/wisconsin/guides/visiting/).

## Mail

Incoming personal mail does not go to the prison. Wisconsin sends personal mail to an off-site vendor, **TextBehind**, which opens and photocopies it and forwards copies to the institution; people with tablets may also receive electronic photos. Address personal mail with the person's full name, **DOC number**, and the full (unabbreviated) facility name to: TextBehind, P.O. Box 189, Phoenix, MD 21131. **Legal and privileged mail** is exempt and goes directly to the institution at its own legal-mail address. **Books and other publications** must come from an approved vendor, shipped directly from the retailer, not from individuals. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/wisconsin/guides/mail/).
