## Overview

Pender Correctional Institution, on Penderlea Highway in Burgaw, Pender County, is a **medium-security state prison for men** in southeastern North Carolina. NCDAC classifies it as a medium-custody men's facility, with an offender capacity of 768. It sits in the coastal-plain region northwest of Wilmington and operates the standard NCDAC programming — education, work assignments, and treatment — within its medium-custody units.

## What Makes Pender Different

- **It is a medium-custody men's prison,** classified by NCDAC as a medium-custody facility for adult males.
- **It houses men in medium custody,** with an offender capacity of 768.
- **It is in southeastern North Carolina,** in Burgaw, Pender County, in the coastal plain.
- **It is within reach of the Wilmington area,** to the southeast of the facility.

## Visiting

The statewide NCDAC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Pender Correctional Institution. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**List:** Every visitor must first be approved on the incarcerated person's visiting list — the person mails an application to each prospective visitor, and the completed form goes to the prison where the person is housed

**Scheduling:** Visits are by appointment only — call or email the prison to schedule; there is no statewide online scheduler

**Before traveling:** Confirm the facility's visitation status the day before
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in North Carolina](/states/north-carolina/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

The Burgaw area has gas, food, and lodging, with more options in the Wilmington area to the southeast. The nearest full-service hospital emergency rooms are in the **Wilmington area**.

## Mail

Incoming personal mail does not go to the prison. North Carolina sends personal mail to an off-site vendor, **TextBehind**, which opens and scans it and delivers a digital copy to the incarcerated person's tablet; original letters, cards, and packages are returned to the sender. Address personal mail with the person's full name and **OPUS number** and the full (unabbreviated) prison name to: TextBehind, P.O. Box 247, Phoenix, MD 21131 — and include the sender's full first and last name in the return address. **Legal mail** is exempt and goes directly to the facility. **Books and publications** must come from a publisher or online retailer, shipped to the prison's street address, not the TextBehind box. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/north-carolina/guides/mail/).
