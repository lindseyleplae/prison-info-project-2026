## Overview

Coastal State Prison, on Gulf Stream Road in Garden City in Chatham County, is a **medium-security prison for men** just west of Savannah on the Georgia coast. The facility opened in 1981 and has a capacity of about **1,836**. Its programming includes the Strategic Intervention Program, substance-abuse and reentry treatment, and a faith- and character-based program.

## What Makes Coastal Different

- **It is a medium-security men's prison** in the Savannah area on the Georgia coast.
- **It runs the Strategic Intervention Program,** along with substance-abuse and reentry treatment.
- **It houses a faith- and character-based program.**
- **It is in Garden City, just west of Savannah,** off the Interstate 95 corridor.

## Visiting

The statewide GDC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Coastal State Prison. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays — confirm the facility's current visiting hours

**Scheduling:** By appointment through GDC's online visitation portal; the visitor must first be approved on the person's list, and requests for a given week are accepted Monday through Wednesday
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Georgia](/states/georgia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Gulf Stream Road in Garden City, just west of Savannah, off the Interstate 95 corridor.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Garden City, Chatham County, a few miles northwest of downtown Savannah; from Interstate 95, take the Savannah-area exits east toward Garden City

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Garden City and Savannah have the closest gas, food, and lodging, and Savannah has the fullest range. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are at the Savannah-area hospitals, a few miles east of the prison.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's full name and **GDC ID number**. Georgia is moving toward centralized mail handling, so confirm the current mailing address before sending. Books and magazines must come from a publisher or established retailer, and legal mail is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/georgia/guides/mail/).
