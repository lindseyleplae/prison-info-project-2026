## Overview

Corrigan Correctional Center, in Uncasville (in the town of Montville), is a **Level 3/4** facility holding **both people awaiting trial and sentenced men** — Connecticut's unified system means one facility does both jobs. It opened in **1994** and serves as the **pretrial intake center** for the Superior Courts in Danielson, New London, Norwich, and Windham. As of mid-2026 it held about **670 men** (roughly 390 sentenced and 270 awaiting trial). It was formerly called **Corrigan-Radgowski Correctional Center**; the separate **Radgowski building closed in October 2021** amid a falling state prison population, and the facility now operates as Corrigan Correctional Center.

## What Makes Corrigan Different

- **It is both a jail and a prison,** holding people before trial and after sentencing — so the visiting rules depend on a person's status and housing unit.
- **It is the pretrial intake point for eastern Connecticut,** serving the Danielson, New London, Norwich, and Windham courts.
- **The Radgowski building closed in 2021,** and the facility was renamed Corrigan Correctional Center.
- **Many visits are non-contact.** Pretrial, protective-custody, and restrictive-housing visits are typically through glass; general-population visits are generally contact.

## Visiting

The statewide Connecticut DOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Corrigan.

:::key-info
**In-person:** Walk-in (no appointment), one hour, assigned by housing unit on a rotating schedule across the week — confirm the person's unit and the current week's schedule before traveling

**Contact vs. non-contact:** Depends on the housing unit — general-population visits are generally contact; pretrial, protective-custody, and restrictive visits are typically non-contact (through glass). Confirm for the specific person by calling (860) 848-5700

**Video:** Free video visits are scheduled through the DOC request form (45 minutes, up to twice a week)
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Connecticut](/states/connecticut/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

Corrigan is on Route 32 (the Norwich-New London Turnpike) in Uncasville, reached from Interstate 395.

:::key-info
**By car:** Interstate 395 to Exit 79 (not 79A) to Route 163, then Route 32 north about 1.25 miles — the facility is on the left; about 10 minutes south of Norwich and 15 north of New London

**By transit:** No public transit serves the facility; a car is the practical way to reach it
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Norwich (about 10 minutes north) and New London (about 15 minutes south) have the nearest food, gas, and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency rooms are **Backus Hospital** in Norwich and **Lawrence + Memorial Hospital** in New London.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's name and **inmate number**; Connecticut delivers the physical letter rather than scanning it off-site. Legal mail marked as privileged is opened in the person's presence and not read. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/connecticut/guides/mail/).
