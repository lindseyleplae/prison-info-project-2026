:::key-info
**Location:** Charlestown Road in Springfield, a mile from I-91's Exit 7 in southeastern Vermont

**Role:** Vermont's newest facility (2003) and its medical center — infirmary, units for elderly and chronically ill men, mental-health units, and the state's sex-offender treatment program

**Population:** About 370 men, mixing the region's pretrial detainees with sentenced men in specialized programs

**Visitation:** Two-hour weekend blocks by housing unit — and the infirmary has its own posted visiting block
:::

## Overview

Southern State opened in 2003 as Vermont's newest facility, a 27-acre campus that has become the system's medical and specialized-care hub: an infirmary with x-ray, dental, and optometry services, a housing unit for elderly and chronically ill men, secure and transitional mental-health units, the state's largest close-custody unit, and the Vermont Treatment Program for Sexual Aggressors. It also serves as the regional jail for Orange, Windham, Windsor, and parts of Bennington County, so its roughly 370 men mix pretrial detainees with sentenced men whose medical or program needs brought them here.

For families, the medical role means two things: a person can land here from anywhere in Vermont when their health requires it, and the **infirmary has its own visiting block** — Saturday late morning — so hospitalization inside the system doesn't cut off visits.

## What Makes Southern State Different

- **The medical hub:** if someone's care needs escalate — age, chronic illness, serious mental health needs — Southern State is usually where the system sends them, whatever their home region.
- **The infirmary has its own posted visiting block** (Saturday 11:00 a.m.-1:00 p.m.) — a regular, published window for visiting someone in medical care.
- **Two-hour blocks by unit** spread across both weekend days, the fullest schedule in Vermont — but the Alpha unit's visits happen only on request, arranged through the facility.
- **The easiest Vermont facility to reach from the south:** a mile from I-91's Exit 7, with verified lodging (a Best Western) one road mile from the gate — though it's two and a half hours from Burlington.

## Visiting Hours and Procedures

The statewide rules above — the 10-name list, dress code, ID, and screening — apply at Southern State. What follows is specific to this facility.

:::key-info
**Posted blocks:** Echo — Saturday 8:30-10:30 a.m.; Bravo, Charlie, and Infirmary — Saturday 11:00 a.m.-1:00 p.m.; India — Saturday 1:30-3:30 p.m.; Fox2 — Saturday 4:00-6:00 p.m.; Delta — Sunday 8:30-10:30 a.m.; Gulf — Sunday 11:00 a.m.-1:00 p.m.; Hotel — Sunday 1:30-3:30 p.m.; Fox3 — Sunday 4:00-6:00 p.m.; Alpha — scheduled on request

**Format:** Walk-in during the unit's block; one visit per week, up to three visitors

**Cutoff:** Arrivals in the last 30 minutes of a period are not processed
:::

The schedule carries no posted date, and how to request an Alpha or off-schedule infirmary visit isn't published — (802) 909-2601 is the number to ask. Video visits run through ICSolutions at a published 16 cents per minute; the full approval and entry rules are in [Visiting in Vermont](/states/vermont/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

Southern State is on Charlestown Road, about a mile from I-91's Exit 7 and four miles from downtown Springfield.

:::key-info
**From Burlington:** I-89 South to I-91 South — about 120 miles, roughly 2.5 hours

**From the south (Massachusetts/Connecticut):** I-91 North to Exit 7

**By train:** Amtrak's Vermonter stops at Bellows Falls, about 12 miles away. Weekday options exist — a Bellows Falls-Springfield bus and a free Springfield town microtransit service — but neither runs on weekends, when visits happen
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. DOC publishes no visitor parking guidance.

## Nearby Services

The Best Western Springfield sits one road mile from the facility on Charlestown Road, with Dunkin' locations and services in Springfield town. Springfield Hospital at 25 Ridgewood Road provides 24/7 emergency services — (802) 885-2151.
