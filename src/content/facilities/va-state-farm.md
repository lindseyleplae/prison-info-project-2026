## Overview

State Farm Correctional Center is a **medium-security prison for men** in Powhatan County, on the long-running State Farm corrections complex along the James River west of Richmond. It holds men in medium custody and offers education, vocational training, and re-entry and cognitive programs. The State Farm site includes several separate VADOC units — this prison, a work center, and an industries unit — and the site was historically known by names such as **Powhatan** and **Deep Meadow**, so families working from older records should confirm the current facility through the inmate locator.

## What Makes State Farm Different

- **It is close to Richmond.** About 25 miles west, it is among the more accessible VADOC men's prisons for families in the Richmond area — though there is no confirmed public transit to the gate.
- **Legacy names cause confusion.** The site has carried names like Powhatan and Deep Meadow over the years; the locator is the way to confirm where a person actually is.
- **Video visits in every housing area.** Video visitation was extended facility-wide in 2024.

## Visiting

The statewide VADOC rules above — the online application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at State Farm. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**In-person:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays — confirm the current hours with the facility

**Video:** Available in every housing area, Monday through Friday 9:00 a.m. to 9:30 p.m. and weekends 7:00 a.m. to 9:30 p.m.
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Virginia](/states/virginia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Woods Way at State Farm, in Powhatan County west of Richmond.

:::key-info
**From Richmond:** About 25 miles — roughly 35 to 40 minutes

**Transit:** There is no confirmed public transit to the facility; a vehicle is the practical way to reach it
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The nearest concentration of gas, food, and lodging is in the western Richmond suburbs — the Short Pump area of Henrico County and the Midlothian area — roughly 20 to 25 minutes away. The nearest 24/7 emergency rooms are in the Richmond and Midlothian area, such as **Bon Secours St. Francis Medical Center** in Midlothian.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the prison at **3500 Woods Way, State Farm, VA 23160**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go to a separate address at the same locale — the **Central Mail Distribution Center, 3521 Woods Way, State Farm, VA 23160** — so check which address you need. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
