## Overview

The Special Management Unit (SMU), on Highway 36 west of Jackson in Butts County, is **Georgia's highest-security restrictive-housing prison for men**. Opened in 2006, it is a **close-security** facility of about **192 beds** that houses men in the Tier III program and in a specialized mental-health treatment unit. The GDC describes its mission as returning close-security men to general-population prisons through structure, programming, incentives, and education. Because it is a restrictive-housing facility, visiting is limited and may be conducted without contact. A second GDC prison — the Georgia Diagnostic and Classification State Prison (GDCP) — is also near Jackson in Butts County, so the inmate search confirms which facility holds a given person.

## What Makes the Special Management Unit Different

- **It is the state's highest-security restrictive-housing prison,** holding men in the Tier III program and a specialized mental-health treatment unit.
- **Visits are limited and may be non-contact.** Schedules and contact rules are set by the facility for each program.
- **It is a small facility,** about 192 beds, opened in 2006.
- **It is about 50 miles south of Atlanta,** off Interstate 75, and shares the Jackson area with the separate GDCP.

## Visiting

The statewide GDC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the Special Management Unit. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Set by the facility and differing by program; visits at this restrictive-housing prison are limited — confirm the current rules

**Restrictive housing:** As Georgia's highest-security restrictive-housing prison, visits may be non-contact, and a person newly placed in the Tier III program may not be able to receive visits at first

**Scheduling:** By appointment through GDC's online visitation portal; the visitor must first be approved on the person's list
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Georgia](/states/georgia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Highway 36 west of Jackson, off the Interstate 75 corridor south of Atlanta.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 50 miles south of Atlanta — take Interstate 75 south to Exit 201 (Jackson), then Highway 36 west to the facility

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Jackson and nearby Griffin have the closest gas, food, and lodging; Atlanta, about 50 miles north, has the fullest range. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Wellstar Sylvan Grove Medical Center** in Jackson; **Wellstar Spalding Medical Center** in Griffin is a larger hospital about 20 minutes west.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's full name and **GDC ID number**. Georgia is moving toward centralized mail handling, so confirm the current mailing address before sending. Books and magazines must come from a publisher or established retailer, and legal mail is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/georgia/guides/mail/).
