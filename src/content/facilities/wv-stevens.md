## Overview

Stevens Correctional Center, in Welch in far southern McDowell County, is a **medium-security men's prison** holding about **320 men** (FY2024), with a designed capacity around 332. It holds **only sentenced state inmates** — it is not a pretrial regional jail. The building began as the **Stevens Clinic hospital**; the McDowell County Economic Development Authority bought it in 2001 and converted it to a correctional center, and it held West Virginia state inmates **under county management from 2006**. In **July 2025**, under House Bill 3456, the facility transferred to the state and became a **full WVDCR-operated prison** — the newest in West Virginia. West Virginia has no death penalty, so there is no death row.

## What Makes Stevens Different

- **It is West Virginia's newest state prison.** Run by McDowell County for nearly two decades, it came under full WVDCR control in July 2025.
- **It holds only sentenced state inmates,** not pretrial detainees — despite the "and Jail" in some listings, it functions as a state prison.
- **It is the only state prison in McDowell County,** the southernmost county in West Virginia.
- **It was converted from a former hospital,** the Stevens Clinic.

## Visiting

The statewide WV DCR rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Stevens.

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Because Stevens only recently became a state prison (July 2025), its schedule is moving onto the statewide system; statewide, prison visits are Saturday and Sunday, 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. — call (304) 436-5420 to confirm the current days and hours

**How it works:** Visits are scheduled in advance by the incarcerated person, not walk-in; the visitor must be on the approved list first

**Contact vs. non-contact:** Prison visits at Stevens are contact visits
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in West Virginia](/states/west-virginia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Virginia Avenue in Welch, in far southern West Virginia.

:::key-info
**By car:** Welch is in McDowell County, reached on U.S. Route 52; it is not on an interstate. From the Interstate 77 corridor near Bluefield or Princeton, it is roughly an hour or more west on winding two-lane roads, where cell signal is limited

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the facility; a private vehicle is the practical way to reach it
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Welch has limited food, gas, and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Welch Community Hospital**, the only acute-care hospital in McDowell County.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2023, West Virginia routes personal letters to an **off-site scanning center in Maryland**, addressed with the person's name and **OID number**; the person receives the mail as **images on a tablet**, and photos must be sent through the tablet rather than mailed. **Legal mail, books, and magazines** go to the facility. Because Stevens only recently joined the state system, confirm the current mailing address in [Mail & Packages](/states/west-virginia/guides/mail/).
