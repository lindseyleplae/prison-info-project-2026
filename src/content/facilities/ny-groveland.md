## Overview

Groveland Correctional Facility, in Sonyea, is a **medium-security prison** for men in western New York, in Livingston County. The facility offers substance-abuse treatment and a sex-offender treatment program.

## What Makes Groveland Different

- **It is a medium-security prison** for men.
- **It offers substance-abuse treatment** and a sex-offender treatment program.
- **It is in western New York,** in Livingston County.
- **Visiting days may be split by the last digit of the DIN,** so confirm the schedule before traveling.

## Visiting

The statewide DOCCS rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Groveland Correctional Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Hours:** Visits are within the facility's posted hours; some visits require advance scheduling — confirm the current schedule

**DIN-based schedule:** Visiting days may be split by the last digit of the incarcerated person's DIN, so confirm the schedule before traveling
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in New York](/states/new-york/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

Sonyea and the surrounding Livingston County area have gas and food, with more in the Rochester area to the north. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Rochester area.

## Mail

New York does not use an off-site mail vendor — incoming personal mail is **opened and photocopied at the facility**, and the incarcerated person receives the **photocopies** (images may also reach their JPay tablet) rather than the original letters. Address it with the person's **name and DIN (Department Identification Number)** and the facility's address. **Legal mail** goes to the facility and is handled under privileged-mail rules. **Books, publications, and packages** must be ordered from **approved vendors**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/new-york/guides/mail/).
