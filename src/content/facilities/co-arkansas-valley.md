## Overview

Arkansas Valley Correctional Facility, on Highway 96 about four miles west of Ordway in Crowley County, is a **Level III medium-security men's prison** in the Arkansas River valley, about 40 miles east of Pueblo. It opened in **August 1987**. Among its housing, it runs a **Restoring Promise Pod** and a **R.E.S.T.O.R.E. pod** — specialized reentry and young-adult program units — along with an incentive living unit.

## What Makes Arkansas Valley Different

- **It is a Level III medium-security men's prison** in the Arkansas River valley.
- **It houses "Restoring Promise" and "R.E.S.T.O.R.E." program pods,** specialized reentry and young-adult units.
- **All visits require an appointment,** booked by the Thursday before the weekend.
- **The nearest services are in Ordway** (about four miles east) and La Junta (about 24 miles south).

## Visiting

The statewide CDOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Arkansas Valley. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Friday, Saturday, and Sunday, 10:30 a.m.–3:30 p.m. (visitor processing begins at 10:00 a.m.)

**Scheduling:** Appointments required — call (719) 267-5137 (Mon–Thu 9:00 a.m.–3:00 p.m.) or email DOC_AVCF_Visiting@state.co.us; requests must arrive before 3:00 p.m. Thursday for that weekend

**Visitors:** Up to three adults and three children per visit
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Colorado](/states/colorado/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Highway 96 west of Ordway, in the Arkansas River valley.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 40 miles east of Pueblo — U.S. 50 east toward the Highway 96 corridor, then to the facility four miles west of Ordway

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Ordway, about four miles east, has basic gas and food; La Junta (about 24 miles south) and Pueblo (about 40 miles west) have fuller services. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Arkansas Valley Regional Medical Center** in La Junta.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's **commitment name and DOC number**. **Arkansas Valley is one of the CDOC facilities that photocopies incoming personal mail, delivers the copy, and destroys the original** — so do not send originals you want returned. **Legal mail** is opened only to inspect for contraband, in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/colorado/guides/mail/).
