## Overview

Avon Park Correctional Institution is a **general-population men's prison** east of Avon Park in Highlands County, in central Florida. It is operated by the Florida Department of Corrections, holds men in minimum, medium, and close custody, and has a capacity of about 956.

## What Makes Avon Park Different

- **It has an on-grounds work camp,** the Avon Park Work Camp, as a separate housing unit — the offender search shows which unit the person is in.
- **It is notably remote,** sitting at the end of Highway 64 about 10 miles east of the city of Avon Park, immediately east of the Avon Park Air Force Bombing Range.
- **It offers broad academic and vocational programs,** including welding, residential carpentry, irrigation, logistics, and business and finance tracks, along with GED and adult basic education.
- **It runs a substance-abuse continuum** of intensive outpatient and outpatient programming and an active chaplaincy.

## Mail

Florida does not deliver original personal mail to the prison. Routine personal letters and photos go to an off-site **Smart Communications** processing center, where they are **opened and scanned** and delivered electronically — the person reads them on a tablet or kiosk, and the mailroom may print copies. Address routine mail with the person's **full name and DC number**, to: **Smart Communications, P.O. Box 23608, Tampa, FL 33623**. Confirm the current address and format on FDC's website before sending. **Legal mail and privileged correspondence go directly to the institution**, not to the scanning center. **Publications** must come from an approved publisher or retailer. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/florida/guides/mail/).
