## Overview

The Tucker Unit, northeast of Pine Bluff in Jefferson County, is **one of Arkansas's oldest prisons**, established in **1916**. It is a prison farm with a rated capacity of about 968 and is best known for its **prison-industries operations** — the **Tucker bus factory** (which refurbishes school buses and other vehicles), along with mattress manufacturing, furniture refinishing, and metal fabrication. It also runs a Paws in Prison dog program.

## What Makes Tucker Different

- **It is a prison farm with the state's largest prison-industries footprint,** including the well-known bus factory.
- **It is one of the oldest units in the system,** dating to 1916.
- **It runs a Paws in Prison program** and a range of education and vocational programs.
- **It is rural,** northeast of Pine Bluff, with no public transit.

## Visiting

The statewide ADC rules above — the approved visitor list, the no-white dress code, ID, and pre-scheduled visits — apply at Tucker.

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturdays and Sundays for routine visits; how many visits a month depends on the inmate's class

**Scheduling:** Request a visit through the TeleGov online portal, or call the unit's Visitation Clerk

**Hours:** ADC does not post Tucker's specific visiting hours online — call (501) 842-2519 to confirm
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Arkansas](/states/arkansas/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The unit is off Highway 15 in Jefferson County, between Pine Bluff and England.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 25 miles northeast of Pine Bluff and about 35 miles southeast of Little Rock

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the unit; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Pine Bluff, about 25 miles southwest, has the nearest cluster of hotels, restaurants, and services. The nearest hospital emergency room is **Jefferson Regional Medical Center** in Pine Bluff.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the unit. Since September 2025, ADC routes non-legal personal letters and photos to an **off-site scanning center in Tampa, Florida**, addressed with the person's name and **ADC number**; the person receives the mail electronically. **Legal mail, books, and magazines** still go directly to the unit at the Tucker address. Full addressing details are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arkansas/guides/mail/).
