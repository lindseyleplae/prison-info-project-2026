## Overview

The Texarkana Regional Correctional Center, in downtown Texarkana on the Arkansas side, is one of the **smallest units in the system** — a **work-release center** with a rated capacity of about 128, for non-violent men who are within a few years of release. Many residents leave during the day for **community jobs** and return at night, so a person may not be at the center during weekday business hours.

## What Makes Texarkana Different

- **It is a small work-release center,** so a resident may be at a job in the community on weekdays.
- **It is on the Arkansas-Texas state line** — many services and the nearest 24-hour ER are across the line in Texarkana, Texas.
- **It is not the federal prison.** The separate **FCI Texarkana** on the Texas side is a federal facility, not this state center — confirm which one holds the person.

## Visiting

The statewide ADC rules above — the approved visitor list, the no-white dress code, ID, and pre-scheduled visits — apply at Texarkana.

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturdays and Sundays for routine visits; how many visits a month depends on the inmate's class

**Scheduling:** Request a visit through the TeleGov online portal, or call the unit's Visitation Clerk

**Hours:** ADC does not post the unit's specific visiting hours online — call (870) 779-3939 to confirm
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Arkansas](/states/arkansas/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The center is on East 5th Street in downtown Texarkana, Arkansas.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Texarkana, off Interstate 30 near the Arkansas-Texas line; about 140 miles southwest of Little Rock

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the unit; a private vehicle is the practical way to reach it
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Texarkana — on both sides of the state line — has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **CHRISTUS St. Michael Hospital** in Texarkana, Texas, just across the line.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the unit. Since September 2025, ADC routes non-legal personal letters and photos to an **off-site scanning center in Tampa, Florida**, addressed with the person's name and **ADC number**; the person receives the mail electronically. **Legal mail, books, and magazines** still go directly to the unit at the Texarkana address. Full addressing details are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arkansas/guides/mail/).
