## Overview

Tipton Correctional Center, on North Osage Avenue in Tipton, is a **minimum- and medium-security prison for men** in central Missouri. The facility sits on about 34 acres in **Moniteau County**, west of Jefferson City along U.S. 50.

## What Makes Tipton Different

- **It is a minimum- and medium-security prison** for men in central Missouri.
- **It sits on about 34 acres** in Moniteau County, west of the state capital.
- **It is reached via U.S. 50,** west of Jefferson City.

## Visiting

The statewide Missouri DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the Tipton Correctional Center. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Missouri DOC online visiting application before visiting

**Right facility:** Confirm which prison holds the person on the Missouri offender search before traveling

**Visit type:** Visiting arrangements can vary by housing unit — confirm the housing and visit type

**Schedule:** The facility sets its own visiting days and hours — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Missouri](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on North Osage Avenue in Tipton, in central Missouri.

:::key-info
**By car:** Tipton is in Moniteau County in central Missouri, reached via U.S. 50 west of Jefferson City; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Tipton has limited services. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the **Jefferson City** area, to the east. Jefferson City, the state capital, has gas, food, and lodging.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
