## Overview

Moberly Correctional Center, on South Morley in Moberly, is a **medium-security prison for men** in north-central Missouri. Visiting and movement follow the statewide Missouri DOC rules, with arrangements set by the facility.

## What Makes Moberly Different

- **It is a medium-security men's prison** in north-central Missouri.
- **It is in Moberly, Randolph County,** north of Columbia.
- **It follows the statewide Missouri DOC rules** for visiting, mail, and communication.

## Visiting

The statewide Missouri DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the Moberly Correctional Center. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Missouri DOC online visiting application before visiting

**Schedule:** The facility sets its own visiting days and hours — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Missouri](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on South Morley in Moberly, in north-central Missouri.

:::key-info
**By car:** Moberly is in north-central Missouri, on US-24 and US-63 north of Columbia; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Moberly has gas, food, and lodging, with more in the Columbia area to the south. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is in Moberly, with additional hospitals in Columbia.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
