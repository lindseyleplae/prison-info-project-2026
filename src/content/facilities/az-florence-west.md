## Overview

Florence West Correctional and Rehabilitation Facility, on East Diversion Dam Road in Florence, is a **minimum-custody prison for men**. It is **operated by the GEO Group**, a private company, under contract with ADCRR. The facility holds men in **DUI** and **return-to-custody (RTC)** programs, along with non-violent general-population inmates, and offers substance-abuse education and counseling, GED and adult basic education (ABE), work programs, and reentry preparation. It has about **750 beds** and opened in **1997**. Like other ADCRR facilities, it follows the statewide visiting, mail, and communication rules.

## What Makes Florence West Different

- **It is operated by the GEO Group under contract with the state,** rather than directly by ADCRR, while still following ADCRR's statewide rules.
- **It is a minimum-custody men's unit for DUI and return-to-custody (RTC) inmates,** plus non-violent general population. "Return to custody" refers to people returned to custody for a parole or probation violation, often for a shorter period rather than a new sentence.
- **Its programs focus on substance-abuse education and counseling, GED/ABE, work, and reentry.**
- **It is in Florence, Pinal County,** southeast of Phoenix, in an area with several other state and contract prisons.

## Getting There and Parking

The facility is on East Diversion Dam Road in Florence, in Pinal County southeast of Phoenix.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Florence, Pinal County, reached from the Phoenix metro area via U.S. 60 and State Route 79; about 1 to 1.5 hours from central Phoenix

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the facility; a private vehicle is the only reliable option
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since December 2025, Arizona routes incoming personal mail to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Dallas, Texas**, where it is scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address it to **[person's name and ADCRR number], P.O. Box 211309, Dallas, TX 75211**. **Legal mail, publications, and parcels** still go directly to the facility. Because the facility is privately operated, confirm the current mail process with the facility before sending anything. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arizona/guides/mail/).
