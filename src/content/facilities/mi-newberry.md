## Overview

Newberry Correctional Facility, on E. County Road 428 in Newberry, is a **Level I men's prison** in the central Upper Peninsula. It opened in **1996** on the former **Newberry Regional Mental Health Center** site. It has about **1,108 beds** and runs a **welding vocational program**.

## What Makes Newberry Different

- **It is on the former Newberry Regional Mental Health Center site.**
- **It has about 1,108 beds.**
- **It runs a welding vocational program.**
- **It is in the central Upper Peninsula,** about 67 miles west of Sault Ste. Marie and roughly 350 to 400 miles north of Detroit.

## Visiting

The statewide MDOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Newberry. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Scheduling:** All visits are by appointment, through the ViaPath scheduler (midoc.gtlvisitme.com), 48 hours to 7 days ahead

**Approved list:** Each visitor must first be approved on the prisoner's visiting list (apply with form CAJ-103)

**Hours:** Confirm the facility's current visiting schedule on MDOC's visiting page
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Michigan](/states/michigan/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on E. County Road 428 in Newberry, in the central Upper Peninsula.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 67 miles west of Sault Ste. Marie via M-28 and M-123; roughly 350 to 400 miles north of Detroit

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The village of Newberry has the nearest gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Helen Newberry Joy Hospital** in Newberry.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's name and **MDOC number**. Michigan **photocopies incoming mail in black and white and delivers the copy; the original is shredded**, so do not send originals you want kept. Color photos and messages can be sent through JPay. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/michigan/guides/mail/).
