## Overview

Dillwyn Correctional Center, in Dillwyn, is a **medium-security prison for men** (Security Level 2) with a capacity of about 1,106. By general reference, it opened in 1993. On **September 1, 2025**, Dillwyn became a **"Virginia Model" facility** — an incentive-based program in which eligible men can earn benefits including expanded visitation and increased programming. Dillwyn is distinct from the separate **Buckingham Correctional Center**, also near Dillwyn; confirm which facility holds the person. The prison is about 30–35 miles northwest of Farmville.

## What Makes Dillwyn Different

- **A "Virginia Model" facility.** Since September 1, 2025, Dillwyn has operated under the Virginia Model, an incentive-based program where eligible men can earn benefits including expanded visitation and increased programming.
- **Distinct from Buckingham Correctional Center.** A separate prison, Buckingham Correctional Center, is also near Dillwyn — confirm which one holds the person.
- **Level 2 custody.** It holds men in medium custody (Security Level 2).
- **Opened in 1993.** By general reference, the prison began operating in 1993, with a capacity of about 1,106.

## Nearby Services

Dillwyn is small; nearby **Farmville** (about 30–35 miles southeast) has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Centra Southside Community Hospital** in Farmville.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the facility at **1522 Prison Road, Dillwyn, VA 23936**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go instead to the Central Mail Distribution Center in State Farm. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
