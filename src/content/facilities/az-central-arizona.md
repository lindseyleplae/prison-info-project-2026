## Overview

Central Arizona Correctional and Rehabilitation Facility, on East Diversion Dam Road in Florence, is a **medium-custody prison for men**. It is **operated by the GEO Group**, a private company, under contract with ADCRR, and it opened in 2006. It holds **Arizona state inmates** and follows ADCRR's statewide visiting, mail, and communication rules. The facility has roughly **1,280 beds** and runs **substance-abuse and reentry programming**, including employability and work programs, GED and adult basic education, counseling, and case management. Like other ADCRR facilities, it is organized into units, and visiting is arranged at the facility level.

## What Makes Central Arizona Correctional and Rehabilitation Facility Different

- **It is operated by the GEO Group under contract with the state,** rather than directly by ADCRR, while still following ADCRR's statewide rules.
- **It is a medium-custody men's prison** of roughly 1,280 beds, holding Arizona state inmates.
- **It emphasizes substance-abuse and reentry programming,** including employability and work programs, GED and adult basic education, counseling, and case management.
- **It is in Florence, in Pinal County,** southeast of Phoenix, in an area that holds several Arizona prisons.

## Visiting

The statewide ADCRR rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Central Arizona Correctional and Rehabilitation Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Application to Visit an Inmate before visiting

**Scheduling:** Visits are by appointment after approval — confirm the facility's current visiting process

**By facility:** Visiting days, hours, and rules are set by the facility — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Arizona](/states/arizona/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The facility is on East Diversion Dam Road in Florence.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Florence in Pinal County, southeast of Phoenix; about 1 to 1.5 hours from Phoenix by car

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the facility; a private vehicle is the only reliable option
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Florence has gas, food, and some lodging, and more is available in the Phoenix metropolitan area to the northwest. The nearest larger hospitals are in the Phoenix and Casa Grande areas.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since December 2025, Arizona routes incoming personal mail to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Dallas, Texas**, where it is scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address it to **[person's name and ADCRR number], P.O. Box 211309, Dallas, TX 75211**. **Legal mail, publications, and parcels** still go directly to the facility. Because this is a privately operated unit, confirm the current mail process with the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arizona/guides/mail/).
