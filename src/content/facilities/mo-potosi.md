## Overview

Potosi Correctional Center, on State Highway O near Mineral Point, is a **maximum-security (Level 5) prison for men** in eastern Missouri. It houses the **men held under a death sentence** in Missouri, alongside a maximum-security general population. Men under a death sentence are housed here and are transferred to ERDCC in Bonne Terre, where the state's execution chamber is located, only shortly before a scheduled execution. It opened in 1989 and holds about **800 men**. Because of its security level and mission, visiting and movement can be more controlled than at lower-security prisons, and arrangements vary by housing unit.

## What Makes Potosi Different

- **It houses the men under a death sentence** in Missouri.
- **It is a maximum-security (Level 5) facility,** so visiting, movement, and property are more restricted than at lower-security prisons.
- **Some housing has non-contact visits,** which differ from general-population contact visits — confirm the person's housing and visit type.
- **It is near Mineral Point, in eastern Missouri,** southwest of St. Louis.

## Visiting

The statewide Missouri DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Potosi. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Missouri DOC online visiting application before visiting

**Visit type:** Visiting for men in maximum-security or death-sentence housing may be non-contact and may differ from general population — confirm the housing and visit type

**Schedule:** The facility sets its own visiting days and hours — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Missouri](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on State Highway O near Mineral Point, in eastern Missouri.

:::key-info
**By car:** Southwest of St. Louis, reached via State Highway 8 and local roads; the town of Potosi is about 8 miles away

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The Potosi area has limited services; gas, food, and lodging are in nearby Potosi and Farmington. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Washington County Memorial Hospital**, in Potosi.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
