## Overview

New Lisbon Correctional Institution is a **medium-security prison for men** in New Lisbon, in Juneau County in central Wisconsin. According to the Wisconsin Department of Corrections, construction began in **2001** and was completed the following year, in **2002**. The facility sits on about 100 acres and is laid out across eight buildings, including four general-population housing units, restrictive housing, a health services unit, a school complex, and administrative offices.

The prison operates at **medium security**. As with other Wisconsin institutions, a person's security level is assigned during intake at the statewide reception center, and the facility shown in the locator changes after classification and any later transfer.

## What Makes New Lisbon Different

- **It is a medium-security men's prison,** one of several medium-security institutions in the Wisconsin system.
- **It is in Juneau County in New Lisbon,** in central Wisconsin off Interstates 90 and 94, between the Wisconsin Dells area and Tomah.
- **It began operating in 2002,** after construction that began in 2001, and houses incarcerated men from across the state after classification.

## Visiting

The statewide WI DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at New Lisbon Correctional Institution. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approval first:** Every visitor must be on the person's approved visiting list — the person mails a DOC-21AA Visitor Questionnaire to the prospective visitor, who completes it and returns it to the institution

**Visitor limits:** General population allows up to **12 adults** on the list (minors do not count toward the cap until 18) — confirm the current rules with the institution

**Scheduling:** Wisconsin has no statewide online visit scheduler; visits are arranged directly with the institution. Video visits are scheduled separately through ICSolutions
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Wisconsin](/states/wisconsin/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

New Lisbon and the surrounding Juneau County area have gas, food, and lodging, with more options in the **Mauston** and **Tomah** areas. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the **Mauston** and **Tomah** areas.

## Mail

Incoming personal mail does not go to the prison. Wisconsin sends personal mail to an off-site vendor, **TextBehind**, which opens and photocopies it and forwards copies to the institution; people with tablets may also receive electronic photos. Address personal mail with the person's full name, **DOC number**, and the full (unabbreviated) facility name to: TextBehind, P.O. Box 189, Phoenix, MD 21131. **Legal and privileged mail** is exempt and goes directly to the institution at its own legal-mail address. **Books and other publications** must come from an approved vendor, shipped directly from the retailer, not from individuals. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/wisconsin/guides/mail/).
