## Overview

ASPC-Douglas, on North BDI Boulevard in Douglas, is a **state prison complex for men** in the far southeastern corner of Arizona, near the U.S.–Mexico border in Cochise County. The complex is made up of **several units spanning different custody levels**. Because of its location in the state's far southeast, it is a **remote prison, far from major cities** — a long drive from Tucson. Like all ADCRR prisons, it is organized into units, and visiting is arranged at the unit level.

## What Makes ASPC-Douglas Different

- **It is in the far southeastern corner of Arizona,** near the U.S.–Mexico border, far from the state's major cities.
- **It is a remote location,** a long drive from Tucson and other population centers.
- **It is made up of several units across different custody levels,** each with its own visiting arrangements.
- **Visiting is arranged by unit,** so the person's specific unit determines the days, hours, and rules.

## Visiting

The statewide ADCRR rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at ASPC-Douglas. The complex's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Application to Visit an Inmate before visiting

**By unit:** Visiting days, hours, and rules are set by the specific unit — confirm them before traveling

**Confirm the unit:** The complex has several units across custody levels; check the person's current unit on the locator
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Arizona](/states/arizona/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The complex is on North BDI Boulevard in Douglas, in the far southeastern corner of Arizona near the U.S.–Mexico border.

:::key-info
**By car:** In far southeastern Arizona; reached from Tucson via Interstate 10 east and State Route 80 south, roughly a two-hour drive

**By transit:** A private vehicle is the most reliable way to reach the complex; confirm any local transit options

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Douglas and nearby Bisbee have gas, food, and limited lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Douglas and Bisbee area, including **Copper Queen Community Hospital** in Bisbee.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since December 2025, Arizona routes incoming personal mail to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Dallas, Texas**, where it is scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address it to **[person's name and ADCRR number], P.O. Box 211309, Dallas, TX 75211**. **Legal mail, publications, and parcels** still go directly to the complex. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arizona/guides/mail/).
