## Overview

Buena Vista Correctional Complex, on U.S. 24 in Buena Vista in Chaffee County, is a men's prison complex in the mountains of central Colorado. It opened in **1892** as the Colorado State Reformatory, making it the **second-oldest prison in Colorado**, and became an adult prison in the late 1970s. The complex combines the **Buena Vista Correctional Facility (BVCF)** (medium and close custody) and the **Buena Vista Minimum Center (BVMC)** (minimum custody), which now share one mailing address. It offers vocational programs — welding, collision repair, carpentry, and culinary — plus college access through Second Chance Pell.

## What Makes Buena Vista Different

- **It is Colorado's second-oldest prison,** opened in 1892 as the Colorado State Reformatory.
- **It is a two-facility complex** — BVCF (medium/close) and BVMC (minimum) — with different visiting days, so confirm which part holds the person.
- **It runs vocational programs** in welding, collision repair, carpentry, and culinary arts.
- **It is in the mountains,** about two hours southwest of Denver.

## Visiting

The statewide CDOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Buena Vista. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days:** BVCF (main facility): Friday and Saturday, 9:00 a.m.–2:30 p.m. BVMC (minimum center): Sunday and Monday, 9:00 a.m.–2:30 p.m.

**Scheduling:** By appointment — email doc_bvcc_visiting@state.co.us or mail an application to P.O. Box 2017, Buena Vista, CO 81211; an approved visiting application must be on file

**Which facility:** Confirm whether the person is at BVCF or BVMC, since the visiting days differ
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Colorado](/states/colorado/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The complex is on U.S. 24 in Buena Vista, in the mountains of central Colorado.

:::key-info
**By car:** About two hours southwest of Denver — U.S. 285 southwest, then U.S. 24 to Buena Vista; Salida is about 25 miles south

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the complex; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Buena Vista has gas, food, and lodging; Salida, about 25 miles south, is the larger service hub. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Heart of the Rockies Regional Medical Center** in Salida.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's **commitment name and DOC number**. **Buena Vista is one of the CDOC facilities that photocopies incoming personal mail, delivers the copy, and destroys the original** — so do not send originals you want returned. **Legal mail** is opened only to inspect for contraband, in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/colorado/guides/mail/).
