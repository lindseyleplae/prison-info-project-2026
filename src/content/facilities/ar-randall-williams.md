## Overview

The Randall L. Williams Correctional Facility, west of Pine Bluff, is a men's unit that serves as an **intake facility** and houses a **special-management barracks** for people who need counseling and mental-health support. It has a rated capacity of about 562 and is one of the three co-located units in the **Pine Bluff Complex**. The building is a former **Jefferson County jail** that the state acquired in 2008.

## What Makes Randall L. Williams Different

- **It is an intake facility,** so a person may be newly arriving and still being classified, which can affect visiting.
- **It houses a special-management / mental-health barracks** for people who need counseling support.
- **It occupies a former county jail,** acquired by the state in 2008.
- **It is part of the co-located Pine Bluff Complex** (with the Barbara Ester and Pine Bluff units).

## Visiting

The statewide ADC rules above — the approved visitor list, the no-white dress code, ID, and pre-scheduled visits — apply at Randall L. Williams.

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturdays and Sundays for routine visits; how many visits a month depends on the inmate's class

**Scheduling:** Request a visit through the TeleGov online portal, or call the unit's Visitation Clerk

**Intake / special management:** People in intake or special-management housing may have limited visiting — confirm with the unit

**Hours:** ADC does not post the unit's specific visiting hours online — call (870) 267-6800 to confirm
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Arkansas](/states/arkansas/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The facility is on West 7th Avenue west of Pine Bluff, in the Pine Bluff Complex.

:::key-info
**By car:** West of Pine Bluff off West 7th Avenue; about 45 miles southeast of Little Rock via Interstate 530

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the unit; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Pine Bluff has the nearest hotels, restaurants, and services. The nearest hospital emergency room is **Jefferson Regional Medical Center** in Pine Bluff.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the unit. Since September 2025, ADC routes non-legal personal letters and photos to an **off-site scanning center in Tampa, Florida**, addressed with the person's name and **ADC number**; the person receives the mail electronically. **Legal mail, books, and magazines** still go directly to the unit at the Pine Bluff address. Full addressing details are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arkansas/guides/mail/).
