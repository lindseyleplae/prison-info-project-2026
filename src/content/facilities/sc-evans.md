## Overview

Evans Correctional Institution, in Bennettsville (Marlboro County, in the Pee Dee region of northeastern South Carolina, near the North Carolina line), is a **medium-security prison for men**. Confirm the person's housing unit and the institution's current rules before traveling.

## What Makes Evans Different

- **It is a medium-security men's prison** in the Pee Dee region of northeastern South Carolina.
- **It is in Bennettsville,** in Marlboro County, near the North Carolina line.
- **Visiting is by appointment after approval,** scheduled through the SCDC/ViaPath scheduler — confirm the institution's current days and hours.

## Visiting

The statewide SCDC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Evans Correctional Institution. The institution's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** The incarcerated person requests visitors from inside using SCDC Form 19-127 before anyone can visit

**Scheduling:** Visits are scheduled through the SCDC/ViaPath scheduler (scdoc.gtlvisitme.com) — confirm the current days and hours before traveling

**Video visits:** ViaPath video visits may be available — confirm the current options
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in South Carolina](/states/south-carolina/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

The Bennettsville and Florence area has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Bennettsville/Florence area.

## Mail

South Carolina does not use an off-site mail vendor — **incoming personal mail goes directly to the institution**, where it is opened and inspected before delivery. Address it with the person's **full committed name and SCDC number**, the living unit, and the institution's address. **Cash, stamps, and writing materials are not allowed** in the mail. **Legal mail** is opened in the person's presence. Since **October 1, 2025**, **publications (books and magazines) must be sent directly from an approved vendor**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/south-carolina/guides/mail/).
