## Overview

Eastern Oregon Correctional Institution, in Pendleton, is a **medium-security** men's prison budgeted for about **1,659** people. It occupies the **former Eastern Oregon State Hospital** building — structures built in **1911–1913** that served as a state mental hospital before the Legislature authorized a prison conversion in 1983; EOCI received its first adults in custody in **1985**. It is one of Oregon's largest centers for **Oregon Corrections Enterprises**, where adults in custody run a garment factory, an embroidery shop, a contact center, and a laundry. The garment factory makes the well-known **"Prison Blues"** denim line — jeans, shirts, and jackets sold to the public. Beyond work, EOCI offers cognitive and substance-abuse programs, an infirmary with 24-hour nursing, mental-health housing, and an assistance-dog program.

## What Makes Eastern Oregon Different

- **It occupies a former state mental hospital,** with buildings dating to the 1910s.
- **It makes "Prison Blues" denim** through Oregon Corrections Enterprises — one of the system's best-known work programs.
- **It is on Pacific time,** like most of Oregon — only the far-southeast corner near Ontario is on Mountain time.
- **It is just off Interstate 84,** about 3.5 hours east of Portland.

## Visiting

The statewide ODOC rules above — the approved list, the no-blue dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Eastern Oregon.

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Thursday through Sunday, 8:30 to 11:00 a.m. and 1:00 to 3:30 p.m.

**Contact vs. non-contact:** General-population visits are contact; disciplinary-segregation visits are non-contact (through glass) and scheduled in advance

**Confirm first:** ODOC changed visiting hours statewide in 2026 — check the current days and sessions on the ODOC visiting page
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Oregon](/states/oregon/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Westgate in Pendleton, just off Interstate 84.

:::key-info
**From Portland:** About 209 miles east on Interstate 84 — roughly 3.5 hours

**Time zone:** Pacific time

**Transit:** Pendleton has an airport and intercity bus service along I-84, but no transit is confirmed to the facility — a car is the practical option
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Pendleton has food, gas, and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **CHI St. Anthony Hospital** at 2801 St. Anthony Way in Pendleton, the same town as the prison.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's name **as ODOC lists it** and their **SID number**. Oregon is moving toward scanning personal mail to tablets, so confirm the current method before sending. **Legal mail** marked "LEGAL MAIL" goes to the facility and is opened in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/oregon/guides/mail/).
