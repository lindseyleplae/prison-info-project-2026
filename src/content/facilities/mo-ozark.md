## Overview

Ozark Correctional Center, on Honor Camp Lane in Fordland, is a **minimum-security prison for men** in southwestern Missouri, east of Springfield. It carries a **substance-use treatment mission**: the facility operates a long-term substance-use disorder treatment program, and many of the men held there are assigned for that programming. Because it is a minimum-security facility, movement and property rules differ from those at higher-security prisons.

## What Makes Ozark Different

- **It is a minimum-security prison for men** in southwestern Missouri, east of Springfield.
- **It has a substance-use treatment mission,** operating a long-term substance-use disorder treatment program for men assigned to it.
- **It is a smaller, minimum-security facility,** so movement, property, and visiting arrangements differ from those at higher-security prisons.
- **It is in rural Webster County,** near Springfield — confirm the facility's current visiting schedule before traveling.

## Nearby Services

Fordland is a small town; the nearest full range of gas, food, and lodging is in the Springfield area, to the west. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in **Springfield**.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
