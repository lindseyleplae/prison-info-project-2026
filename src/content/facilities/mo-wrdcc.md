## Overview

The Western Reception, Diagnostic and Correctional Center (WRDCC), on Faraon Street in St. Joseph, is the **reception and diagnostic center for men in western Missouri** — one of three regional intake points, with Fulton and Bonne Terre. Newly committed men from the western part of the state are **received, screened, and classified** here, and it also holds a general-population men's population. Because of its reception role, a man received for intake may be transferred after classification, so the prison shown in the offender search can change.

## What Makes WRDCC Different

- **It is a men's reception and diagnostic center** for western Missouri, so a newly committed man may begin here before transferring.
- **It is one of three regional intake points** in Missouri, with Fulton and Bonne Terre.
- **It holds both reception and general-population men,** with the offender-search location subject to change after classification.
- **It is in St. Joseph, in northwest Missouri,** north of Kansas City.

## Visiting

The statewide Missouri DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the Western Reception, Diagnostic and Correctional Center. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Missouri DOC online visiting application before visiting

**Reception status:** A man in reception has limited visiting and may transfer to a permanent prison after classification

**Schedule:** The facility sets its own visiting days and hours — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Missouri](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Faraon Street in St. Joseph, in northwest Missouri.

:::key-info
**By car:** St. Joseph is reached from Interstate 29 in northwest Missouri, north of Kansas City; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

St. Joseph has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Mosaic Life Care**, in St. Joseph.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
