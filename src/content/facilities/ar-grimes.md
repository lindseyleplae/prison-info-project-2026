## Overview

The Grimes Unit, near Newport in Jackson County, is a **medium- and maximum-security men's prison** with a rated capacity of about 1,012. It opened in 1998, run for several years by a private contractor before the state took over operation in 2001, and runs education and vocational programs, including barbering, along with garden and bee operations. It sits on the same Newport corrections complex as the **McPherson Unit**, the state's women's prison.

## What Makes Grimes Different

- **It is co-located with the McPherson women's unit** near Newport, so families with relatives at both visit the same area — but each unit schedules visits separately.
- **It holds medium- and maximum-security men.**
- **It opened in 1998** under a private operator and came under direct state operation in 2001.
- **It runs barbering and agricultural programs** (including a garden and bees).

## Visiting

The statewide ADC rules above — the approved visitor list, the no-white dress code, ID, and pre-scheduled visits — apply at Grimes.

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturdays and Sundays for routine visits; how many visits a month depends on the inmate's class

**Scheduling:** Request a visit through the TeleGov online portal, or call the unit's Visitation Clerk

**Hours:** ADC does not post Grimes's specific visiting hours online — call (870) 523-5877 to confirm
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Arkansas](/states/arkansas/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The unit is on Corrections Drive northeast of Newport, in northeast Arkansas.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 4 miles northeast of Newport, off Highway 384; roughly 85 miles northeast of Little Rock

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the unit; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Newport has the nearest gas, food, and lodging. The nearest hospital emergency room is **Unity Health – Harris Medical Center** in Newport.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the unit. Since September 2025, ADC routes non-legal personal letters and photos to an **off-site scanning center in Tampa, Florida**, addressed with the person's name and **ADC number**; the person receives the mail electronically. **Legal mail, books, and magazines** still go directly to the unit at the Newport address. Full addressing details are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arkansas/guides/mail/).
