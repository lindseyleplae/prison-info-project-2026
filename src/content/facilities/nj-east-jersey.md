## Overview

East Jersey State Prison is a **maximum-security prison for men** near Rahway, in the Avenel section of Woodbridge Township. It was long known as **Rahway State Prison** (renamed in 1988) and opened in 1901; its large **domed building** is a local landmark, visible from Route 1-9 and the nearby rail line. It also operates a **minimum-security satellite camp**.

## What Makes East Jersey Different

- **It is a maximum-security men's prison** with a separate minimum-security camp on the grounds.
- **The historic Rahway name and the dome.** Many families and older records still refer to it as Rahway State Prison.
- **Transit access is unusually good for a state prison.** NJ Transit's Rahway and Avenel stations are both within about a mile, in a densely served part of central New Jersey.

## Visiting

The statewide NJDOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at East Jersey. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Saturday and Sunday; registration begins at 7:30 a.m. for a 9:00–11:00 a.m. session, with **two-hour** contact visits and one contact visit per day

**Scheduling:** Call (732) 499-5010 ext. 2908, prompt 1, Monday through Thursday, 8 a.m. to 4 p.m., at least 48 hours ahead

**How many:** Up to four adult visitors and an unlimited number of children per visiting period
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in New Jersey](/states/new-jersey/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Woodbridge Road off U.S. Route 1-9, near Rahway.

:::key-info
**From Newark:** About 15 to 20 miles — roughly 25 to 35 minutes by car

**By transit:** NJ Transit's Rahway station (about half a mile) and Avenel station (about a mile) are the nearest rail stations; a short ride or walk completes the trip
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The Rahway and Woodbridge area, along the Route 1 corridor, has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **RWJ University Hospital Rahway** at 865 Stone Street in Rahway.

## Mail

Personal **letters** do not go to the prison — they go to the state's **off-site scanning center**, addressed with the person's name, **SBI number**, and the facility's **code (1401)**, to **P.O. Box 96777, Las Vegas, NV 89193** (confirm the code and current format on NJDOC's Staying Connected page). **Legal mail and publications** go directly to the facility at **1100 Woodbridge Road, Rahway, NJ 07065**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/new-jersey/guides/mail/).
