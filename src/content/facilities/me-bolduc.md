## Overview

Bolduc Correctional Facility is Maine's **minimum-security men's work-release facility**, sharing the Warren campus with the Maine State Prison. It holds men in minimum and community custody who have **five years or less left to serve**, and its focus is reentry: education, vocational training, substance-use treatment, and a **community work-release program** that lets residents work in the community as they near release. Reflecting that community-custody mission, the facility is led by a **director** rather than a warden.

## What Makes Bolduc Different

- **It is a work-release facility.** Bolduc is built around community work release for men close to the end of their sentences.
- **It shares the Warren campus.** Bolduc sits next to the Maine State Prison, but it is a separate facility with its own address and visit line — confirm which one holds a person.
- **A different leadership structure.** A director and a community programs coordinator run the facility, in keeping with its pre-release role.

## Visiting

The statewide Maine DOC rules above — the application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Bolduc. The facility's own schedule:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Saturday and Sunday, plus most major holidays — 8:00 to 10:30 a.m., 12:30 to 2:30 p.m., and 3:00 to 5:00 p.m.

**Length:** Two-hour visits

**Scheduling:** Approved visitors call (207) 273-5125, Monday through Thursday, 8:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Maine](/states/maine/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The facility is on Cushing Road in Warren, off Route 1 near Thomaston and Rockland.

:::key-info
**From Augusta:** About 45 miles — roughly 50 minutes

**From Portland:** About 85 miles — roughly 1 hour 45 minutes

**Transit:** No public transit serves the facility. The nearest intercity bus is Concord Coach Lines in Rockland, about 10 to 12 miles away; a vehicle is needed for the final leg
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Rockland and Thomaston, about 10 to 15 minutes away, have gas, food, and lodging, with Rockland the largest nearby hub. **MaineHealth Pen Bay Hospital** at 6 Glen Cove Drive in Rockport runs a 24/7 emergency room, about 12 to 15 miles from the facility.

## Mail

First-class **letters** and **packages** for Bolduc both go to **516 Cushing Road, Warren, ME 04864**, with the resident's name and MDOC number. Full rules, including legal mail, are in [Mail & Packages](/states/maine/guides/mail/).
