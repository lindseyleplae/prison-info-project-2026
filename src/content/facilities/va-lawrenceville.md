## Overview

Lawrenceville Correctional Center, in Brunswick County, is a **medium-security prison for men**. Opened in 1998, it was **Virginia's only privately operated state prison** — run by a private company, GEO Group, from 2003 — until **VADOC assumed operation of it on August 1, 2024**. It is now a state-run facility following standard VADOC procedures, and it was chosen as one of the first **pilot sites for VADOC's new ViaPath tablet system** in 2026.

## What Makes Lawrenceville Different

- **It recently changed hands.** Families who knew the prison under its private operator should expect **VADOC's procedures now** — the centralized online visitor application and scheduler, VADOC mail handling, and VADOC's phone and tablet systems.
- **A tablet pilot site.** Lawrenceville was among the first facilities to receive VADOC's new ViaPath tablets, which changed how calls and messaging work — see the phone and video guide.
- **South-central Virginia location.** It is off I-85 in Brunswick County, roughly an hour and a half from Richmond.

## Visiting

The statewide VADOC rules above — the online application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Lawrenceville. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays — confirm the current hours with the facility

**Video:** Video visits are offered, including from home on a computer or Android device
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Virginia](/states/virginia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Planters Road in Lawrenceville, off the I-85 corridor in Brunswick County.

:::key-info
**From Richmond:** About 70 miles — roughly 1 hour 30 minutes via I-85

**From the South Hill area:** About 18 miles — roughly 25 minutes

**Transit:** No intercity service reaches Lawrenceville directly; a vehicle is the practical way to reach the prison
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Lawrenceville is a small town; the nearest larger clusters of gas, food, and lodging are in **South Hill** (about 25 minutes west) and **Emporia** (about 30 minutes east, at I-95). The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **VCU Health Community Memorial Hospital** in South Hill, about 18 miles away.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the facility at **1607 Planters Road, Lawrenceville, VA 23868**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go instead to the Central Mail Distribution Center in State Farm. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
