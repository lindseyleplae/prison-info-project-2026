## Overview

Walker State Prison, on Kevin Lane in Rock Spring in Walker County, is a **medium-security state prison for men** in northwest Georgia, with a capacity of about **444**. GDC designates it as a **faith- and character-based program prison** — a facility built around a voluntary program for men who request to take part. Alongside that programming, the prison provides counseling, religious activities such as worship services and Bible study, and residential substance-abuse treatment with counseling and mentoring.

## What Makes Walker Different

- **It is a specially designated faith- and character-based program prison.** GDC describes it as providing a programmatic environment for men who voluntarily request to participate, with the stated aims of fostering moral character development and supporting spiritual enrichment across different faith traditions.
- **Participation is voluntary,** so a person enters the program by request and may transfer in or out as their assignment changes.
- **It offers residential substance-abuse treatment** with counseling and mentoring, in addition to the faith- and character-based programming.
- **It is in far northwest Georgia,** near LaFayette and within reach of the Chattanooga, Tennessee, area.

## Visiting

The statewide GDC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Walker State Prison. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays — confirm the current hours with the facility

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Georgia](/states/georgia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Kevin Lane in Rock Spring, in Walker County in the far northwest corner of Georgia, near LaFayette.

:::key-info
**By car:** Rock Spring is between LaFayette and Chattanooga, Tennessee, off the U.S. 27 corridor; the Chattanooga metro area is roughly 25 miles north

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

LaFayette, just south, has the closest gas and food; Fort Oglethorpe and the Chattanooga, Tennessee, area to the north have a fuller range of food and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Fort Oglethorpe and Chattanooga area, about 25 to 30 minutes north.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's full name and **GDC ID number**. Georgia is moving toward centralized mail handling, so confirm the current mailing address before sending. Books and magazines must come from a publisher or established retailer, and legal mail is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/georgia/guides/mail/).
