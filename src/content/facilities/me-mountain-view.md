## Overview

Mountain View Correctional Facility holds **men in medium and minimum custody** in Charleston, northeast of Bangor. It was created in **2017**, when the Legislature repurposed the former Mountain View Youth Development Center as an adult facility. Alongside general programming — education, vocational training, and substance-use and mental-health treatment — it runs a **young-adult program** for residents 25 and under with five years or less to serve, a **logging program**, and a community work-release program for minimum-custody residents. A unit on the grounds provides **long-term and end-of-life medical care** for residents from across the system.

## What Makes Mountain View Different

- **A young-adult track.** The facility runs a dedicated program for younger residents nearing release.
- **Vocational programs.** These include a logging program, and residents have taken part in building shelters for homeless veterans.
- **A children's playroom.** Mountain View offers a playroom for children under 12 in 45-minute blocks, which must be requested when scheduling a visit.

## Visiting

The statewide Maine DOC rules above — the application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Mountain View. The facility's own schedule:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Saturday 2:00 to 3:30 p.m. and 7:00 to 8:30 p.m.; Sunday 2:00 to 3:30 p.m. Arrive 15 minutes early

**How often:** One visit per weekend and two per month per visitor

**With children:** A playroom for children under 12 runs in 45-minute blocks — request it when scheduling
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Maine](/states/maine/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The facility is on Dover Road in Charleston, reached from the Bangor area.

:::key-info
**From Bangor:** About 30 minutes northeast

**From Portland:** About 135 miles — roughly 2 hours 15 minutes

**Transit:** No public transit serves the facility. The nearest intercity bus is Concord Coach Lines in Bangor, about 30 minutes away; a vehicle is needed for the final leg
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Charleston is rural; the nearest full range of gas, food, and lodging is in the **Bangor area**, about 30 minutes south, with limited services in nearby Dover-Foxcroft. **Northern Light Eastern Maine Medical Center** in Bangor runs a 24/7 emergency room and is the region's trauma center.

## Mail

Mountain View uses Maine's split mail routing. First-class **letters** go to **809 Cushing Road, Warren, ME 04864** (the central scanning address), while **packages, magazines, and legal mail** go to the campus at **1182 Dover Road, Charleston, ME 04422**. Put the resident's name and MDOC number on everything — full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/maine/guides/mail/).
