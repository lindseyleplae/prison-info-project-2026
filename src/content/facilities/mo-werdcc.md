## Overview

The Women's Eastern Reception, Diagnostic and Correctional Center (WERDCC), on East Highway 54 in Vandalia, is **Missouri's reception and diagnostic center for women** — the single statewide intake point where every woman entering the prison system is received, assessed (medical, mental health, and custody classification), and then assigned and transferred to a permanent prison. It also holds a general-population women's population. Because of its reception role, a woman may be here only briefly, and the prison shown in the offender search can change within the first weeks or months. It opened in 1998 and holds about **2,000 women** across minimum to maximum security. It is the **designated facility for a woman under a death sentence** in Missouri.

## What Makes WERDCC Different

- **It is the statewide women's reception and diagnostic center,** so a newly committed woman begins here before transferring to a permanent prison.
- **It is the designated facility for a woman under a death sentence** in Missouri.
- **Visiting is limited during the reception period,** so confirm the person's status and location before traveling.
- **It is in Vandalia, in northeast Missouri,** between Mexico and Hannibal.

## Visiting

The statewide Missouri DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at WERDCC. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Missouri DOC online visiting application before visiting

**Reception status:** A woman in reception has limited visiting and will transfer to a permanent prison after classification

**Schedule:** The facility sets its own visiting days and hours — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Missouri](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on East Highway 54 in Vandalia, in northeast Missouri.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Vandalia, reached via U.S. Highway 54; Mexico, Missouri is the nearest larger city, about 20 miles to the west

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Vandalia has limited services; Mexico, Missouri, about 20 miles west, has the fuller range of gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **SSM Health St. Mary's Hospital–Audrain**, in Mexico.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
