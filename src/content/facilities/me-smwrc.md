## Overview

The Southern Maine Women's Reentry Center is Maine's **minimum-security, pre-release facility for women** — those with five years or less left to serve, preparing for release. It **opened in Windham in 2017**, a purpose-built facility that replaced a leased site in Alfred, and it operates alongside the Maine Correctional Center's Women's Center, which holds women at higher custody levels. Programming centers on reentry: education, substance-use treatment, cognitive-behavioral classes, community restitution, and a community work-release program.

## What Makes the Reentry Center Different

- **It is a women's pre-release facility.** Women here are close to release and may take part in community work release.
- **Outside food is allowed in visits.** Unlike the rest of the system, the Reentry Center lets visitors bring outside food into the visiting room (in sealed containers, with paper goods and plastic utensils) — confirm the current rules when scheduling.
- **Free video visits.** The facility is one of Maine's sites for free Ameelio video, voice, and messaging.

## Visiting

The statewide Maine DOC rules above — the application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at the Reentry Center. The facility's own schedule:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Sunday 9:30 to 11:00 a.m. and 1:00 to 2:30 p.m.; Tuesday and Thursday 3:30 to 5:00 p.m. Arrive at least 15 minutes early

**Scheduling:** By appointment through the SMWRC online visit-request form, at least two business days ahead; the line (207) 893-7109 is for questions only

**Food:** Visitors may bring outside food into the visiting room — an exception to the usual rule
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Maine](/states/maine/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The facility is in Windham, northwest of Portland.

:::key-info
**From Portland:** About 15 miles — roughly 20 minutes

**From Augusta:** About 60 miles — roughly 1 hour 15 minutes

**Transit:** The Lakes Region Explorer bus serves the Route 302 corridor in Windham, not the facility; a vehicle is needed for the final leg
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Windham and North Windham, on Route 302, have gas, food, and lodging, with more in Portland a short drive south. **MaineHealth Maine Medical Center** at 22 Bramhall Street in Portland is the region's Level I trauma center with a 24/7 emergency room.

## Mail

The Reentry Center uses Maine's split mail routing. First-class **letters** go to **813 Cushing Road, Warren, ME 04864** (the central scanning address), while **packages, magazines, and legal mail** go **c/o Maine Correctional Center, 17 Mallison Falls Road, Windham, ME 04062**. Put the resident's name and MDOC number on everything — full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/maine/guides/mail/).
