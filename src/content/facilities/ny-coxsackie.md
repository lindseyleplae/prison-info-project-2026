## Overview

Coxsackie Correctional Facility, in the town of Coxsackie, is a **maximum-security prison** for men in Greene County, in the Hudson Valley. Like all maximum-security facilities, visiting and movement are more controlled than at lower-security prisons, and some housing units have non-contact visits.

Coxsackie Correctional Facility (maximum security) is a **separate prison** from the nearby Greene Correctional Facility (medium security), which is also in the town of Coxsackie. Families should confirm the exact facility on the DOCCS Incarcerated Lookup before traveling or sending mail.

## What Makes Coxsackie Different

- **It is a maximum-security prison,** so visiting, movement, and property are more restricted.
- **It offers Wednesday and weekend visiting,** unlike facilities that schedule visits only on weekends.
- **It is in the Hudson Valley,** in Greene County.
- **Some housing is non-contact,** so confirm the person's unit and visit type before traveling.

## Visiting

The statewide DOCCS rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Coxsackie Correctional Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Days:** Coxsackie offers visiting on Wednesdays and on weekends, within the facility's posted hours — confirm the current schedule

**Screening:** A body scanner is required for a full-contact visit (a medical exemption can be requested)
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in New York](/states/new-york/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

Coxsackie and the surrounding Greene County area have gas, food, and lodging, with more in the Catskill and Albany areas nearby. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Catskill/Albany area.

## Mail

New York does not use an off-site mail vendor — incoming personal mail is **opened and photocopied at the facility**, and the incarcerated person receives the **photocopies** (images may also reach their JPay tablet) rather than the original letters. Address it with the person's **name and DIN (Department Identification Number)** and the facility's mailing address (**P.O. Box 999, Coxsackie, NY 12051-0999**). **Legal mail** goes to the facility and is handled under privileged-mail rules. **Books, publications, and packages** must be ordered from **approved vendors**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/new-york/guides/mail/).
