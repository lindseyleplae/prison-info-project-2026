## Overview

Washington Correctional Facility, on Lock Eleven Lane in Comstock, Washington County, in eastern New York near the Vermont border, is a **medium-security state prison for men**. It holds a medium-security general population with programming that includes education and vocational training, substance-abuse treatment, and transitional services.

## What Makes Washington Different

- **It is a medium-security men's prison,** so visiting and movement are less restricted than at a maximum-security facility.
- **It is the open DOCCS prison in Comstock;** the separate Great Meadow Correctional Facility, also in Comstock, closed in 2024 — confirm the exact facility before traveling.
- **It is in Comstock, Washington County,** in eastern New York near the Vermont border.

## Visiting

The statewide DOCCS rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Washington Correctional Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Hours:** Visits are within the facility's posted hours; some visits require advance scheduling — confirm the current schedule

**Confirm the facility:** Washington Correctional Facility and the closed Great Meadow Correctional Facility share the town of Comstock — confirm the current facility on the DOCCS Incarcerated Lookup before traveling
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in New York](/states/new-york/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

Comstock and the surrounding Washington County area have gas, food, and lodging, with more in the Glens Falls and Saratoga Springs areas to the west. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Glens Falls area.

## Mail

New York does not use an off-site mail vendor — incoming personal mail is **opened and photocopied at the facility**, and the incarcerated person receives the **photocopies** (images may also reach their JPay tablet) rather than the original letters. Address it with the person's **name and DIN (Department Identification Number)** and the facility's address. **Legal mail** goes to the facility and is handled under privileged-mail rules. **Books, publications, and packages** must be ordered from **approved vendors**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/new-york/guides/mail/).
