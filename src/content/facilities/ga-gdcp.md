## Overview

The Georgia Diagnostic and Classification State Prison (GDCP), on Highway 36 west of Jackson in Butts County, is **Georgia's statewide intake and classification prison for men**. Most newly sentenced men enter the GDC system here, where they are assessed — medically, psychologically, and educationally — and assigned a security level and a permanent facility. Because of that role, a man may be at GDCP only briefly before transfer, so the prison shown in the inmate search can change. GDCP is a **close-security** facility holding about **2,487 men**, and it also holds the state's **male death row** and the **execution chamber**, along with a 192-bed special management (restrictive-housing) unit.

## What Makes GDCP Different

- **It is the men's intake and classification center,** so a newly sentenced man is usually processed here before transferring to a permanent prison.
- **Visits are limited during intake** — no visits for the first 60 days in diagnostic status, and then only immediate family.
- **It holds the state's male death row and the execution chamber.** Executions in Georgia are carried out here.
- **It is about 50 miles south of Atlanta,** off Interstate 75.

## Visiting

The statewide GDC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at GDCP. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays, generally 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. — confirm with the facility

**Diagnostic status:** A person in intake cannot receive visits for their first 60 days, and then only from immediate family

**Scheduling:** By appointment through GDC's online visitation portal; the visitor must first be approved on the person's list, and requests for a given week are accepted Monday through Wednesday
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Georgia](/states/georgia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Highway 36 west of Jackson, off the Interstate 75 corridor south of Atlanta.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 50 miles south of Atlanta — take Interstate 75 south to Exit 201 (Jackson), then Highway 36 west to the facility

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Jackson and nearby Griffin have the closest gas, food, and lodging; Atlanta, about 50 miles north, has the fullest range. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Wellstar Sylvan Grove Medical Center** in Jackson; **Wellstar Spalding Medical Center** in Griffin is a larger hospital about 20 minutes west.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's full name and **GDC ID number**. Georgia is moving toward centralized mail handling, so confirm the current mailing address before sending. Books and magazines must come from a publisher or established retailer, and legal mail is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/georgia/guides/mail/).
