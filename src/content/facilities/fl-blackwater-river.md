## Overview

Blackwater River Correctional and Rehabilitation Facility is a **men's prison** near Milton in Santa Rosa County, in the Florida Panhandle. It is privately operated by the GEO Group under contract for the Florida Department of Corrections, holds men in medium and close custody, and has a capacity of about 2,000 — the largest private prison in Florida.

## What Makes Blackwater River Different

- **It is privately operated by the GEO Group** under contract for the FDC, with the contract monitored by the state Department of Management Services; the people held here are in FDC custody and appear on the FDC Corrections Offender Network.
- **It is the largest private prison in Florida,** a campus-style facility that opened in 2010.
- **It offers academic and vocational programs,** along with medical care, substance-abuse programming, counseling, and life-skills training.
- **Phone accounts run through the operator's vendor** (ViaPath, set up through ConnectNetwork); confirm the current phone, video, and deposit vendors with the facility and in the [Sending Money](/states/florida/guides/money/) guide.

## Mail

Florida does not deliver original personal mail to the prison. Routine personal letters and photos go to an off-site **Smart Communications** processing center, where they are **opened and scanned** and delivered electronically — the person reads them on a tablet or kiosk, and the mailroom may print copies. Address routine mail with the person's **full name and DC number**, to: **Smart Communications, P.O. Box 23608, Tampa, FL 33623**. Confirm the current address and format on FDC's website before sending. **Legal mail and privileged correspondence go directly to the institution**, not to the scanning center. **Publications** must come from an approved publisher or retailer. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/florida/guides/mail/).
