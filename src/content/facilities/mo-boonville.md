## Overview

Boonville Correctional Center, on East Morgan Street in Boonville, is a **minimum-security prison for men** in central Missouri. It sits on roughly 80 acres in **Cooper County**, west of Columbia. Visiting days, hours, and other arrangements are set by the facility and can change, so confirm them before traveling.

## What Makes Boonville Different

- **It is a minimum-security prison** for men in central Missouri.
- **It sits on about 80 acres in Cooper County,** in Boonville, west of Columbia.
- **Visiting days and hours are set by the facility** and can change — confirm them before traveling.
- **People are transferred between Missouri prisons,** so confirm which one holds the person before traveling.

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on East Morgan Street in Boonville, in central Missouri.

:::key-info
**By car:** Boonville is in central Missouri on Interstate 70, west of Columbia; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Boonville has gas, food, and lodging, and the larger Columbia area is a short drive east on I-70. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Boonville and Columbia area.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
