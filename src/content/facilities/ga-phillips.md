## Overview

Phillips State Prison, on West Rock Quarry Road in Buford in Gwinnett County, is a **medium-security GDC prison for men with a mental-health treatment mission**. According to the GDC, the facility houses men who have had management problems at other prisons or centers and those who could pose a risk if housed elsewhere, and it provides all levels of mental-health service, including Level IV care and crisis stabilization. Its capacity is about **918 men**. Because men are assigned here from across the system, the prison shown in the inmate search can change.

## What Makes Phillips Different

- **It has a statewide mental-health treatment mission,** providing all levels of mental-health service, including Level IV care and crisis stabilization.
- **It houses men transferred from other facilities** — men who have had management problems elsewhere or who could pose a risk if housed elsewhere.
- **It offers structured mental-health programming,** including mental-health groups and counseling, alongside academic, vocational, and reentry programs.
- **It is in metro Atlanta,** in Buford, northeast of the city off Interstate 985.

## Visiting

The statewide GDC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Phillips State Prison. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays, generally 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. — confirm with the facility

**Scheduling:** By appointment through GDC's online visitation portal; the visitor must first be approved on the person's list, and requests for a given week are accepted Monday through Wednesday
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Georgia](/states/georgia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is in Buford, Gwinnett County, in metro Atlanta, off the Interstate 985 / Interstate 85 corridor northeast of the city.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Buford, northeast of Atlanta — from Interstate 85 north, take Interstate 985 toward Gainesville, then follow surface streets to West Rock Quarry Road

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Buford and the surrounding Gwinnett County suburbs — including Sugar Hill, Suwanee, and Gainesville to the north — have gas, food, and lodging; Atlanta, to the southwest, has the fullest range. Northeast Georgia Medical Center has 24-hour emergency rooms in Gainesville and Braselton, and several hospital emergency departments operate across Gwinnett County.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's full name and **GDC ID number**. Georgia is moving toward centralized mail handling, so confirm the current mailing address before sending. Books and magazines must come from a publisher or established retailer, and legal mail is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/georgia/guides/mail/).
