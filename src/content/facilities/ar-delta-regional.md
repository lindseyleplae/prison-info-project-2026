## Overview

The Delta Regional Unit, at Dermott in the far southeast corner of Arkansas, is a **regional men's prison** with a rated capacity of about 599. It also operates a jail and runs the WAGE workforce program and other education and vocational offerings. Its defining feature for families is its remoteness — deep in the Arkansas Delta, about 50 miles southeast of Pine Bluff.

## What Makes Delta Regional Different

- **It is one of the most remote units in the state,** about 50 miles southeast of Pine Bluff — the drive is the defining logistical fact for visitors.
- **It is a regional unit that also operates a jail.**
- **It runs the WAGE workforce program** and other education and vocational programs.
- **There is no public transit** to the unit.

## Visiting

The statewide ADC rules above — the approved visitor list, the no-white dress code, ID, and pre-scheduled visits — apply at the Delta Regional Unit.

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturdays and Sundays for routine visits; how many visits a month depends on the inmate's class

**Scheduling:** Request a visit through the TeleGov online portal, or call the unit's Visitation Clerk

**Hours:** ADC does not post the unit's specific visiting hours online — call (870) 538-2000 to confirm
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Arkansas](/states/arkansas/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The unit is on East Gaines in Dermott, in the southeast Delta.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 50 miles southeast of Pine Bluff and about 120 miles southeast of Little Rock, via U.S. 65 and U.S. 165

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the unit; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Dermott is small and no longer has a hospital. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are **Chicot Memorial Medical Center** in Lake Village, to the south, and **McGehee-Desha County Hospital** in McGehee, to the north. Lodging and food are limited near the unit.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the unit. Since September 2025, ADC routes non-legal personal letters and photos to an **off-site scanning center in Tampa, Florida**, addressed with the person's name and **ADC number**; the person receives the mail electronically. **Legal mail, books, and magazines** still go directly to the unit at the Dermott address. Full addressing details are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arkansas/guides/mail/).
