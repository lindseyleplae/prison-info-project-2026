## Overview

Saginaw Correctional Facility, on Pierce Road in Freeland, is a **Level I, II, and IV men's prison** in Saginaw County. It opened in **1993**. It has a **Veterans' Unit** and an associate-degree program through **Delta College**, and it carries a reentry **In-Reach** designation. It also runs **dog-training programs** in partnership with Paws With A Cause, Blue Star, and Iron Paws. Freeland is about 12 to 15 miles northwest of the city of Saginaw.

## What Makes Saginaw Different

- **It has a Veterans' Unit.**
- **It hosts a Delta College associate-degree program.**
- **It carries a reentry In-Reach designation.**
- **It runs dog-training programs** (Paws With A Cause, Blue Star, and Iron Paws).

## Visiting

The statewide MDOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Saginaw. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Scheduling:** All visits are by appointment, through the ViaPath scheduler (midoc.gtlvisitme.com), 48 hours to 7 days ahead

**Approved list:** Each visitor must first be approved on the prisoner's visiting list (apply with form CAJ-103)

**Custody levels:** The facility holds Level I, II, and IV prisoners
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Michigan](/states/michigan/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Pierce Road in Freeland, northwest of the city of Saginaw.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Freeland, about 12 to 15 miles northwest of the city of Saginaw

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Saginaw has the nearest gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Covenant HealthCare** in Saginaw.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's name and **MDOC number**. Michigan **photocopies incoming mail in black and white and delivers the copy; the original is shredded**, so do not send originals you want kept. Color photos and messages can be sent through JPay. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/michigan/guides/mail/).
