## Overview

Gadsden Correctional Facility is a **privately operated women's prison** near Quincy in Gadsden County, in the Florida Panhandle west of Tallahassee. It is operated by Management & Training Corporation (MTC) under contract with the Florida Department of Corrections, holds women in minimum, medium, and community custody, and has a capacity of about 1,544.

## What Makes Gadsden Different

- **It is privately operated by MTC** under contract with the Florida Department of Corrections, with the contract monitored by the Florida Department of Management Services (DMS); the people held here remain in FDC custody.
- **It is a women's facility** — one of Florida's prisons for women.
- **It offers women's rehabilitation and vocational programming,** including horticulture training, cosmetology, education, and reentry initiatives.
- **Phone service runs through Securus, and money deposits are directed to Access Corrections** — confirm the current vendors and steps in [Phone & Video Calls](/states/florida/guides/phone-video/) and [Sending Money](/states/florida/guides/money/).

## Mail

Florida does not deliver original personal mail to the prison. Routine personal letters and photos go to an off-site **Smart Communications** processing center, where they are **opened and scanned** and delivered electronically — the person reads them on a tablet or kiosk, and the mailroom may print copies. Address routine mail with the person's **full name and DC number**, to: **Smart Communications, P.O. Box 23608, Tampa, FL 33623**. Confirm the current address and format on FDC's website before sending. **Legal mail and privileged correspondence go directly to the institution**, not to the scanning center. **Publications** must come from an approved publisher or retailer. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/florida/guides/mail/).
