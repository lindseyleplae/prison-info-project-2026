## Overview

Wheeler Correctional Facility, on North Broad Street in Alamo in Wheeler County, is a **medium-security state prison for men** in south-central Georgia. It opened in 1998 and has a capacity of about **2,874**. Wheeler is operated by **CoreCivic** under contract with the Georgia Department of Corrections, which means GDC's statewide rules — the approved visitor list, the dress code, mail handling, and the inmate locator — apply to the men held here.

## What Makes Wheeler Different

- **It is operated by CoreCivic under contract with GDC.** The facility is privately run, but it holds Georgia state inmates, so GDC visiting and mail rules apply.
- **The visitation schedule rotates by housing unit.** Confirm the current schedule with the facility before traveling.
- **It is in Alamo, in south-central Georgia,** roughly between Dublin and Vidalia.

## Visiting

The statewide GDC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Wheeler Correctional Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays — the schedule rotates by housing unit each quarter, so confirm the current days and hours with the facility

**Scheduling:** By appointment through GDC's online visitation portal; the visitor must first be approved on the person's list, and requests for a given week are accepted Monday through Wednesday
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Georgia](/states/georgia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on North Broad Street in Alamo, in Wheeler County in south-central Georgia.

:::key-info
**By car:** Alamo is at the junction of U.S. 280 and State Route 19/State Route 126, about 35 miles southeast of Dublin and about 30 miles northwest of Vidalia

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Alamo has limited services; Dublin to the northwest and Vidalia to the southeast have the closest range of gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in those towns — **Fairview Park Hospital** and **Piedmont Dublin** in Dublin, and **Memorial Health Meadows Hospital** in Vidalia.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's full name and **GDC ID number**. Georgia is moving toward centralized mail handling, so confirm the current mailing address before sending. Books and magazines must come from a publisher or established retailer, and legal mail is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/georgia/guides/mail/).
