## Overview

Taylor Correctional Institution is a **close-custody general-population men's prison** near Perry in Taylor County, in the Big Bend region of north Florida. It is operated by the Florida Department of Corrections and holds men in mixed custody — minimum, medium, and close — with a main-unit capacity of about 1,301.

## What Makes Taylor Different

- **It is a major close-custody men's institution** in the Big Bend region of north Florida.
- **An adjacent Taylor Annex** operates on the same site (capacity about 1,409). A visitor may be directed to the main institution or to the Annex depending on where the person is housed — confirm the exact unit on the FDC offender search before traveling.
- **It offers standard rehabilitative programming,** including education, vocational training, substance-abuse programs, and faith services.
- **The institution shown in the offender search can change** if the person is transferred.

## Mail

Florida does not deliver original personal mail to the prison. Routine personal letters and photos go to an off-site **Smart Communications** processing center, where they are **opened and scanned** and delivered electronically — the person reads them on a tablet or kiosk, and the mailroom may print copies. Address routine mail with the person's **full name and DC number**, to: **Smart Communications, P.O. Box 23608, Tampa, FL 33623**. Confirm the current address and format on FDC's website before sending. **Legal mail and privileged correspondence go directly to the institution**, not to the scanning center. **Publications** must come from an approved publisher or retailer. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/florida/guides/mail/).
