## Overview

Delta Correctional Facility, in Greenwood, is a **state-run women's prison** that MDOC **reopened in 2022** with a focus on **rehabilitation and workforce development** (vocational training, GED education, and substance-abuse counseling). It holds about **400 women** (September 2025), many of whom were **transferred from the Central Mississippi Correctional Facility (CMCF) in Pearl** in early 2023, when MDOC consolidated part of its female population into Greenwood. Because of that move, Mississippi now houses women at **two** facilities — Delta and CMCF — so families should confirm which one holds the person.

## What Makes Delta Different

- It is **one of Mississippi's two women's prisons** (with CMCF in Pearl), reopened in 2022.
- Its mission centers on **workforce development and rehabilitation** — vocational training, education, and substance-abuse counseling.
- Many women were **moved here from Pearl in 2023**, placing them in the rural Delta, farther from the Jackson metro.
- It was a privately run prison years ago; the state reopened it as a women's facility (see history).

## A Note on History

The facility opened in **1996** as a privately operated prison, **closed in 2012** when the state had excess capacity, and sat unused until the state **reopened it in 2022 as a women's prison**. This history is documented in the sources cited above.

## Visiting

The statewide MDOC rules above — the inmate-initiated application, the approved list, the dress code, ID, and screening — apply at Delta. MDOC posts no schedule, and visiting frequency runs by custody level, so confirm the assigned day by phone. There are no video visits at MDOC state prisons. The full approval process is in [Visiting in Mississippi](/states/mississippi/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

Greenwood is in the Mississippi Delta, reached by U.S. 82 and Interstate 55.

:::key-info
**From Jackson:** About 95-100 miles north via Interstate 55 and U.S. 82 — roughly 2 hours

**From Memphis, TN:** About 130 miles south via U.S. 49 and other Delta highways — roughly 2 hours

**Parking:** Visitor parking is available on-site.
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. There is no public transit to the facility.

## Nearby Services

Greenwood has gas, food, and lodging. The area's hospital, **Greenwood Leflore Hospital**, has been in serious financial distress and has warned it could close in 2026, so confirm its emergency room is open before relying on it; larger 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Jackson area and in Greenville to the west.

## Mail

Mail to a person at Delta follows MDOC's process: addressed with the person's committed name and MDOC number. Mississippi photocopies incoming personal mail rather than delivering the originals; full addressing details are in [Mail & Packages](/states/mississippi/guides/mail/).
