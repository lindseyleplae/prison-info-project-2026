## Overview

Beaumont Correctional Center, on the **State Farm Correctional Complex** in Powhatan County, is a **small medium-security work facility for men**, with an operational capacity of about 248. It provides labor for the **Virginia Correctional Enterprises** shops and houses the **Capital Construction Work Detail** — men with electrical, plumbing, HVAC, and construction training who work on department construction projects. Housing at Beaumont has restrictive eligibility: a minimum 15-year sentence and a high school diploma or equivalency. The complex is about 25–30 miles west of Richmond.

## What Makes Beaumont Different

- **It is a small work facility.** Operational capacity is about 248 — much smaller than most VADOC prisons.
- **A Virginia Correctional Enterprises mission.** Men assigned here provide labor for the Virginia Correctional Enterprises shops.
- **The Capital Construction Work Detail.** Beaumont houses men with electrical, plumbing, HVAC, and construction training who work on department construction projects.
- **Restrictive housing eligibility.** Assignment requires a minimum 15-year sentence and a high school diploma or equivalency.

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Beaumont Road in the State Farm area of Powhatan County.

:::key-info
**From Richmond:** About 25–30 miles west

**Transit:** No fixed-route public transit serves the State Farm area; a vehicle is the practical way to reach the prison
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The State Farm complex is rural; **Midlothian** and the western Richmond suburbs (to the east) have gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Bon Secours St. Francis Medical Center** in Midlothian.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the facility at **3500 Beaumont Road, Beaumont, VA 23014**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go instead to the Central Mail Distribution Center in State Farm. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
