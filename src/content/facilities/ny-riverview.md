## Overview

Riverview Correctional Facility, in Ogdensburg, is a **medium-security prison** for men in St. Lawrence County, in the far North Country on the St. Lawrence River near the Canadian border. It holds a general population and offers substance-abuse treatment, education and vocational, and transitional programming. It is the open DOCCS prison in the Ogdensburg area — the separate Ogdensburg Correctional Facility closed in 2021 — so confirm the exact facility on the DOCCS Incarcerated Lookup before traveling.

## What Makes Riverview Different

- **It is a medium-security prison,** with a general population and standard programming.
- **It is in the far North Country,** in Ogdensburg on the St. Lawrence River near the Canadian border — a long drive from downstate.
- **It has a Special Housing Unit (SHU)** for disciplinary confinement; visiting for that housing is more limited.
- **It is the open Ogdensburg-area DOCCS prison;** the separate Ogdensburg Correctional Facility closed in 2021.

## Visiting

The statewide DOCCS rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at Riverview Correctional Facility. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Hours:** Visits are within the facility's posted hours; some visits require advance scheduling — confirm the current schedule

**Screening:** A body scanner is required for a full-contact visit (a medical exemption can be requested)
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in New York](/states/new-york/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is in Ogdensburg, in St. Lawrence County, in the far North Country on the St. Lawrence River. It is a long drive from downstate New York and the New York City area.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Ogdensburg, St. Lawrence County, in the far North Country near the Canadian border; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no direct public-transit service to the facility; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Ogdensburg and the surrounding St. Lawrence County area have gas, food, and lodging, with more in the Watertown and Potsdam areas. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Ogdensburg/Watertown area.

## Mail

New York does not use an off-site mail vendor — incoming personal mail is **opened and photocopied at the facility**, and the incarcerated person receives the **photocopies** (images may also reach their JPay tablet) rather than the original letters. Address it with the person's **name and DIN (Department Identification Number)** and the facility's address. **Legal mail** goes to the facility and is handled under privileged-mail rules. **Books, publications, and packages** must be ordered from **approved vendors**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/new-york/guides/mail/).
