## Overview

The Maine State Prison is the state's prison for **men at higher custody levels**, holding close, medium, and special-management custody. It opened at its current site in **Warren in 2002**, replacing the historic prison in Thomaston that had operated since 1824. The prison includes the **Intensive Mental Health Unit** for residents with acute psychiatric needs, and it serves as the reception point for the few men who cannot be received at the Maine Correctional Center. Maine has **no death penalty** — abolished in 1887 — so there is no death row here. Programming includes education, college courses through the University of Maine at Augusta, vocational training, and substance-use and mental-health treatment.

## What Makes the Maine State Prison Different

- **The Showroom.** The **Maine State Prison Showroom** at 358 Main Street (U.S. Route 1) in Thomaston is a public store selling furniture, woodcraft, and other goods made by residents — a long-running, publicly visited program distinct to this prison.
- **It is the higher-custody men's prison.** Close and special-management custody, and the Intensive Mental Health Unit, are here.
- **Midcoast location.** Set in coastal Knox County near Rockland, it is a significant drive from southern Maine — roughly 1 hour 50 minutes from Portland, but closer to Augusta.

## Visiting

The statewide Maine DOC rules above — the application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at the Maine State Prison. The facility's own schedule:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Thursday through Sunday, 8:30 to 10:30 a.m. and 1:30 to 3:30 p.m., for general-population residents in the close- and medium-custody units

**Scheduling:** By appointment, at least two business days ahead — call (207) 273-5526 (Monday through Wednesday, mornings) or email Visit.MSP@maine.gov

**With children:** A child may visit the visiting-room bookcase and bring a book or toy back to the table; an in-room photo service is available
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Maine](/states/maine/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Cushing Road in Warren, off Route 1 near Thomaston and Rockland.

:::key-info
**From Portland:** About 74 miles — roughly 1 hour 50 minutes

**From Augusta:** About 40 miles — roughly 1 hour 10 minutes

**Transit:** No public transit serves the prison. The nearest intercity bus is Concord Coach Lines in Rockland, about 10 to 12 miles away; a vehicle is needed for the final leg
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Rockland and Thomaston, about 10 to 15 minutes away, have gas, food, and lodging, with Rockland the largest nearby hub. **MaineHealth Pen Bay Hospital** at 6 Glen Cove Drive in Rockport runs a 24/7 emergency room — about 12 to 15 miles from the prison.

## Mail

First-class **letters** and **packages** for the Maine State Prison both go to **807 Cushing Road, Warren, ME 04864-4600**, with the resident's name and MDOC number — the prison is the rare Maine facility where the letter and package addresses are the same. Full rules, including legal mail, are in [Mail & Packages](/states/maine/guides/mail/).
