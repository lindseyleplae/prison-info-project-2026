## Overview

Alger Correctional Facility, on Industrial Park Drive in Munising, is a **Level IV and II men's prison** in the Upper Peninsula, and it also holds a segregation unit. It opened in **1990**. Its **Level II unit runs a Rescue Dog Training Program**. For families, the defining fact is **distance**: Alger is about **43 miles east of Marquette** via M-28 and roughly **460 miles — about seven hours — from Detroit**.

## What Makes Alger Different

- **It is a Level IV and II men's prison,** and also holds a segregation unit.
- **Its Level II unit runs a Rescue Dog Training Program.**
- **It is in the Upper Peninsula,** about 43 miles east of Marquette via M-28.
- **It is remote,** roughly 460 miles (about seven hours) from Detroit.

## Visiting

The statewide MDOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Alger. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Scheduling:** All visits are by appointment, through the ViaPath scheduler (midoc.gtlvisitme.com), 48 hours to 7 days ahead

**Approved list:** Each visitor must first be approved on the prisoner's visiting list (apply with form CAJ-103)

**Schedule:** Confirm the facility's current visiting days and hours on MDOC's visiting page
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Michigan](/states/michigan/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

Munising has the nearest gas, food, and lodging; Marquette, about 43 miles west, has more. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Munising Memorial Hospital** in Munising.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's name and **MDOC number**. Michigan **photocopies incoming mail in black and white and delivers the copy; the original is shredded**, so do not send originals you want kept. Color photos and messages can be sent through JPay. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/michigan/guides/mail/).
