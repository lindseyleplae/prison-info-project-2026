## Overview

Coffeewood Correctional Center, near Mitchells, is a **medium-security prison for men** (Security Level 2) with a capacity of about 1,193. By general reference, it opened in 1994. The facility is in Culpeper County, in the northern Piedmont, about 38–40 miles north of Charlottesville and about 80 miles northwest of Richmond.

## What Makes Coffeewood Different

- **Northern Piedmont location.** Coffeewood is in Culpeper County, north of Charlottesville and northwest of Richmond.
- **Level 2 custody.** It holds men in medium custody (Security Level 2).
- **A mid-sized facility.** Capacity is about 1,193.
- **Opened in 1994.** By general reference, the prison began operating in 1994.

## Nearby Services

Mitchells is small; nearby **Culpeper** (about 7 miles) has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **UVA Health Culpeper Medical Center** in Culpeper.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the facility at **12352 Coffeewood Drive, Mitchells, VA 22729**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go instead to the Central Mail Distribution Center in State Farm. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
