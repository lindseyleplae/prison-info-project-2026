## Overview

Wallens Ridge State Prison, in Big Stone Gap, is a **maximum-security prison for men** at Security Level 5 — one of Virginia's two highest-security prisons, alongside Red Onion. It opened in **1999** and holds men serving long sentences. In its **early years (about 1999 to the mid-2000s), Wallens Ridge held inmates from other states under contract** — most prominently roughly 500 sent from Connecticut — an arrangement that drew litigation and criticism and ended when those states withdrew their prisoners; it now operates as a Virginia men's facility.

## What Makes Wallens Ridge Different

- **It is a maximum-security prison.** At Virginia's highest custody level, visiting conditions are stricter, and whether a visit is contact or non-contact depends on the person's status — confirm with the facility.
- **It is remote.** Far southwest Virginia, about six hours by car from Richmond, with no convenient public transit.
- **An out-of-state history.** In its early years it housed contract inmates from other states, a now-ended arrangement that is part of the prison's history.

## Visiting

The statewide VADOC rules above — the online application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Wallens Ridge. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays; the day and time rotate by unit, so confirm the current schedule with the facility

**Contact vs. non-contact:** As a maximum-security prison, this depends on the person's status — confirm with the facility

**Video:** VADOC offers video visits through its statewide program; confirm whether they are available at Wallens Ridge
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Virginia](/states/virginia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Dogwood Drive in Big Stone Gap, in Wise County.

:::key-info
**From Richmond:** About 355 miles — roughly 6 hours by car

**From the Bristol/Abingdon area:** Roughly 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes

**By air:** The nearest commercial airport is Tri-Cities Regional (Tennessee/Virginia), about 50 miles away; there is no convenient public transit to the facility
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Big Stone Gap, Norton, and Wise — clustered within about 15 miles — have gas, food, and lodging, with Norton the area's commercial hub. **Lonesome Pine Hospital** in Big Stone Gap runs a 24/7 emergency room in the same town as the prison, and **Norton Community Hospital** is about 10 miles away.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the facility at **P.O. Box 759, Big Stone Gap, VA 24219**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go instead to the Central Mail Distribution Center in State Farm. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
