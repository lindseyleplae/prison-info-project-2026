## Overview

The Estelle Unit is one of TDCJ's large Huntsville-area facilities and has a significant medical and psychiatric role. TDCJ lists it as housing multiple custody levels along with geriatric, substance-abuse, and transient medical populations.

## Why People Are Sent Here

Many people arrive at Estelle because of specialized needs rather than long-term classification alone.

-   Medical treatment
-   Psychiatric or behavioral-health placement
-   Geriatric needs
-   Transient medical housing during evaluation or movement

:::reality-check
An incarcerated person may be at Estelle for treatment or temporary placement rather than a long-term assignment. Unit location should be rechecked before every visit and before mailing anything outside the digital-mail system.
:::

## Visiting Considerations

-   Standard TDCJ scheduling rules apply.
-   Medical status, housing status, or treatment movement can interrupt regular visitation.
-   Some people at Estelle are in temporary housing, which can change timing and visit eligibility.

## Directions

From Huntsville:

-   Leave town on local roads toward FM 3478.
-   Continue north on FM 3478 for roughly 10 miles.
-   Watch for the unit entrance and posted signs.

## Parking and Nearby Services

-   Visitor parking is available at the unit.
-   Immediate services near the unit are limited.
-   Most fuel, restaurants, pharmacies, and lodging are in Huntsville rather than at the facility itself.

:::callout{variant="tip"}
For Estelle trips, most visitors handle food, fuel, and other stops in Huntsville before continuing to the unit.
:::
