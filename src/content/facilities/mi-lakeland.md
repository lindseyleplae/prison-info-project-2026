## Overview

Lakeland Correctional Facility, on First St. in Coldwater, is a **Level II men's prison** in south-central Michigan, near the Indiana line. It opened in **1985**. It sits on **Interstate 69**, which connects Coldwater to Battle Creek to the north and to Fort Wayne, Indiana, to the south.

## What Makes Lakeland Different

- **It is a Level II men's prison** in south-central Michigan.
- **It is near the Indiana line,** in Coldwater, Branch County.
- **It is on Interstate 69,** reachable from Battle Creek (about 38 miles north) and from Fort Wayne, Indiana (about 60 miles south).
- **It opened in 1985.**

## Visiting

The statewide MDOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Lakeland. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Scheduling:** All visits are by appointment, through the ViaPath scheduler (midoc.gtlvisitme.com), 48 hours to 7 days ahead

**Approved list:** Each visitor must first be approved on the prisoner's visiting list (apply with form CAJ-103)

**Schedule:** Confirm the facility's current visiting days and hours on MDOC's visiting page
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Michigan](/states/michigan/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on First St. in Coldwater, in south-central Michigan.

:::key-info
**By car:** On Interstate 69 — about 38 miles south of Battle Creek and about 60 miles north of Fort Wayne, Indiana

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Coldwater has the nearest gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Insight Hospital & Medical Center Coldwater** in Coldwater.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's name and **MDOC number**. Michigan **photocopies incoming mail in black and white and delivers the copy; the original is shredded**, so do not send originals you want kept. Color photos and messages can be sent through JPay. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/michigan/guides/mail/).
