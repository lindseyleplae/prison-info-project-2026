## Overview

Bayside State Prison, in the Leesburg section of Maurice River Township, is a **medium-security prison for men** in far southern New Jersey. It opened in **1970** as Leesburg State Prison and was renamed in **1988**. It held about **1,280 men** as of January 1, 2026.

Bayside is organized as a **complex**: a main medium-security prison and a minimum-security unit at the Leesburg site, plus a satellite unit elsewhere in the state. This structure matters for visiting, because the satellite is not at the main prison.

## What Makes Bayside Different

- **It is a multi-site complex.** In addition to the main prison and an on-site minimum unit in Leesburg, Bayside oversees a satellite unit away from the main grounds — the Ancora unit near Hammonton, roughly an hour north.
- **Confirm the unit before traveling.** Because "Bayside" can refer to more than one location, the housing unit determines where a visit happens and how far the drive is.
- **It is in the rural far south of the state**, off Route 47 and Route 55 in Cumberland County — close to Philadelphia and Atlantic City but a long drive from northern New Jersey.

## Visiting

The statewide NJDOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Bayside. The facility's own schedule:

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturday and Sunday for the main and minimum units

**Sessions:** Morning, 8 to 10:30 a.m.; afternoon, 12 to 2:30 p.m. — with separate weekday windows for primary-caregiver visits

**Scheduling:** Call (856) 785-0040 ext. 5416, Monday through Thursday, 8 a.m. to 4 p.m., at least 48 hours ahead — and confirm which unit's schedule applies
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in New Jersey](/states/new-jersey/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Route 47 in Leesburg, reached from Route 55 in Cumberland County.

:::key-info
**From Philadelphia:** About 50 to 55 miles — roughly an hour by car

**From Atlantic City:** About 38 miles

**From northern New Jersey:** A 2- to 2.5-hour drive via the New Jersey Turnpike and Route 55

**By transit:** This is a rural area with no transit stop at the prison; a car is the practical way to get there
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The Vineland and Millville area, along Route 55, has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Inspira Medical Center Vineland**, about 18 to 20 miles north of the main prison.

## Mail

Personal **letters** do not go to the prison — they go to the state's **off-site scanning center**, addressed with the person's name, **SBI number**, and the facility's code (**1403**), to **P.O. Box 96777, Las Vegas, NV 89193** (confirm the code and current format on NJDOC's Staying Connected page). **Legal mail and publications** go directly to the facility; because Bayside has units in more than one location, confirm the correct legal-mail address for the person's unit. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/new-jersey/guides/mail/).
