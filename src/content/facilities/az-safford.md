## Overview

ASPC-Safford, on South Cook Road in Safford, is an **Arizona state prison complex for men** in Graham County, in the southeastern part of the state. The complex is made up of **several units across multiple custody levels**, including **lower-custody and minimum housing**. Like all ADCRR prisons, it is organized into units, and visiting is arranged at the unit level. The complex is in a **remote location**, away from the state's major metropolitan areas.

## What Makes ASPC-Safford Different

- **It is a men's complex in southeastern Arizona,** in Graham County.
- **It has units across multiple custody levels,** including lower-custody and minimum housing.
- **It is in a remote location,** so confirm the person's unit and travel route before going.
- **Visiting is arranged by unit,** so days, hours, and rules vary across the complex.

## Visiting

The statewide ADCRR rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at ASPC-Safford. The complex's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Application to Visit an Inmate before visiting

**By unit:** Visiting days, hours, and rules are set by the specific unit — confirm them before traveling

**Reservations:** After approval, visits are scheduled with the unit; confirm its current process
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Arizona](/states/arizona/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

Safford has gas, food, and lodging serving the surrounding area. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is in Safford.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since December 2025, Arizona routes incoming personal mail to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Dallas, Texas**, where it is scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address it to **[person's name and ADCRR number], P.O. Box 211309, Dallas, TX 75211**. **Legal mail, publications, and parcels** still go directly to the complex. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/arizona/guides/mail/).
