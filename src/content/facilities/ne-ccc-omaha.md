## Overview

The Community Corrections Center–Omaha is one of Nebraska's two **community corrections centers** — the lowest custody level and least restrictive setting. It holds **men only** (about 160 as of August 2025) who are typically **nearing release** and on **work release**, **work detail**, or **educational release**, so they **regularly leave the facility during the day** for jobs or school. It sits directly across from the Omaha Correctional Center and uses that prison's medical clinic. Residents pay a daily room-and-board charge of $12.

## What Makes CCC-Omaha Different

- It is a **work-release facility**, so a resident may be at a job or school in the community during weekday business hours rather than at the center.
- It is **men-only** and the smaller of Nebraska's two community corrections centers.
- **In-person visiting is weekend-only and split alphabetically by last name** (A-L on Saturday, M-Z on Sunday), in short time slots.
- It is across the street from the Omaha Correctional Center, whose clinic provides medical care.

## Visiting

The statewide NDCS rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at CCC-Omaha.

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** NDCS sets in-person visiting on weekends, split by last name (A-L Saturday, M-Z Sunday), in short slots. Confirm current days and hours on the CCC-O visit page or by calling (402) 595-2010

**How it works:** Visits are scheduled online in advance, not walk-in; the visitor must be approved first (typically four to six weeks)

**Contact vs. non-contact:** Visits at this community-custody facility are contact visits
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Nebraska](/states/nebraska/guides/visiting/).

## Nearby Services

Omaha has food, gas, and lodging throughout the city. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **CHI Health Creighton University Medical Center–University Campus** on Cuming Street.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the facility. Nebraska scans incoming personal letters, cards, and photos through an off-site vendor (**TextBehind**, Box 247, Phoenix, MD 21131); the person reads them as images on a **ViaPath tablet** or receives photocopies. **Money, books, publications, and legal mail** go to the facility's own address — 2320 Avenue J, Omaha, NE 68110. Full addressing details are in [Mail & Packages](/states/nebraska/guides/mail/).
