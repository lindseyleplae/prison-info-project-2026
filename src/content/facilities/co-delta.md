## Overview

Delta Correctional Center, on Lockhart Road about nine miles west of the town of Delta, is a **minimum-custody men's facility** on Colorado's Western Slope. It began as the **Roubideaux Conservation Training Camp** — construction started in 1963 and it was occupied by 1964 — starting with 99 residents and 17 staff, and has expanded several times since on its 113-acre site.

## What Makes Delta Different

- **It is a minimum-custody men's facility** on the Western Slope.
- **It began in the 1960s as a conservation training camp** (the Roubideaux Conservation Training Camp).
- **It is about nine miles west of the town of Delta,** about 40 miles southeast of Grand Junction.
- **Original incoming personal mail is delivered** here (the facility is not on CDOC's photocopy list).

## Visiting

The statewide CDOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Delta. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Weekends, 9:00 a.m.–3:45 p.m.

**Scheduling:** By appointment — call (970) 874-7614 or email doc_dcc_visiting_staff@state.co.us by 1:30 p.m. the Wednesday before the weekend; an approved visiting application must be on file
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Colorado](/states/colorado/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The center is on Lockhart Road, about nine miles west of Delta, on the Western Slope.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 40 miles southeast of Grand Junction via U.S. 50; Grand Junction is about four hours west of Denver on Interstate 70

**By transit:** There is no public transit to the center; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The town of Delta, about nine miles east, has gas, food, and lodging; Grand Junction (about 40 miles northwest) is the nearest city. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Delta Health (Delta County Memorial Hospital)** in Delta.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's **commitment name and DOC number**. **Legal mail** is opened only to inspect for contraband, in the person's presence. Delta is not on CDOC's mail-photocopy list, so original incoming personal mail is delivered; the current statewide rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/colorado/guides/mail/).
