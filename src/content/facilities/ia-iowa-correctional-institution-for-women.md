## Overview

The Iowa Correctional Institution for Women (ICIW), on Mill Street SW in Mitchellville, Polk County, is **Iowa's only state prison for women**. It is in the Des Moines metro area. The campus, expanded to 888 beds in 2017, holds women across the full custody range — minimum, medium, and maximum — and offers gender-responsive services in both dormitory and celled housing. The Iowa DOC's summary page labels the facility "Medium," but the campus holds all custody levels.

## What Makes ICIW Different

- **It is Iowa's only women's prison.** Women sentenced to the Iowa DOC are held here, across all custody levels.
- **It is the statewide women's reception and classification center.** Newly committed women enter Iowa's prison system through ICIW, where intake processing, diagnostic assessment, and classification are completed — the women's counterpart to the Iowa Medical and Classification Center, which serves as the men's reception center.

## Visiting

The statewide Iowa DOC rules — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and what visitors may bring — apply at ICIW. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Schedule:** Days and hours are set by the facility — confirm the current visiting schedule before traveling

**List:** Every visitor must be approved first

**Scheduling:** After approval, visits and video visits are scheduled through the Ameelio Connect app

**Before traveling:** Confirm the visit and confirm the person's current location on the Offender Search
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Iowa](/states/iowa/guides/visiting/).

## Communication

Iowa uses Ameelio, a nonprofit provider, for phone calls, video visits, and electronic messaging. Ameelio's services are provided free to families. Video visits are scheduled through the Ameelio Connect app. Details are in [Phone & Video Calls](/states/iowa/guides/phone-video/).

## Mail

Iowa does not deliver friends-and-family mail to the prison. Personal mail is processed off-site by a private vendor, Pigeonly, in Las Vegas, Nevada, which opens and scans each item and sends a copy to the facility. Mail sent directly to the prison is returned to the sender.

Personal mail is addressed to the person and the facility at Pigeonly's Las Vegas P.O. Box, and each facility has a numeric ID code that must appear on the envelope. The exact address and ICIW's facility ID code are in the [Mail & Packages](/states/iowa/guides/mail/) guide. Legal and privileged mail goes directly to the facility's own address, not to Pigeonly.

Verify the current mailing rules and the facility's address before sending anything.
