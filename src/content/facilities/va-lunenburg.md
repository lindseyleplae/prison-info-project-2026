## Overview

Lunenburg Correctional Center, near Victoria in **Lunenburg County**, is a **medium-security prison for men** (Security Level 2) in **Southside Virginia**. The facility opened in 1995 and has a capacity of about **1,185**. It houses a general-population men's population with education, vocational, and re-entry programming.

On **August 13, 2024**, VADOC expanded video visitation at Lunenburg, equipping **every living area** so that video visits can be taken from the housing units.

## What Makes Lunenburg Different

- **Video visits from the housing units.** Since August 13, 2024, every living area is equipped for video visitation, so a person can take a video visit without leaving the unit.
- **A Southside location.** The prison is in rural Lunenburg County, about 70 miles southwest of Richmond.
- **The nearest hospital is in Farmville.** The closest 24/7 emergency room is about 33 miles away in Farmville.

## Visiting

The statewide VADOC rules above — the online application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Lunenburg. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Days:** Saturday, Sunday, and state holidays — confirm the current hours with the facility

**Scheduling:** Apply online to the Central Visitation Unit, then book through the statewide scheduler up to 14 days ahead

**Video:** Video visits are offered, including from home on a computer or Android device; every living area is equipped for video visitation
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Virginia](/states/virginia/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Falls Road near Victoria, in Lunenburg County.

:::key-info
**From Richmond:** About 70 miles southwest — generally via US-360

**From Farmville:** About 33 miles

**Transit:** No fixed-route public transit serves Victoria; a vehicle is the practical way to reach the prison

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Victoria is small; the surrounding towns of Lunenburg County and **Farmville** (about 33 miles northwest) have gas, food, and lodging, with Farmville the largest nearby hub. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **Centra Southside Community Hospital** in Farmville.

## Mail

Personal **letters** go to the facility at **690 Falls Road, Victoria, VA 23974**, with the person's name and seven-digit DOC number — where they are photocopied and the copies delivered. **Books, photos, and legal mail** go instead to the Central Mail Distribution Center in State Farm. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/virginia/guides/mail/).
