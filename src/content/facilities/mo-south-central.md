## Overview

South Central Correctional Center, on West Highway 32 in Licking, is a **maximum-security prison for men** in the south-central Missouri Ozarks. Because of its security level, visiting and movement can be more controlled than at lower-security prisons, and arrangements vary by housing unit.

## What Makes South Central Different

- **It is a maximum-security prison** for men in the south-central Missouri Ozarks.
- **As a maximum-security facility,** visiting, movement, and property are more restricted than at lower-security prisons.
- **It is in a rural part of the state,** in Texas County, a long drive from Missouri's major cities.
- **It is a separate prison from the Southeast Correctional Center** — confirm which one holds the person.

## Visiting

The statewide Missouri DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the South Central Correctional Center. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Missouri DOC online visiting application before visiting

**Right facility:** South Central Correctional Center is a separate prison from the Southeast Correctional Center — confirm which one holds the person before traveling

**Visit type:** Visiting for men in maximum-security or restrictive housing may differ from general population — confirm the housing and visit type

**Schedule:** The facility sets its own visiting days and hours — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Missouri](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on West Highway 32 in Licking, in Texas County, in the south-central Missouri Ozarks.

:::key-info
**By car:** Licking is in south-central Missouri, reached via U.S. 63 south of Rolla; it is a long drive from major cities, and a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Licking is a small town; Rolla, to the north, has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Licking and Rolla area.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
