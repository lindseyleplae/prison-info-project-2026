## Overview

The Old Colony Correctional Center, at 1 Administration Road in Bridgewater, is a men's prison with both **medium- and minimum-security** housing, in southeastern Massachusetts. It opened in 1987. The prison is one of several DOC facilities at the **Bridgewater complex** on Administration Road, along with the Massachusetts Treatment Center and Bridgewater State Hospital, so confirm which one holds the person before traveling.

## What Makes Old Colony Different

- **It has both medium- and minimum-security housing,** so visiting and movement arrangements can vary by unit.
- **It is one of several DOC facilities at the Bridgewater complex** — along with the Massachusetts Treatment Center and Bridgewater State Hospital — so confirm which one holds the person.
- **It opened in 1987** and sits on the shared Administration Road campus in Bridgewater.

## Visiting

The statewide MA DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the Old Colony Correctional Center. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list before visiting

**Scheduling:** Visits are scheduled by phone with the facility — there is no statewide online scheduler; confirm the days, hours, and how far ahead to call

**Right facility:** Old Colony is one of several DOC facilities at the Bridgewater complex — confirm which one holds the person before traveling

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Massachusetts](/states/massachusetts/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is at 1 Administration Road in Bridgewater, in southeastern Massachusetts.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Bridgewater, reached from Route 24 and Route 104, south of Brockton; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The Bridgewater and Brockton areas have gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Signature Healthcare Brockton Hospital** in Brockton, about 10 miles away.

## Mail

Incoming personal mail goes to the prison — Massachusetts does not use an off-site mail vendor. Address it with the person's full name and commitment number and the facility's mailing address (above); all incoming mail is opened and inspected. **Legal mail** is opened only in the person's presence. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/massachusetts/guides/mail/).
