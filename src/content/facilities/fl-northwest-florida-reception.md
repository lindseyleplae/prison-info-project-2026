## Overview

The Northwest Florida Reception Center, in Chipley (Washington County, in the Florida Panhandle), is the **reception and classification center for men in the northwest region**. Newly committed men there are received, assessed, and classified before transfer to a permanent institution; because of that role, a man may be here only briefly, and the institution shown in the offender search can change within the first weeks.

## What Makes Northwest Florida Reception Center Different

- **It is the men's intake center for the Panhandle region,** so a newly committed man in the northwest usually begins here before transferring.
- **Visiting is limited during the reception period,** so confirm the person's status and institution before traveling.
- **The institution shown in the offender search can change** once the person is classified and transferred.

## Visiting

The statewide FDC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the Northwest Florida Reception Center. The institution's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved on the person's visitor list before visiting (the person starts the application, Form DC6-111A, at their permanent institution)

**Reception status:** A man in reception has limited visiting and will transfer to a permanent institution after classification

**Reservations:** Many Florida institutions require an advance visit reservation — confirm the current days, hours, and how to reserve
:::

The full visitor process is in [Visiting in Florida](/states/florida/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The center is in Chipley, in the Florida Panhandle.

:::key-info
**By car:** In Chipley, reached via Interstate 10 in the Panhandle, between Tallahassee and Pensacola; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the center; a private vehicle is required

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The Chipley area has limited services; the Chipley and Panama City areas, within driving distance, have the fuller range of gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency rooms are in the Chipley and Panama City areas.

## Mail

Florida does not deliver original personal mail to the prison. Routine personal letters and photos go to an off-site **Smart Communications** processing center, where they are **opened and scanned** and delivered electronically — the person reads them on a tablet or kiosk, and the mailroom may print copies. Address routine mail with the person's **full name and DC number**, to: **Smart Communications, P.O. Box 23608, Tampa, FL 33623**. Confirm the current address and format on FDC's website before sending. **Legal mail and privileged correspondence go directly to the institution**, not to the scanning center. **Publications** must come from an approved publisher or retailer. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/florida/guides/mail/).
