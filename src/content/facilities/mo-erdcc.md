## Overview

The Eastern Reception, Diagnostic and Correctional Center (ERDCC), on Highway K in Bonne Terre, is one of Missouri's **largest prisons**. It is a **reception and diagnostic center** that receives and classifies newly committed men from eastern Missouri, alongside a general-population men's population across minimum to maximum security. It also houses the **state's execution chamber** — Missouri's executions are carried out here. It opened in 2003 and holds about **2,600 men**. Because of its reception role, a man received for intake may be transferred after classification, so the prison shown in the offender search can change.

## What Makes ERDCC Different

- **It is a men's reception and diagnostic center** for eastern Missouri, so a newly committed man may begin here before transferring.
- **It houses the state's execution chamber,** where Missouri carries out executions.
- **It is one of the largest prisons in the state,** holding both reception and general-population men.
- **It is in Bonne Terre, in eastern Missouri,** about an hour south of St. Louis.

## Visiting

The statewide Missouri DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at ERDCC. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Missouri DOC online visiting application before visiting

**Reception status:** A man in reception has limited visiting and may transfer to a permanent prison after classification

**Schedule:** The facility sets its own visiting days and hours — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Missouri](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on Highway K in Bonne Terre, in eastern Missouri.

:::key-info
**By car:** About an hour south of St. Louis via U.S. 67; Farmington is the nearest larger city, about 10 miles away

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Bonne Terre and nearby Farmington have gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Parkland Health Center**, in Farmington.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
