## Overview

Algoa Correctional Center, on No More Victims Road in Jefferson City, is a **minimum- and medium-security prison for men** in the state capital. It is a separate prison from the **Jefferson City Correctional Center**, Missouri's flagship maximum-security prison, which is also in Jefferson City. Visiting and movement arrangements vary by housing unit.

## What Makes Algoa Different

- **It is a minimum- and medium-security prison** for men in the state capital.
- **It is a separate prison from the Jefferson City Correctional Center,** which is also in Jefferson City — confirm which one holds the person.
- **Both prisons sit on No More Victims Road in Jefferson City,** so the addresses are easy to confuse.

## Visiting

The statewide Missouri DOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and item limits — apply at the Algoa Correctional Center. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Approved list:** Every visitor must be approved through the Missouri DOC online visiting application before visiting

**Right facility:** Jefferson City also has a separate prison, the Jefferson City Correctional Center — confirm which one holds the person before traveling

**Schedule:** The facility sets its own visiting days and hours — confirm them before traveling
:::

The full application process is in [Visiting in Missouri](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on No More Victims Road in Jefferson City, in central Missouri.

:::key-info
**By car:** Jefferson City is in central Missouri, reached via U.S. 50 and U.S. 54; a private vehicle is required

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Jefferson City, the state capital, has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **Capital Region Medical Center**, in Jefferson City.

## Mail

Personal mail does **not** go to the prison. Since 2022, Missouri routes incoming letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, where they are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**; address them to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678**. **Legal mail** still goes directly to the prison, and **books** come publisher-direct to the facility. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).
