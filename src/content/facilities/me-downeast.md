## Overview

Downeast Correctional Facility is a small, **minimum-security men's work-release facility** — about 48 beds — in Machiasport, in far-eastern Washington County. The original facility, on the grounds of a former Air Force station, was **closed abruptly in February 2018**; after litigation and a state agreement, a **new facility was built on the same grounds and reopened in April 2022**. It holds men in minimum and community custody with **five years or less left to serve**, and its mission is reentry through a community work-release program, along with education, substance-use treatment, and community restitution work. The facility is **associated with Mountain View Correctional Facility** in Charleston.

## What Makes Downeast Different

- **Remoteness.** It is one of the most remote prisons in Maine — roughly two hours from Bangor and four and a half hours from Portland — and no public transit reaches the facility itself.
- **The closure and reopening.** Closed overnight in 2018 and rebuilt and reopened in 2022, it is the reason families may find conflicting "closed" information online.
- **Small and reentry-focused.** With about 48 beds, it is among the smallest adult facilities in the state, holding men close to release who work in the community.

## Visiting

The statewide Maine DOC rules above — the application and background check, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Downeast. The facility's own schedule:

:::key-info
**Days and hours:** Saturday 1:00 to 2:30 p.m.; Sunday 1:00 to 2:30 p.m. and 7:00 to 8:30 p.m.

**Scheduling:** By appointment — call (207) 255-1104, Monday through Thursday, 10:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. Walk-in visits are not permitted

**Approval:** Only visitors on the resident's approved list may visit — apply early, since clearance can take up to six weeks
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Maine](/states/maine/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The facility is on Base Road in Machiasport, off the Route 1 corridor past the town of Machias.

:::key-info
**From Bangor:** About 85 to 95 miles — roughly 2 hours

**From Portland:** About 195 to 200 miles — roughly 4 hours 30 minutes

**Transit:** West's Coastal Connection runs an intercity bus through Machias, about 6 miles away, but no transit reaches the facility; a vehicle is needed for the final leg
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Machias, about 6 miles north, is the nearest service town, with gas, restaurants, and lodging such as the Machias River Inn and the Bluebird Motel. **Down East Community Hospital** at 11 Hospital Drive in Machias runs a 24/7 emergency room.

## Mail

Downeast uses Maine's split mail routing. First-class **letters** go to **811 Cushing Road, Warren, ME 04864** (the central scanning address), while **packages, magazines, and legal mail** go to the facility at **64 Base Road, Machiasport, ME 04655**. Put the resident's name and MDOC number on everything — full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/maine/guides/mail/).
