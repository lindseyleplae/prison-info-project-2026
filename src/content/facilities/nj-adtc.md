## Overview

The Adult Diagnostic and Treatment Center, in Avenel, is a New Jersey prison that holds **men sentenced for sex offenses** and provides a court-ordered treatment program under the state's **Sex Offender Act**. It opened in **1976** and held about **424 men** as of January 1, 2026. The facility is next to **East Jersey State Prison** in Avenel.

A separate facility, the **Special Treatment Unit**, is also in Avenel but is not the same place: it holds people who have been **civilly committed after their sentences end** under a different state law. ADTC holds people serving criminal sentences, and the two are run separately — so the first step is confirming which facility holds the person.

## About ADTC

- **It is a specialized treatment facility.** ADTC provides a court-ordered treatment program under the New Jersey Sex Offender Act for the men sentenced there.
- **It is next to East Jersey State Prison.** Both are in Avenel; confirm the person is at ADTC (8 Production Way) and not the neighboring prison.
- **It is separate from the Special Treatment Unit.** The Special Treatment Unit, also in Avenel, is a different facility for people civilly committed after their sentences, with its own visiting.

## Visiting

The statewide NJDOC rules above — the approved visitor list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at ADTC. The facility's own schedule:

:::key-info
**General population:** Saturday and Sunday, in two sessions — registration 8 to 9 a.m. for a 9 to 11 a.m. visit, or registration noon to 1 p.m. for a 1 to 3 p.m. visit

**Restrictive housing:** Wednesday, 6 to 7 p.m.

**Scheduling:** Call (732) 574-2250 ext. 8006, Monday through Thursday, 8 a.m. to 4 p.m., at least 48 hours ahead

**Video:** Video visits are available; see the phone and video guide
:::

Visiting times change; confirm them when scheduling. The full approval process is in [Visiting in New Jersey](/states/new-jersey/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The facility is on Production Way in Avenel, off U.S. Route 1-9 near Rahway.

:::key-info
**By car:** Off U.S. Route 1-9 in Avenel, in the same area as East Jersey State Prison

**By transit:** NJ Transit's Avenel and Rahway stations (North Jersey Coast Line) are each about a mile away; a short ride completes the trip
:::

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

The Avenel and Rahway area, along the Route 1 corridor, has gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24/7 emergency room is **RWJ University Hospital Rahway** at 865 Stone Street in Rahway, a few miles away.

## Mail

Personal **letters** do not go to the prison — they go to the state's **off-site scanning center**, addressed with the person's name, **SBI number**, and the facility's code (**1407**), to **P.O. Box 96777, Las Vegas, NV 89193** (confirm the code and current format on NJDOC's Staying Connected page). **Legal mail and publications** go directly to the facility at **8 Production Way, Avenel, NJ 07001**. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/new-jersey/guides/mail/).
