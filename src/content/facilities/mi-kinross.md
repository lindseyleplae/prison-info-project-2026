## Overview

Kinross Correctional Facility, on W. Industrial Park Drive in Kincheloe, is a **Level I and II men's prison** in the eastern Upper Peninsula. It originally opened in **1977** and **relocated to the former Hiawatha Correctional Facility site in October 2015**. It offers **welding and CDL (commercial driver) vocational training**, including on-site simulators. It is adjacent to the separate Chippewa Correctional Facility, also in Kincheloe.

## What Makes Kinross Different

- **It offers welding and CDL (commercial driver) vocational training,** with on-site simulators.
- **It relocated to the former Hiawatha Correctional Facility site in October 2015,** having originally opened in 1977.
- **It is one of two prisons in Kincheloe** (with Chippewa), so confirm which one holds the person.
- **It is in the eastern Upper Peninsula,** about 21 miles southwest of Sault Ste. Marie and about 344 miles north of Detroit.

## Visiting

The statewide MDOC rules above — the approved list, the dress code, ID, and search rules — apply at Kinross. The facility's own arrangements:

:::key-info
**Scheduling:** All visits are by appointment, through the ViaPath scheduler (midoc.gtlvisitme.com), 48 hours to 7 days ahead

**Approved list:** Each visitor must first be approved on the prisoner's visiting list (apply with form CAJ-103)

**Which facility:** Confirm whether the person is at Kinross or the adjacent Chippewa Correctional Facility
:::

The full approval process is in [Visiting in Michigan](/states/michigan/guides/visiting/).

## Getting There and Parking

The prison is on W. Industrial Park Drive in Kincheloe, in the eastern Upper Peninsula.

:::key-info
**By car:** About 21 miles southwest of Sault Ste. Marie via Interstate 75; about 344 miles north of Detroit

**By transit:** There is no practical public transit to the prison; a private vehicle is required

Distances are approximate, based on map routing. Visitor parking is on site.

## Nearby Services

Sault Ste. Marie, about 21 miles north, has the nearest gas, food, and lodging. The nearest 24-hour emergency room is **MyMichigan Medical Center Sault** (War Memorial Hospital) in Sault Ste. Marie.

## Mail

Personal mail goes to the facility, addressed with the person's name and **MDOC number**. Michigan **photocopies incoming mail in black and white and delivers the copy; the original is shredded**, so do not send originals you want kept. Color photos and messages can be sent through JPay. Full rules are in [Mail & Packages](/states/michigan/guides/mail/).
