## Setup steps

:::steps
1. **Identify the provider.** Confirm which company handles calls or video visits.
2. **Create the account.** Match the legal name and contact details used for approval.
3. **Verify funding rules.** Check minimum deposits, payment methods, and fee schedules.
4. **Test access early.** Complete login and device checks before the first scheduled visit or call.
:::
