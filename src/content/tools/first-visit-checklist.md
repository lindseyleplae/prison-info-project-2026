## Checklist

:::steps
1. **Verify the schedule.** Confirm current visiting hours and restrictions.
2. **Lay out clothing.** Match the current dress code.
3. **Prepare identification.** Bring the required government-issued ID.
4. **Plan the route.** Add time for parking and security processing.
5. **Store prohibited items.** Keep phones, bags, and restricted items out of the visiting room.
:::
