:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Personal mail is now digital.** Since September 15, 2025, non-legal letters and photos go to a **scanning center in Tampa, Florida**, and reach the person electronically — on a tablet, or printed and handed over.

**Address personal mail with the ADC number** to the Tampa P.O. box.

**Legal mail, books, and magazines do NOT go to Tampa** — they go directly to the unit. Packages, checks, and money orders sent to Tampa are returned.

**No food or care packages may be mailed.**
:::

## How to address personal mail

Since **September 15, 2025**, non-legal personal mail (letters, cards, photos, drawings) goes to ADC's off-site scanning vendor, **not** the unit:

[Inmate's Name and ADC Number]
ARDOC
P.O. Box 25918
Tampa, FL 33622

Look up the **ADC number** on the ADC Inmate Population Information Search. The mail is **scanned and delivered to the person electronically** — to a tablet, or, for someone without tablet access, printed at the unit and handed over. Because mail routes through Florida first, **delivery takes longer** than it once did.

## Restrictions on personal mail

**Photos and drawings are accepted only if they are no larger than 8.5 by 11 inches**, and non-paper items are not scanned. ADC does **not publish** a per-letter page limit or a specific greeting-card policy, so confirm anything unusual before sending. ADC's materials also do not say what happens to the **paper original** after scanning — do not assume it is returned or kept.

## What does not go to Tampa

Several things are **rejected if sent to the Tampa address** and must go **directly to the unit** instead:

- **Legal and privileged mail** (from attorneys, courts, and officials).
- **Books, magazines, newspapers, and other subscriptions.**

For these, use the **unit's own mailing address**, and check with the unit about where publications should be sent and any publisher-only rule (ADC does not post these limits centrally). **Packages, certified mail, checks, and money orders** sent to Tampa are returned to the sender.

## Legal mail

**Legal mail** goes directly to the unit, not the scanning center. It is inspected for contraband and, when marked privileged, generally opened **in the inmate's presence** and not read.

## Packages

**No food or care packages may be mailed** to an inmate. Approved items are bought through the commissary and vendor programs instead. See [Sending Money](/states/arkansas/guides/money/) for funding an account.

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Send personal letters and photos to the **Tampa scanning address with the ADC number** — and expect the person to receive a scan rather than the original. Send legal mail, books, and magazines **directly to the unit**, not Tampa. Because this system is recent and the publication rules are not posted centrally, confirm the current addresses with ADC and the unit before sending.
:::
