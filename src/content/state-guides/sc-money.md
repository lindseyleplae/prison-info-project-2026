:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Deposits go through ViaPath/TouchPay** — SCDC's money vendor. SCDC operates a **cashless system**; cash is not handed to the person.

**Send it online, by phone, or by money order.** Online and phone deposits (debit or credit card) may carry a fee; a mailed money order is free.

**Money-order rule effective November 3, 2025:** the **sender's name on the money order must match a name on the incarcerated person's approved visitor list**, and the sender must include their **Visitor ID number**.

**Every method needs the person's full committed name, SCDC number, and current institution.** Money in the account is used for phone calls and commissary.
:::

Money for an incarcerated person in South Carolina is sent to the person's account through **ViaPath/TouchPay**, the SCDC money vendor. SCDC runs a **cashless system**, so funds are added electronically and **cash is not handed over during a visit**.

## Online and phone deposits

Card deposits (debit or credit) are made through **ViaPath/TouchPay**:

- **Online** through the ViaPath/TouchPay deposit service, or
- **By phone** through the ViaPath/TouchPay deposit line.

Each deposit requires the person's **full committed name, SCDC number, and current institution**. ViaPath/TouchPay charges a **service fee that varies by amount and method**, so confirm the current fee on the vendor's site before sending rather than relying on a fixed figure.

## Sending a money order by mail

A money order mailed to the **TouchPay lockbox** is processed free of charge. Money orders are made payable to **TouchPay Holdings, LLC**, and a deposit slip must be read and processed electronically before the funds post, so a mailed deposit takes longer than an online or phone deposit.

Money orders sent to SCDC or to an individual institution are returned rather than processed. For the **current TouchPay mailing address and deposit slip**, see the [SCDC Family page](https://www.doc.sc.gov/family).

## Money-order rule effective November 3, 2025

As of **November 3, 2025**, every money order must meet two requirements:

- The **sender's name on the money order must match exactly** a name on the **incarcerated person's approved visitor list**, and
- The sender must include their **Visitor ID number** on the money order.

A sender who is not on the approved visitor list, or who omits the Visitor ID number, may have the money order rejected. Getting on the approved visitor list is described in [Visiting in South Carolina](/states/south-carolina/guides/visiting/).

## What the account pays for

Funds added through ViaPath/TouchPay sit in the person's account and are used inside the institution — most commonly for **commissary** purchases and for **phone calls**. Account money cannot be handed over during a visit. Setting up phone and video communication is covered in [Phone & Video in South Carolina](/states/south-carolina/guides/phone-video/). Other obligations may be deducted from deposits under SCDC policy.

:::reality-check{title="Confirm the vendor, the address, and the sender-name rule"}
SCDC's money vendor, the **TouchPay mailing address**, and deposit fees can change. Before mailing a money order, confirm the **current address and deposit slip** on the **SCDC Family page** — money orders sent to SCDC or to a specific institution are returned. Confirm, too, that the **sender's name matches a name on the approved visitor list** and that the **Visitor ID number** is included, as required since November 3, 2025.
:::

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Have the person's full committed name, SCDC number, and current institution ready, and confirm the current fee on the ViaPath/TouchPay deposit service before sending. To mail a money order, confirm the current **TouchPay address and deposit slip** on the [SCDC Family page](https://www.doc.sc.gov/family) — money orders sent to SCDC or to an institution are returned. Since **November 3, 2025**, the **sender's name on a money order must match a name on the approved visitor list** and must include the sender's **Visitor ID number**. Send funds only through ViaPath/TouchPay; money cannot be handed over during a visit.
:::
