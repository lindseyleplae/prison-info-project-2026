:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Trust-account deposits go through ViaPath Financial Services on the ConnectNetwork platform** — online, through the app, by phone, or by mailed money order. This applies to every Indiana state prison, including the two operated by GEO.

**A mailed money order has a maximum of $300**, must be made payable to "ViaPath Financial Services," and must include the required deposit form.

**Trust-account money is separate from phone/tablet (calling) funds**, which also go through ConnectNetwork. See the [phone & video guide](/states/indiana/guides/phone-video/).

**Fees and limits change.** Check ConnectNetwork for current fees, limits, and account setup.
:::

## How to deposit

Indiana routes money to an incarcerated person's **trust (commissary) account** through **ViaPath Financial Services on the ConnectNetwork platform**. Deposits can be made:

- **Online** at ConnectNetwork.com,
- through the **"ConnectNetwork by ViaPath" app**,
- **by phone** — Trust Fund deposits **888-988-4768**, AdvancePay **800-483-8314**, PIN Debit **855-706-2445** — or
- by **mailing a money order**.

This applies to **every Indiana state prison, including the two operated by GEO**.

## Mailing a money order

Mailed money orders go to:

**ViaPath Financial Services**
2603 NW 13th Street, P.O. Box #328
Gainesville, FL 32609-2835

A money order has a **maximum of $300**, must be made **payable to "ViaPath Financial Services,"** and must include the **required deposit form**.

## How the account works

Money sits in the person's **trust account** and is used for **commissary and approved purchases**.

Trust-account deposits are **separate from money put on a phone/tablet (calling) account**, which also goes through **ConnectNetwork**. For calling funds, see the [phone & video guide](/states/indiana/guides/phone-video/).

## Fees and limits

**Fees and limits change.** Check **ConnectNetwork** for current fees, limits, and how to set up an account. A quoted figure is not reliable; the vendor's current schedule is the authoritative source.

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Send funds only through ViaPath Financial Services on the ConnectNetwork platform — online, through the app, by phone, or by mailed money order (payable to "ViaPath Financial Services," maximum $300 per money order, with the required deposit form). Confirm the current process, fees, and any limits with ViaPath/ConnectNetwork before sending. Trust-account funds are separate from phone/tablet (calling) money, which also goes through ConnectNetwork.
:::
