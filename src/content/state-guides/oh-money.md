## How to deposit

Ohio routes money to an inmate's **trust account** through **ViaPath / ConnectNetwork**, processed by **TouchPay Holdings, doing business as GTL Financial Services**. Deposits can be made:

- **Online** at **ConnectNetwork.com**,
- through the **app**,
- **by phone**, or
- by a **mailed money order**.

For any method you need the person's **full name, ID number, and current location**. Fees vary by amount and method, so **check the vendor's fee schedule** for the current fee rather than relying on a fixed figure.

## Confirming the current vendor

Some third-party sites still list **JPay** or **Access Corrections** for Ohio. Those are **legacy from the prior vendor** and are no longer the deposit method. For the authoritative current deposit method, use **ODRC's official "Incarcerated Population Funds" page**, and check the vendor's fee schedule for current fees.

## How the account works

Money sits in the person's **trust account** and is sent through ViaPath / ConnectNetwork; it **cannot be handed over during a visit**. Funds are spent through the prison's system, and other obligations may be deducted from deposits.

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Have the person's full name, ID number, and current location ready, and confirm the current fee on the vendor's fee schedule before depositing. Use ODRC's official "Incarcerated Population Funds" page to confirm the current deposit method — disregard third-party sites still listing JPay or Access Corrections, which are legacy from the prior vendor. Send funds only through ViaPath / ConnectNetwork; money cannot be handed over during a visit.
:::
