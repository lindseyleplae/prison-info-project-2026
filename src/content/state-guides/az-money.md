## How to deposit

Money for an incarcerated person's **spendable (commissary) account** is deposited through **ADCRR's designated online deposit service**. This applies to **every Arizona state prison**.

Because the exact deposit vendor and methods can change, the current deposit options — online, by phone, or by mail — should be confirmed on **ADCRR's website** before sending.

## How the account works

The incarcerated person uses **spendable-account funds** for **commissary and approved purchases**.

Spendable-account money is **separate from money put on a phone/messaging account**. Calling and messaging funds are handled through **Securus (AdvanceConnect)**, not the spendable account. For phone and messaging funds, see the [phone & video guide](/states/arizona/guides/phone-video/).

## Fees and limits

**Fees, limits, and the deposit vendor change.** The current deposit method and any fees are listed by **ADCRR**. A quoted figure or vendor name is not reliable on its own; ADCRR's current information is the authoritative source.
