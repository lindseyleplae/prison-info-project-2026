:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Phone, tablet, messaging, and video all run through Securus.** Phone service is prepaid through Securus Correctional Billing Services, and a Securus Debit account covers calls, messaging stamps, tablet media, and video.

**Per-minute call and video rates are set by the vendor and change.** Confirm the current rates with Securus before funding an account.

**Tablets handle messaging and are the channel through which scanned personal mail is delivered.** New Mexico issued the tablets statewide beginning July 1, 2024.

**Video visits run through the tablet system and require an approved visitor.** The visitor must be on the inmate's approved list before a video visit can be scheduled.
:::

## How phone calls work

The New Mexico Corrections Department's phone vendor is **Securus**, and families set up and fund accounts through **Securus Correctional Billing Services**. Phone service is prepaid: money is added in advance, and calls draw down the balance. The per-minute call rate is set in the vendor contract and is subject to change, so confirm the current rate with Securus before funding an account.

A **Securus Debit** account is the single funding source for Securus communication services — it covers calls, messaging stamps, tablet media, and video. The incarcerated person places the call; families cannot call in. To receive calls, set up an account through Securus and have the incarcerated person's full committed name and NMCD number ready. Securus lists the service fees that apply to deposits and account transactions; review those fees at the point of payment, as they are charged in addition to call costs.

## Tablets and messaging

New Mexico issued tablets statewide beginning **July 1, 2024**. In addition to placing phone calls, the tablets handle **electronic messaging** and other media, and they are the device through which scanned personal mail is delivered. (Incoming personal mail is scanned off-site before it reaches the tablet — see [Mail](/states/new-mexico/guides/mail/).) The platform is part of the Securus system; messaging stamps and tablet media are paid for from the Securus Debit account. Per-message and per-item prices are set by the vendor and change, so confirm them before sending.

## Video visits

The New Mexico Corrections Department offers **video visits through the tablet system**. Video visitation is **separate from in-person visiting**, and the visitor must be on the incarcerated person's **approved visitor list** before a video visit can be scheduled. Because the video service and any per-session price are set by the vendor and change, confirm the current cost with Securus before booking, and confirm what is offered for the specific facility — arrangements are handled facility by facility. For the approved-list and in-person process, see [Visiting](/states/new-mexico/guides/visiting/).

## Sending money

Calls, tablet messaging, and the trust account that funds commissary are separate. The Securus Debit account funds Securus communication services; a trust account, funded separately, covers commissary. For how to deposit to a trust account, see [Sending Money](/states/new-mexico/guides/money/).

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Set up a Securus account and have the incarcerated person's full committed name and NMCD number ready; the person places the call. Confirm the current per-minute call rate, the Securus service fees, the messaging and tablet-media prices, and the video session cost with Securus before funding anything. For video visits, confirm that the visitor is on the approved list and confirm the arrangements with the specific facility.
:::
