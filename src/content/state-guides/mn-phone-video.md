:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Minnesota uses two vendors.** Phone calls and tablets run through **ViaPath**; messaging, video visits, and money run through **JPay**. Families set up a ViaPath account for calls and a separate JPay account for messaging and video visits.

**ViaPath replaced JPay as the tablet provider in May 2023.** Tablets place calls and are the channel through which scanned personal mail is delivered.

**Per-minute and per-message rates are set by the vendors and change.** Confirm the current call rate with ViaPath and the current video and messaging prices with JPay before funding an account.

**Video visits run through JPay Video Connect,** used on the JPay app or website. This is a separate product from in-person visiting.
:::

## How phone calls work

Minnesota DOC's phone vendor is **ViaPath** (formerly GTL / Global Tel*Link), and families set up and fund a phone account through ViaPath. The per-minute call rate is set in the vendor contract and is subject to change, so confirm the current rate with ViaPath before funding an account.

Calls are placed on the housing unit's phones, and the tablets described below can also place calls. The incarcerated person places the call; families cannot call in. To receive calls, set up an account through ViaPath and have the incarcerated person's full name and OID number ready. ViaPath lists the service fees that apply to deposits and account transactions; review those fees at the point of payment, as they are charged in addition to call costs.

## Tablets and messaging

Minnesota DOC tablets run through **ViaPath**, which replaced JPay as the tablet provider in May 2023; tablet distribution rolled out across the facilities through 2024 and 2025. The tablets place phone calls and are the device through which scanned personal mail is delivered. (Incoming personal mail is scanned off-site before it reaches the tablet — see [Mail](/states/minnesota/guides/mail/).)

**Electronic messaging and email run through JPay,** not ViaPath. JPay messaging is stamp-based: a sender buys stamps and a message uses one or more stamps depending on its length and any attachments. Stamp prices are set by the vendor and change, so confirm the current cost with JPay before sending.

## Video visits

Minnesota DOC offers **video visits through JPay Video Connect,** used on the JPay app or website. Video visitation is a **separate product from in-person visiting.** Because the video schedule and any per-session price are set by the vendor and change, confirm the current video session cost with JPay before booking, and confirm what is offered for the specific facility.

## Sending money

Calls run through ViaPath, while messaging, video, and the personal money deposits that fund them run through JPay. For depositing to a trust account — through JPay — and where government or corporate checks go, see [Sending Money](/states/minnesota/guides/money/).

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Set up a ViaPath account for phone calls and a separate JPay account for messaging and video visits, and have the incarcerated person's full name and OID number ready; the person places the call. Because rates and fees change and are not posted here, confirm the current per-minute call rate and service fees with ViaPath, and confirm the current messaging stamp price and video session cost with JPay, before funding anything.
:::
