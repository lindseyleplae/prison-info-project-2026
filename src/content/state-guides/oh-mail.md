:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Incoming personal mail does not go to the prison.** Letters and cards go to the off-site **ODRC Mail Processing Center (OMPC)** in Youngstown, where they are opened, scanned, and delivered to the person electronically on their **ViaPath tablet** — typically within about 48 hours, excluding weekends and holidays.

**Address personal mail to the OMPC** with the person's full name and ID number: **ODRC Mail Processing Center, 884 Coitsville-Hubbard Road, Youngstown, OH 44505.**

**Some mail still goes directly to the facility** — not the OMPC: **legal mail, publications** (magazines, newspapers, and books), and **packages**.

**Publications must come from a publisher or established retailer.**

**Legal mail is opened only in the person's presence.**
:::

## How to address personal mail

Incoming **personal mail (letters and cards)** does **not** go to the prison. It is routed to the off-site **ODRC Mail Processing Center (OMPC)** in Youngstown. There, mail is **opened, scanned, and delivered to the incarcerated person electronically on their ViaPath tablet**, typically within about **48 hours**, excluding weekends and holidays.

Address personal mail with the person's **full name and ID number** to:

> **ODRC Mail Processing Center**
> 884 Coitsville-Hubbard Road
> Youngstown, OH 44505

:::reality-check{title="Confirm the current address before sending"}
This off-site mail system is recent. Confirm the **exact current address and format on ODRC's site** before sending, rather than relying on an address used previously — mail sent to the wrong location may be delayed or returned.
:::

## What still goes directly to the facility

Some categories of mail are **not** sent to the OMPC and instead go **directly to the prison** where the person is held:

- **Legal mail**
- **Publications** — magazines, newspapers, and books
- **Packages**

## Books and publications

Publications — **magazines, newspapers, and books** — must come from a **publisher or an established retailer**, and they go directly to the facility rather than to the OMPC.

## Legal mail

**Legal mail is opened only in the person's presence.** It goes directly to the facility, not to the off-site mail center.

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Confirm the **current address and format on ODRC's site before sending**, since the off-site mail system is recent. Send **personal mail** (letters and cards) to the **ODRC Mail Processing Center, 884 Coitsville-Hubbard Road, Youngstown, OH 44505**, with the person's **full name and ID number** — those items are opened, scanned, and delivered to the person's ViaPath tablet. Send **legal mail, publications, and packages directly to the prison** instead; order **books and magazines from a publisher or established retailer**; and send **legal mail** so it is opened only in the person's presence.
:::
