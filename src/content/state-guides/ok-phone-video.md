:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Phone, tablet, messaging, and video all run through Securus Technologies,** with accounts set up through Securus.

**Per-minute call and video rates are set by the vendor and change.** Confirm the current rate with Securus before funding an account.

**Tablets are Securus JP6S devices.** They handle messaging and are the channel through which scanned personal mail is delivered.

**Video visits run through Securus video visits,** a separate product from in-person visiting. Confirm the current app and per-session cost with Securus before booking.
:::

## How phone calls work

Oklahoma DOC's phone vendor is **Securus Technologies**, and families set up and fund accounts through Securus. The per-minute call rate is set in the vendor contract and is subject to change, so confirm the current rate with Securus before funding an account.

Calls are placed on the housing unit's phones, and the tablets described below can also place calls. The incarcerated person places the call; families cannot call in. To receive calls, set up an account through Securus and have the incarcerated person's full name and seven-digit ODOC number ready. Securus lists the service fees that apply to deposits and account transactions; review those fees at the point of payment, as they are charged in addition to call costs.

## Tablets and messaging

Oklahoma DOC issues **Securus JP6S tablets** (JPay is a Securus subsidiary). In addition to placing phone calls, the tablets handle **electronic messaging**, ebooks, and other media services, and they are the device through which scanned personal mail is delivered. (Incoming personal mail is scanned off-site before it reaches the tablet — see [Mail](/states/oklahoma/guides/mail/).) Per-message and per-item prices are set by the vendor and change, so confirm them before sending. Kiosks on the housing units are an alternative for account and messaging functions where a personal tablet is not in hand.

## Video visits

Oklahoma DOC offers **video visits through Securus**, used on a smartphone, computer, or the JP6S tablet. Video visitation is a **separate product from in-person visiting**; the official visitation page covers in-person visits and does not address video. Because the video app and any per-session price are set by the vendor and change, confirm the current app and the per-session cost with Securus before booking, and confirm what is offered for the specific facility.

## Sending money

Calls, tablet messaging, and the trust account that funds them are funded separately. For depositing to a trust account — through JPay — see [Sending Money](/states/oklahoma/guides/money/).

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Set up a Securus account and have the incarcerated person's full name and seven-digit ODOC number ready; the person places the call. Because rates and fees change and the video per-session price is not posted, confirm the current per-minute call rate, the Securus service fees, the current video app, and the video session cost with Securus before funding anything.
:::
