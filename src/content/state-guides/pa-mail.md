:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Incoming personal mail does not go to the prison.** Letters, cards, and photos go to an off-site vendor, **Smart Communications ("MailGuard")**, which opens and scans them. The facility then prints a **photocopy** and delivers it — the person receives a copy, not the original.

**Address personal mail to Florida**, with the person's name and inmate number, the SCI name, and the MailGuard PO box: **Smart Communications / PA DOC, [name and inmate number], [SCI name], PO Box 33028, St. Petersburg, FL 33733.**

**Photos are limited to 25 per mailing** and may not contain nudity or obscenity. Originals are kept about **45 days** and then destroyed.

**Legal mail does not go to Florida.** It goes directly to the institution and is opened and inspected in the incarcerated person's presence.

**Books and publications come from a publisher, bookstore, or approved distributor** and ship to the PADOC **Security Processing Center (SPC)** in Bellefonte for screening.
:::

## How to address personal mail

Incoming **personal mail and photos** do **not** go to the prison. They are routed to an off-site vendor, **Smart Communications**, under its **"MailGuard"** program, which opens and scans them. The facility then prints a **photocopy** and delivers it to the incarcerated person, who receives a **copy rather than the original**.

Address personal mail with the person's **name and inmate number** to:

> **Smart Communications / PA DOC**
> [Incarcerated person's name and inmate number]
> [SCI name]
> PO Box 33028
> St. Petersburg, FL 33733

Photos are limited to **25 per mailing** and may not contain **nudity or obscenity**. Original mail and photos are retained for about **45 days** and then destroyed.

:::callout{variant="tip"}
Senders can register for a **free MailGuard tracking number** through Smart Communications to receive delivery and rejection notices.
:::

:::reality-check{title="Confirm the current address before sending"}
This off-site mail system routes personal mail out of state. Confirm the **exact current address and format on PADOC's site** before sending, and include the **SCI name and inmate number** — mail sent without the correct details may be delayed or returned.
:::

## Legal mail

**Legal mail does not go to Florida.** It goes **directly to the institution** and is opened and inspected **in the incarcerated person's presence**. Attorneys and courts use a **PADOC-issued control number**.

:::callout{variant="warning"}
Pennsylvania's legal-mail process has been litigated and revised over time. Confirm the **current legal-mail address and procedure** with the specific prison before sending.
:::

## Books and publications

**Books and publications** must come from a **publisher, bookstore, or approved distributor**. They ship to the PADOC **Security Processing Center (SPC)** in **Bellefonte** for screening before delivery. **Used or personally mailed books are not accepted.**

**eBooks** are also available on the **ViaPath** tablet.

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Confirm the **current address and format on PADOC's site before sending**, since personal mail routes out of state. Send **personal mail and photos** to **Smart Communications / PA DOC, [name and inmate number], [SCI name], PO Box 33028, St. Petersburg, FL 33733** — those items are opened, scanned, and delivered as a **photocopy**, with photos limited to **25 per mailing** and originals destroyed after about 45 days. Send **legal mail directly to the institution** (not to Florida), where it is opened in the person's presence using a **PADOC-issued control number** — and confirm the current legal-mail procedure with the prison, since it has been revised. Order **books and publications from a publisher, bookstore, or approved distributor**; they ship to the **Security Processing Center in Bellefonte**, and used or personally mailed books are not accepted.
:::
