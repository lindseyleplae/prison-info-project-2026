## How phone calls work

ADCRR provides inmate phone service through **Securus**, which the department adopted in **August 2024**, replacing the prior vendor, **ICSolutions**. Calls are **placed by the incarcerated person** — a person outside cannot call in.

To receive or pay for calls, a family member sets up a Securus account — a prepaid **AdvanceConnect** account — at **securustech.net** or by phone at **1-800-844-6591**.

This arrangement applies to **every Arizona state prison, including the contracted private prisons**.

## Call recording

Calls are **generally monitored and recorded**, except properly designated **privileged legal calls**.

## Call rates

Do not rely on a fixed per-minute figure. Rates, fees, and features change. Check **Securus** for current rates and how to open an account.

## Tablets, messaging, and email

Tablets are provided through **Securus**. Incarcerated people place calls and send messages from the tablets.

Securus also handles ADCRR's **off-site digital mail** — incoming personal mail scanned at an off-site center (see the [mail guide](/states/arizona/guides/mail/)).

:::callout{variant="tip" title="Two separate accounts"}
Money for calling and messaging is **separate from money for the person's spendable / commissary account**. Funding one does not fund the other. For deposits to the person's spendable account, see the [money guide](/states/arizona/guides/money/).
:::

## Video visits

Video visits run through **Securus**. The visitor must be on the **approved visitor list** — the same list used for an in-person visit. The [visiting guide](/states/arizona/guides/visiting/) covers how the approved list and visits are arranged.

:::reality-check{title="Vendor arrangements change"}
Vendor arrangements, rates, and features change. Confirm current details with **Securus** or the **facility** before relying on them.
:::

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Set up a prepaid Securus AdvanceConnect account and have the person's full name and ADCRR number ready. Confirm current call rates and account options on Securus (securustech.net, or 1-800-844-6591) rather than relying on a fixed figure. Money for calling and messaging is separate from money for the person's spendable account. For video visits, the visitor must be on the approved visitor list; see the [visiting guide](/states/arizona/guides/visiting/) for how that list and visits are arranged.
:::
