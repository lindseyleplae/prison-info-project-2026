:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Incoming personal mail does not go to the prison.** Since **December 15, 2025**, Arizona routes personal letters and photos to an **off-site Securus digital-mail center in Dallas, Texas**, which scans them (in color) and delivers the scans to the person's **tablet** (a black-and-white printout is provided to a person without a tablet).

**Address personal mail to Dallas**, with the person's name and ADCRR number:
**[Person's name and ADCRR number], P.O. Box 211309, Dallas, TX 75211.**

**Mail sent to the prison itself is returned** (after a grace period that ran into January 2026).

**Legal mail, publications, and parcels still go directly to the prison** — these are not part of the digital-mail change.
:::

## How to address personal mail

Since **December 15, 2025**, incoming **personal mail and photos** do **not** go to the prison. ADCRR routes them to an off-site **Securus digital-mail center in Dallas, Texas**, which opens and scans them and delivers the scans to the incarcerated person's **tablet** (color scans), with a black-and-white printout for a person who does not have a tablet. Delivery typically takes a few business days.

Address personal mail with the person's **name and ADCRR number** to:

> **[Incarcerated person's name and ADCRR number]**
> P.O. Box 211309
> Dallas, TX 75211

Mail sent directly to the prison after the changeover is **returned to the sender**.

:::reality-check{title="Confirm the current address before sending"}
This digital-mail system is recent and routes personal mail out of state. Confirm the **exact current address and format on ADCRR's site** before sending, and include the person's **ADCRR number** — mail sent without the correct details, or sent to the prison directly, may be returned.
:::

## What still goes to the prison

**Legal mail, publications, and parcels** are **not** part of the digital-mail change — they still go **directly to the facility** where the person is held. **Legal mail** is handled under the privileged-mail rules. **Publications** (books, magazines) must come from an approved source; confirm the current rules and where to send them with the facility.

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Confirm the **current address and format on ADCRR's site before sending**, since personal mail routes out of state and the digital-mail system is recent. Send **personal mail and photos** to **[person's name and ADCRR number], P.O. Box 211309, Dallas, TX 75211** — those items are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**, and mail sent to the prison directly is returned. Send **legal mail, publications, and parcels** to the **facility** (not to Dallas), and confirm the current publication rules with the prison.
:::
