## How calls work

Ohio's communications vendor is **ViaPath Technologies** (formerly GTL; its consumer brand is **ConnectNetwork**). Ohio switched from its prior vendor to ViaPath in **2023**. ViaPath provides **phones, tablets, electronic messaging/email, and video visits**.

ODRC provides several **free allowances**:

- **Twelve free 10-minute phone calls per month** to the whole population,
- **60 free minutes at reception** for newly received people, and
- **about 15 free video-visit minutes per month**.

For calls beyond the free allowances, families set up a **prepaid account through ConnectNetwork**. ViaPath customer service is **877-650-4249**.

## Call and video rates

ODRC states that the ViaPath contract **cut domestic call costs by about 60%** and **video-visit costs by more than half**, with video billed **per actual minute** rather than in 30-minute blocks. Because the published per-minute rate changes, **check ConnectNetwork's current ODRC rate sheet** for the exact figure rather than relying on a fixed number.

## Messaging and email

ViaPath provides **electronic messaging/email** through ConnectNetwork and the tablet program. Confirm which platform currently reaches the person's facility before relying on it.

## Video visits

Video visits are **scheduled and approved the same way as in-person visits**, through **ViaPath at gtlvisitme.com** (see the visiting guide), and are **monitored and recorded**.

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Set up a prepaid ConnectNetwork account and have the person's full name, ID number, and current location ready. Confirm the current per-minute call and video rates on ConnectNetwork's ODRC rate sheet rather than relying on a fixed figure, and schedule and confirm any video visit through ViaPath the same way as an in-person visit.
:::
