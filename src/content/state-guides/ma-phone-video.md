:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Calls are free.** Since **December 1, 2023**, Massachusetts has made prison phone calls — along with **video visits and electronic messaging** — **free** statewide. Massachusetts was the first state to also make county-jail calls free.

**The vendor is Securus.** Phone calls, **Securus Video Connect** video visits, and **Securus Text Connect** messaging all run through Securus.

**The incarcerated person places the calls;** a person outside cannot call in. To receive calls and connect, set up a free Securus account.

**Video visits are scheduled** (and free); attorney video visits are handled separately. The person must be on the approved visitor list for video visits, the same as for in-person visits.
:::

## Free calls and video

Massachusetts made prison and jail communications **free effective December 1, 2023**. That covers **phone calls, Securus Video Connect (video visits), Securus Text Connect (messaging), electronic cards, VideoGrams, and photo attachments** — there is **no per-minute or per-message charge**. (Older DOC web pages that mention a per-minute rate or a per-message cost predate this change.)

## Setting up Securus

Phone, video, and messaging run through **Securus Technologies**. A family member sets up a **free Securus account** at securustechnologies.com or by phone at **1-800-844-6591**, adds the incarcerated person, and can then receive calls, schedule **Securus Video Connect** visits, and exchange messages. Tablets used by incarcerated people are also provided through Securus.

The **incarcerated person places phone calls** — a person on the outside cannot call into the prison. Calls are generally **monitored and recorded**, except properly designated privileged legal calls.

## Video visits

**Video visits run on Securus Video Connect** and are **scheduled in advance** (and free). The visitor must be on the incarcerated person's **approved visitor list**, the same as for an in-person visit (see [Visiting in Massachusetts](/states/massachusetts/guides/visiting/)). **Attorney** video visits are arranged separately. Confirm the specific facility's video-visit scheduling.

## Messaging and money

**Electronic messaging** is free through Securus Text Connect and through **Corrlinks** (see [Mail & Packages](/states/massachusetts/guides/mail/)). Money for the person's canteen account is **separate** from communications and is sent through Access Corrections (see [Sending Money](/states/massachusetts/guides/money/)).

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Massachusetts prison **calls, video visits, and messaging are free** (since December 2023) — if a service tries to charge a per-minute or per-message rate, confirm it against current MA DOC guidance. Set up a free **Securus** account (securustechnologies.com or 1-800-844-6591) to receive calls and schedule **Securus Video Connect** visits; the visitor must be on the person's **approved visitor list** for video. The **incarcerated person places the calls**, which are recorded except privileged legal calls. Confirm the specific facility's video-visit scheduling, and send canteen money separately through **Access Corrections**.
:::
