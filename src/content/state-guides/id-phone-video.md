:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Idaho uses different vendors for different channels.** Phone calls run through **ICSolutions**; tablets, messaging, and media run through **ViaPath**. Set each one up separately.

**Phone calls** run through ICSolutions. The resident places the call; families cannot call in. To receive calls, families set up a prepaid account in advance.

**Tablets** run through ViaPath. Idaho moved residents onto ViaPath "Bridge 8" tablets, with the rollout completed across all facilities by December 2025. The tablet replaced the prior JPay tablet, and each resident receives a tablet, charger, and earbuds at no cost.

**Video visiting** is offered through the tablet platform. Confirm whether it is available at the specific facility before relying on it.

**Per-minute call rates and per-item prices are set by the vendors and change.** Confirm current rates with ICSolutions and ViaPath before funding an account.
:::

## How phone calls work

Idaho's phone vendor is **ICSolutions** (ICS Corrections, Inc.). The resident places the call; families cannot call in. To receive calls, families set up a prepaid account through ICSolutions in advance. ICSolutions customer service is **1-888-506-8407**.

Have the resident's full name and IDOC number ready when setting up the account. The per-minute call rate is set in the vendor contract and is subject to change. As of April 1, 2026, the Idaho Department of Correction listed the phone rate at **$0.08 per minute**; because the rate changes, confirm the current per-minute cost with ICSolutions or the facility before funding an account. Service fees may apply to deposits and account transactions in addition to call costs; review those at the point of payment.

## Tablets

Idaho's tablet vendor is **ViaPath**. As of the December 2025 communications update, ViaPath "Bridge 8" tablet deployment was complete across all facilities, replacing the prior JPay tablet. Each resident receives a tablet, charger, and earbuds at no cost. Tablet services — including messaging and media — are accessed through ViaPath, separate from the ICSolutions phone account.

Tablet features and the prices for messages and media are set by the vendor and change. Confirm what is currently available and what each item costs through ViaPath before funding an account.

## Video visiting

Video visiting is offered through the **tablet platform**. It is a separate channel from in-person visiting and from the ICSolutions phone service.

Because the video service and its pricing are set by the vendor and can change, and because availability can differ from one facility to the next, confirm with the facility whether video visiting is currently offered there and how to set it up before relying on it. In-person visiting in Idaho is arranged per facility — see [Visiting](/states/idaho/guides/visiting/).

## Out-of-state facilities

Some Idaho residents are held in **CoreCivic-operated prisons in Arizona** rather than at an Idaho facility. A person found on Idaho's [Resident/Client Search](https://www.idoc.idaho.gov/content/prisons/resident-client-search) may physically be in Arizona, where the communications process is run by the contract facility, not by the standard Idaho setup.

For anyone held out of state, **confirm phone, tablet, messaging, and video procedures directly with the Arizona facility.** Tablet and video services can take time to set up after a person arrives at a new facility, so families should expect a delay before those channels are available. See the [Idaho overview](/states/idaho/) for more on out-of-state placements.

## Sending money

Phone service through ICSolutions, tablet and messaging service through ViaPath, and the resident's trust account are funded separately, through different vendors. For depositing to a resident's trust account — through Access Corrections — see [Sending Money](/states/idaho/guides/money/).

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Idaho splits these channels across vendors, so set up each one separately: phone through ICSolutions (1-888-506-8407), and tablets, messaging, and video through ViaPath. Have the resident's full name and IDOC number ready; the resident places phone calls. Because rates and fees change, confirm the current per-minute call rate, service fees, and tablet and video pricing with the vendors before funding anything. If the resident is held in an out-of-state facility, confirm all communications procedures directly with that facility.
:::
