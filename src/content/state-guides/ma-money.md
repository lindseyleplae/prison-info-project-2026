:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Canteen-account deposits go through Access Corrections** — the Massachusetts DOC's "Secure Deposits" vendor. Deposits can be made online, through the app, by phone, or, at some institutions, in person. This applies to every MA DOC prison.

**Communications do not require a separate paid account.** Since December 2023, phone calls, video visits, and messaging are free in Massachusetts. See the [phone & video guide](/states/massachusetts/guides/phone-video/).

**Fees and limits change.** Check Access Corrections for current fees, limits, and account setup.
:::

## How to deposit

Massachusetts routes money to an incarcerated person's **canteen (personal) account** through **Access Corrections**, the DOC's "Secure Deposits" vendor. Deposits can be made:

- **Online** at accesscorrections.com,
- through the **Access Corrections app**, or
- **by phone** at **1-866-345-1884** (available 24/7; Visa/Mastercard).

This applies to **every MA DOC prison**.

## In-person deposits

Some institutions also accept an **in-person deposit by check or money order**. Whether a facility offers this varies, so the facility can confirm before a deposit is brought in person.

All deposits must be in **U.S. funds**.

## How the account works

Money sits in the person's **canteen (personal) account** and is used for **commissary purchases**.

## Money is separate from communications

Money for the canteen account is **separate from communications**. In Massachusetts, **phone calls, video visits, and messaging are free** (since December 2023), so funding a separate phone account is not required. For details on calling and video, see the [phone & video guide](/states/massachusetts/guides/phone-video/).

## Fees and limits

**Fees and limits change.** Check **Access Corrections** for current fees, limits, and how to set up an account. A quoted figure is not reliable; the vendor's current schedule is the authoritative source.

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Send funds through Access Corrections (online at accesscorrections.com, the app, or 1-866-345-1884). Confirm the current process, fees, and any in-person options with Access Corrections or the facility before sending. All deposits must be in U.S. funds. Canteen-account money is separate from communications, which are free in Massachusetts and do not require a separate paid account.
:::
