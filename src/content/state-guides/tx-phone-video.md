:::key-info{title="Key Information"}
-   **Provider:** Securus Technologies
-   **Phone calls:** TDCJ lists calls at $0.06 per minute with a 30-minute limit
-   **Registration:** Receiving numbers must be registered through [TexasPrisonPhone.com](https://texasprisonphone.com) or Securus
-   **Monitoring:** Calls are recorded and monitored except for attorney calls
-   **Remote video:** Securus visits cost $10.00 for 60 minutes where offered
-   **Tablet video:** One 60-minute tablet visit per month is available at no cost at eligible units
:::

## Phone Calls

TDCJ's Inmate Telephone System uses Securus. The incarcerated person initiates the call. Incoming calls to a unit are not available.

### Basic phone rules

-   Calls are limited to 30 minutes.
-   Phones are generally available from 5:00 AM to 12:00 AM unless unit conditions change access.
-   Calls are recorded and monitored except calls to the attorney of record.
-   The system gives a one-minute warning before disconnection.

### Receiving calls

The receiving number must be registered before calls can be completed.

:::steps
1. Register the phone number through [TexasPrisonPhone.com](https://texasprisonphone.com) or by telephone through the vendor.
1. Provide the TDCJ number for the incarcerated person.
1. Confirm the registrant's identity and the ownership of the number.
1. Wait for the incarcerated person to add or activate the number through unit procedures.
:::

The registered owner of the number must match the identification and telephone records used for verification.

## Call Costs

As of March 29, 2026, TDCJ lists standard phone calls at $0.06 per minute.

### Cost examples

| Communication type | Posted price | Typical session | Estimated charge |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Phone call | $0.06/minute | 15 minutes | $0.90 |
| Phone call | $0.06/minute | 30 minutes | $1.80 |
| Securus remote video | Flat fee | 60 minutes | $10.00 |
| Tablet video | No charge | 60 minutes | $0.00 |

:::callout{variant="tip"}
A 60-minute Securus remote video visit costs more than a 30-minute phone call at the posted TDCJ phone rate. The cheaper option depends on whether the unit offers tablet video at no charge.
:::

### Rate caps and contract structure

-   TDCJ currently posts a $0.06 per-minute phone rate.
-   Texas Government Code Section 495.027 requires the telephone vendor contract to pay TDCJ a commission of at least 40 percent of gross revenue.
-   In practice, that means the vendor retains the remaining share of gross revenue under the contract structure.
-   Federal inmate-calling regulations continue to change. New national caps or litigation outcomes can affect future posted rates.

:::reality-check
The posted rate is the current operating figure. State-law provisions, FCC orders, and contract terms do not always change on the same date.
:::

## Tablet Messaging and Tablets

TDCJ and Securus use secure tablets for messaging and other services at eligible units.

### Tablets

-   TDCJ announced the tablet rollout as a no-cost distribution to eligible inmates.
-   Tablets are used for educational, vocational, religious, legal, and communication functions.
-   Tablets do not provide open internet access.
-   Premium media or paid communication features require funds.

### Electronic messaging

-   Securus eMessaging is available through the tablet system.
-   Messages are paid for with electronic stamps.
-   Stamp prices vary by facility and message type.
-   Attachments such as photos or reply stamps usually require additional stamps.

## Video Visitation

Texas uses two remote video systems: Securus remote video and tablet video visitation.

### Securus remote video

-   Offered only at specific units
-   One 60-minute visit per month
-   Cost: $10.00
-   Registration occurs through the Securus website

### Tablet video visitation

-   Available at many additional units
-   One 60-minute visit per month
-   No cost
-   TDCJ states that tablet visits do not count against in-person visits

### Relationship to in-person visits

-   Tablet and video visits are in addition to regular contact or non-contact visits where applicable.
-   Availability is unit-specific.
-   Not every unit offers both systems.

:::callout{variant="warning"}
Video availability depends on the specific unit. A facility page, unit office, or the TDCJ remote-video list is more reliable than a general statewide assumption.
:::

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Phone, messaging, and video offerings change by unit, custody level, and vendor updates. Confirm the current posted rate, registration requirements, and video-visit availability before funding an account.
:::
