## How calls work

Georgia's phone vendor is **Securus Technologies**. The **inmate places outgoing calls only** — there are no incoming calls. **GDC lists calls at $0.08 per minute** for all call types, with a **25-minute maximum per call**. Calls go only to numbers on the person's approved **Call Allow List**, and GDC states that calls are **monitored and recorded**.

To fund calls, families set up a **prepaid account with Securus** (phone **1-800-844-6591**). GDC notes a **maximum deposit of $200 per transaction**.

## Messaging and email

Georgia historically used **JPay** for tablets and electronic messaging. After Securus's parent company acquired JPay, GDC's communications have been migrating toward **Securus** — a transition that produced documented service disruptions in 2023. Because the JPay-to-Securus migration has been ongoing, **confirm which app or platform currently handles messaging and email** at the person's facility before relying on it. Electronic messaging is limited to people on the approved visitation list.

## Video visits

GDC does not advertise a public remote or at-home video-visitation program. **Confirm whether any video option is available at the specific facility** before assuming one exists.

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Set up a prepaid Securus account and have the person's number ready; the inmate must place the call, and the number must be on the approved Call Allow List. Confirm the current per-minute rate, which app handles messaging and email at the specific facility during the JPay-to-Securus transition, and whether any video option exists there.
:::
