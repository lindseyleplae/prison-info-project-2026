:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Money goes to the person's trust account.** Funds are used for **commissary** and **phone/tablet** charges; NDOC takes statutory deductions for debts the person owes.

**Deposit online or by phone through ConnectNetwork.** Register at **connectnetwork.com** or call **1-888-428-1845**; deposits take Visa, Mastercard, or Discover. **A per-deposit fee applies.**

**Mailed deposits go to a lockbox**, not to the prison. NDOC's money-order deposit form directs mail to **Access Corrections – Nevada DOC, P.O. Box 12486, St. Louis, MO 63132**, with the money order made payable to **Access Corrections**.

**No cash at visits.** Money is never handed over in the visiting room.
:::

Money for an incarcerated person in Nevada is deposited into the person's **trust account**, which the person uses for **commissary** and **phone and tablet** charges. NDOC processes deposits through outside vendors, **not** at the prison or in the visiting room. Because NDOC has used more than one banking vendor, confirm the **current vendor, payee, and address** on NDOC's banking page before sending.

## Online and phone deposits (ConnectNetwork)

NDOC's Offender Banking Services page directs online and phone deposits through **ConnectNetwork** (the ViaPath/GTL platform):

- **Online:** register at **connectnetwork.com** and send money with a debit or credit card (**Visa, Mastercard, or Discover**).
- **By phone:** call ConnectNetwork at **1-888-428-1845**.

**A per-deposit fee applies**, set by the vendor. The deposit needs the person's **name and NDOC ID number** and the **institution**, so confirm the person's current location on the NDOC inmate search first.

## By mail (money order)

NDOC's **Money Order Deposit Form** routes mailed deposits to an outside lockbox rather than to the institution. As printed on that form:

- Make the **money order or cashier's check payable to Access Corrections**.
- Mail it with the completed deposit form to:

  **Access Corrections – Nevada DOC**
  **P.O. Box 12486**
  **St. Louis, MO 63132**

The form lists a **daily deposit limit of $2,999**, a **hold on personal checks** (with funds released after a processing period), and a requirement that the sender provide a **Social Security number for deposits of $3,000 or more**. Mailed deposits take longer to post than card deposits. Cash is **not** accepted by mail.

## What the account pays for

Funds in the trust account are used for:

- **Commissary** — food, hygiene items, and other approved goods sold at the institution.
- **Phone and tablet charges** — calls and tablet services run through the NDOC communications vendor. For how calls and video visits are set up, see [Phone & Video in Nevada](/states/nevada/guides/phone-video/).

NDOC applies **statutory deductions** to incoming funds for debts the person owes (such as restitution, fees, or medical co-pays), so the full deposit amount may not be available for spending.

:::reality-check{title="Confirm the current vendor and address"}
NDOC's banking materials show a **vendor transition**: the online/phone deposit page routes to **ConnectNetwork** (connectnetwork.com, 1-888-428-1845), while the mailed **money-order form is an Access Corrections form** payable to **Access Corrections** at the St. Louis P.O. box above. Vendor, payee, fees, and the mailing address can change. Confirm the **current** details on NDOC's [Offender Banking Services](https://doc.nv.gov/Inmates/Inmate_Banking_Services/Home) page before sending money. Questions can go to NDOC **Family Services at (775) 977-5707**.
:::

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Confirm the **current vendor, payee, and mailing address** on NDOC's [Offender Banking Services](https://doc.nv.gov/Inmates/Inmate_Banking_Services/Home) page before sending money — NDOC's materials reference both **ConnectNetwork** (online at connectnetwork.com or by phone at 1-888-428-1845, using Visa/Mastercard/Discover, with a per-deposit fee) and **Access Corrections** (mailed money order payable to Access Corrections, sent to P.O. Box 12486, St. Louis, MO 63132). Verify the person's **current institution and NDOC ID** on the inmate search first, since funds post to the **trust account** used for **commissary** and **phone/tablet** charges and are subject to **statutory deductions**. Money is **never** handed over at a visit. NDOC **Family Services: (775) 977-5707**.
:::
