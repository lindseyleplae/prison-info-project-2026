:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**One vendor handles everything: ICSolutions** (since August 2025) — phone, tablets, messaging, and video.

**The inmate places the calls; there are no incoming calls.** A prepaid ICSolutions account funds them.

**Calls run about 8 cents a minute.**

**Video and messaging cost extra and are still rolling out** — confirm what's available and the current price.
:::

## How calls work

Alabama's communications vendor is **ICSolutions** (the change took effect August 1, 2025; it replaced the previous providers). The **inmate places outgoing calls only** — there are no incoming calls — and calls run about **$0.08 per minute** (a rate that changes, so confirm it). ADOC does not publish a recurring free-call allowance. Families set up and fund a prepaid account at **icsonline.icsolutions.com** or by phone at **(888) 506-8407**. Inmates may also have tablets that place calls.

## Messaging

ICSolutions also runs **electronic messaging** to the tablet, at roughly **$0.15 per message** (with a few free each week). During the rollout, the app families use may differ by facility (for example, CorrLinks or GettingOut), so confirm which one reaches the person at their specific prison.

## Video visits

**Video visiting** through ICSolutions is priced at about **$0.16 per minute**, but as of the 2025 transition it was marked "coming soon" and may not be live at every facility yet. Confirm whether video is available for the person's prison, and the current cost, before relying on it.

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Set up a prepaid ICSolutions account and have the inmate's number ready; the inmate must place the call. Confirm the current per-minute rate, the messaging app for the specific facility, and whether video visiting is available — these are new and still changing.
:::
