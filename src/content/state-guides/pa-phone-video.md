:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Phone calls run through Securus.** The incarcerated person places outgoing calls; to receive them, families set up a prepaid account with Securus (Securus / Correctional Billing Services, 800-844-6591).

**Tablets, email, and messaging run through ViaPath Technologies, via ConnectNetwork.** Families send electronic messages through ConnectNetwork, and ViaPath supplies the inmate tablets (email, music, games, eBooks).

**Video visits run on Zoom through PADOC's Inmate Visitation System, free of charge**, and are scheduled like an in-person visit. This is separate from Securus and ViaPath.

**Per-minute call rates change.** Pennsylvania's rates are set under the FCC's incarcerated-calling rules, which changed in 2024 and again in late 2025; confirm the current rate with Securus or on PADOC's contact-an-inmate page.
:::

## How phone calls work

Pennsylvania's phone vendor is **Securus**. Calls are **placed by the incarcerated person** — families cannot call in. To receive calls, set up a **prepaid account with Securus** (Securus / Correctional Billing Services, **800-844-6591**) using the person's name and DOC inmate number.

## Call rates

Do not rely on a fixed per-minute figure. Pennsylvania's call rates are set under the **FCC's incarcerated-calling rules**, which **changed in 2024 and again in late 2025**. Confirm the current rate with **Securus** or on **PADOC's "Contact an Inmate" page**.

## Tablets, email, and messaging

Tablets and electronic messaging run through **ViaPath Technologies**, via its consumer brand **ConnectNetwork**. Families send **electronic messages through ConnectNetwork**, and **ViaPath supplies the inmate tablets**, which provide email, music, games, and eBooks.

:::callout{variant="tip" title="What the tablets cannot do"}
The inmate tablets have **no camera, no Wi-Fi, and no photo capability**, and must be **synced at a kiosk periodically** for messages to send and arrive. They are not a substitute for the video-visit system below.
:::

## Video visits

Video visits run on **Zoom** through **PADOC's Inmate Visitation System** and are **free of charge**. They are **scheduled and approved like an in-person visit** (see the visiting guide), and are **separate from Securus and ViaPath**.

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Set up a prepaid Securus account and have the person's full name and DOC inmate number ready. Confirm the current per-minute call rate with Securus or on PADOC's "Contact an Inmate" page rather than relying on a fixed figure. Schedule any video visit through PADOC's Inmate Visitation System the same way as an in-person visit — it is separate from the Securus phone account and the ViaPath tablet program.
:::
