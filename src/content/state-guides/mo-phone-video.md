## How phone calls work

Missouri DOC provides inmate phone service through **Securus**. Calls are **placed by the incarcerated person** using a phone app on the **JPay tablets** that incarcerated people are issued — a person outside cannot call in. The tablet platform is **JPay** and the calling is powered by **Securus**; the two are part of the same corporate family.

To receive or pay for calls, a family member can:

- buy **debit call time** (funded at the canteen/kiosk or by the family),
- set up a **Securus AdvanceConnect** prepaid account, or
- use **Securus Collect** (direct-billed).

Family account setup is at **securustech.net** or by phone at **1-800-844-6591**.

This arrangement applies to **every Missouri state prison**.

## Call recording

Calls are **generally monitored and recorded**, except properly designated **privileged legal calls**.

## Call rates

Do not rely on a fixed per-minute figure. Rates, fees, and features change. Check **Securus** (**securustech.net**) for current rates and how to open an account.

## Tablets, messaging, and email

Tablets are **JPay tablets**. Email and messaging run through **Securus** (**securustech.net**) and include **text messages, photo attachments, electronic greeting cards, and short "VideoGram" clips**.

Digital greeting cards through Securus/JPay are an alternative to postal mail. For postal mail, see the [mail guide](/states/missouri/guides/mail/).

:::callout{variant="tip" title="Two separate accounts"}
Money for calling and messaging is **separate from a trust-account (commissary) deposit**. Funding one does not fund the other. For trust-account deposits, see the [money guide](/states/missouri/guides/money/).
:::

## Video visits

Video visits run through **Securus**. **Remote video visits** are scheduled in advance and carry a fee, while **on-site video visits at the facility** are free. Confirm current scheduling and fees with **Securus**. The [visiting guide](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/) covers how visits are arranged.

:::reality-check{title="Vendor arrangements change"}
Vendor arrangements, rates, and features change. Confirm current details with **Securus** or the **facility** before relying on them.
:::

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Set up a Securus account at securustech.net or by phone at 1-800-844-6591, and have the person's full name and Missouri DOC number ready. Confirm current call rates and account options on Securus rather than relying on a fixed figure. Money for calling and messaging is separate from a trust-account (commissary) deposit — see the [money guide](/states/missouri/guides/money/). For video-visit scheduling and fees, confirm current details with Securus or see the [visiting guide](/states/missouri/guides/visiting/).
:::
