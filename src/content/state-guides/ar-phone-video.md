:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**One vendor handles it all: Securus** — phone, tablets, messaging, and video.

**The inmate places the calls; there are no incoming calls.** A prepaid Securus account receives them.

**ADC does not publish a per-minute rate.** It is set in the Securus portal per unit and has been changing — check the live rate before funding an account.

**Video is moving to a remote, app-based system** (about 25 cents a minute). Confirm what is available at the specific unit.
:::

## How calls work

Arkansas's communications vendor is **Securus**. The **inmate places outgoing calls only** — there are no incoming calls. Families receive calls by setting up a prepaid **Securus AdvanceConnect** account (funded up to $50 per transaction), or the inmate can pay from a **debit** account they fund themselves. A phone deposit can also be made by automated phone line at **(501) 474-6460** (with a $25 minimum); Securus customer support is **1-800-844-6591**.

**ADC does not publish a per-minute call rate.** The rate is set in the **Securus portal for each unit** and has been changing — both because Securus sets it per facility and because federal price caps for prison calls have been in flux. **Check the current rate in the Securus portal before funding an account.** ADC does not publish any free-call allowance.

## Tablets

ADC runs the **Securus SecureView tablet program**. Tablets can place phone calls (subject to unit rules) and handle **messaging, music, e-books, games, and job-search** content. Eligibility depends on the inmate's class and housing. The **monthly tablet fee is not published** by ADC — confirm it through Securus.

## Messaging

Securus also provides **electronic messaging** (email-style messages, with photo attachments), all subject to review by the facility. The **price per message is not published** by ADC; Securus shows it in the portal only after you select the specific person. Set up messaging through Securus.

## Video visits

Video visitation runs through **Securus**, and the system is **changing**:

- The **older system** uses **on-site kiosks** at the unit and has been priced at **$5.00 per 30-minute session**, scheduled in advance.
- ADC has announced a move to **Securus Video Connect** — a **remote, app-based** service at about **$0.25 per minute**, available on demand from home.

Because this transition was underway in 2026, **confirm which system is active at the unit and the current price** before relying on it. Securus video support is **(877) 578-3658**.

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Set up a prepaid Securus account and have the inmate's information ready; the inmate must place the call. Because ADC does not post call rates and the video system is mid-transition, check the current per-minute rate, the messaging price, and which video service is live at the unit in the Securus portal before funding anything.
:::
