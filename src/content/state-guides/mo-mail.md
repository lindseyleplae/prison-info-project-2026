:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Incoming personal mail does not go to the prison.** Since **July 1, 2022**, Missouri routes personal letters and photos to an off-site **Securus digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, which opens and scans them and delivers the scans to the person's **tablet** (or prints them for a person without a tablet).

**Address personal mail to Tampa**, with the person's name and DOC ID number:
**[Person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678.**

**Scanned mail is held about 45 days** at the center and then discarded; the person keeps the digital copy.

**Greeting cards are not accepted by postal mail** — digital cards can be sent through a Securus/JPay account.

**Legal mail and books do not go to Tampa.** Legal mail goes directly to the prison; books come publisher-direct, by mail to the prison.
:::

## How to address personal mail

Since **July 1, 2022**, facilities no longer accept incoming **personal mail and photos**. They are routed to an off-site vendor, **Securus**, at its **digital-mail center in Tampa, Florida**, which opens and scans them and delivers the scans to the incarcerated person's **tablet** (printed copies are provided to a person without a tablet).

Address personal mail with the person's **name and DOC ID number** to:

> **[Incarcerated person's name and DOC ID#]**
> C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC
> P.O. Box 25678
> Tampa, FL 33622-5678

Scanned mail is retained at the center for about **45 days** and then discarded; the incarcerated person keeps the **digital copy**. **Photos** are rejected if they are nude, altered, two-ply, or collaged. **Greeting cards are not accepted by postal mail**, but digital greeting cards can be sent through a Securus/JPay account.

:::reality-check{title="Confirm the current address before sending"}
This off-site system routes personal mail out of state, and vendor addresses can change. Confirm the **exact current address and format on the Missouri DOC site** before sending, and include the person's **name and DOC ID number** — mail sent without the correct details, or sent to the prison directly, may be returned or rejected.
:::

## Legal mail

**Legal and privileged mail does not go to Tampa.** Mail from courts and attorneys goes **directly to the correctional center** in original paper form and is handled under the legal-mail rules. Confirm the current legal-mail procedure with the specific prison before sending.

## Books and publications

**Books and publications do not go to the Tampa center.** They must come **directly from a publisher, distributor, or approved vendor** and are sent by mail **to the facility**. Used or personally mailed books are not accepted. Confirm the current rules and any limits with the facility before ordering.

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Confirm the **current address and format on the Missouri DOC site before sending**, since personal mail routes out of state and vendor addresses change. Send **personal mail and photos** to **[person's name and DOC ID#], C/O Digital Mail Center–Missouri DOC, P.O. Box 25678, Tampa, FL 33622-5678** — those items are scanned and delivered to the person's **tablet**, held about 45 days, then discarded. Do **not** send personal mail to the prison directly. Send **legal mail directly to the correctional center** (not to Tampa), and order **books publisher-direct, by mail to the facility**. **Greeting cards** are not accepted by postal mail; send a digital card through a Securus/JPay account instead.
