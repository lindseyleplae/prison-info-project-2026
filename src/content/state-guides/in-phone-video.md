:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Phone service, tablets, video visits, and digital messaging all run through ViaPath (formerly GTL) on the ConnectNetwork platform.** This applies to every Indiana state prison, including the two operated by GEO Group (New Castle and Heritage Trail).

**The incarcerated person places the calls.** A person outside cannot call in. To receive or pay for calls, a family member sets up AdvancePay or PIN Debit through ConnectNetwork, or accepts collect calls.

**Video visits are scheduled and paid at gtlvisitme.com** — the same platform used to register and schedule in-person visits. The visitor must be on the approved visitor list.

**Digital messages and photos are sent through GettingOut** (a ViaPath service) at GettingOut.com.

**Rates, fees, and features change.** Check ConnectNetwork (ConnectNetwork.com) for current rates and how to open an account.
:::

## How phone calls work

IDOC provides inmate phone service through **ViaPath** (formerly **GTL**) on the **ConnectNetwork** platform. Calls are **placed by the incarcerated person** — a person outside cannot call in.

To receive or pay for calls, a family member can:

- Set up **AdvancePay**, a prepaid account, at **ConnectNetwork.com** or by phone;
- Fund **PIN Debit**, where the incarcerated person buys call time through commissary and the family can add funds through ConnectNetwork; or
- Accept **collect calls**.

This arrangement applies to **every Indiana state prison, including the two operated by GEO Group** — New Castle Correctional Facility and Heritage Trail Correctional Facility.

## Call recording

Calls are **generally monitored and recorded**, except properly designated **privileged legal calls**.

## Call rates

Do not rely on a fixed per-minute figure. Rates, fees, and features change. Check **ConnectNetwork** (**ConnectNetwork.com**) for current rates and how to open an account.

## Tablets

Tablets are provided through **ViaPath**. **PIN-debit funds can be used for both phone calls and tablet services.**

:::callout{variant="tip" title="Two separate accounts"}
Money on a phone or tablet (calling) account is **separate from a trust-account (commissary) deposit**. Funding one does not fund the other. For trust-account deposits, see the [money guide](/states/indiana/guides/money/).
:::

## Video visits

Video visits run through **ViaPath** and are **scheduled and paid at gtlvisitme.com** — the same platform used to register and schedule in-person visits. The **visitor must be on the approved visitor list**. The [visiting guide](/states/indiana/guides/visiting/) covers how visits are arranged.

## Digital messages and photos

Digital messages and photos are sent through **GettingOut** (a ViaPath service) at **GettingOut.com**. To send messages, create an account, add the incarcerated person as a contact, and fund the account. The [mail guide](/states/indiana/guides/mail/) covers other forms of correspondence.

:::reality-check{title="Vendor arrangements change"}
Vendor arrangements, rates, and features change. Confirm current details with **ConnectNetwork** or the **facility** before relying on them.
:::

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Set up a ConnectNetwork account and have the person's full name and IDOC number ready. Confirm current call rates and account options on ConnectNetwork (ConnectNetwork.com) rather than relying on a fixed figure. The phone and tablet (calling) account is separate from a trust-account (commissary) deposit. Schedule video visits at gtlvisitme.com, where the visitor must be on the approved visitor list; send digital messages and photos through GettingOut (GettingOut.com).
:::
