:::key-info{title="Key info"}
**Kansas uses three different vendors for three different channels.** Phone calls and video visits run through **ICSolutions**; electronic messaging (email and photos) runs through **GettingOut** (ViaPath). Set each one up separately.

**Phone calls** run through ICSolutions. The called party sets up a prepaid account at no cost, by phone or online.

**Video visits** also run through ICSolutions, but are a separate product from in-person visiting. Remote video visits are fee-based and the cost varies by facility.

**Electronic messaging** runs through GettingOut at gettingout.com/kansas-doc or the GettingOut app.

**Per-minute call rates and per-message prices are set by the vendors and change.** Confirm the current rates with ICSolutions and GettingOut before funding an account.
:::

## How phone calls work

Kansas's phone vendor is **ICSolutions** (ICS Corrections, Inc.). The resident places the call; families cannot call in. To receive calls, the called party sets up a prepaid account through ICSolutions — by phone or online at no cost to open. ICSolutions customer service is **1-888-506-8407**.

Have the resident's full name and KDOC registration number ready when setting up the account. The per-minute call rate is set in the vendor contract and is subject to change, so confirm the current rate with ICSolutions before funding an account. Service fees may apply to deposits and account transactions in addition to call costs; review those at the point of payment.

## Video visits

Kansas offers **remote video visits through ICSolutions** — a different product from in-person visiting, and a different channel from the GettingOut messaging tablet. Remote video visits are conducted through the ICS Mobile app, a Windows app, or the web at icsolutions.com.

To use video visits, the visitor must first be on the resident's approved visitation list, then register for free at icsolutions.com. Remote video visits are **fee-based, and the cost varies by facility**. Because the app and the per-session price are set by the vendor and change, confirm the current app and the session cost with ICSolutions, and confirm what is offered at the specific facility, before booking. In-person visiting is also scheduled through ICSolutions — see [Visiting](/states/kansas/guides/visiting/).

## Electronic messaging

Electronic messaging — email and photos — runs through a **separate vendor, GettingOut** (ViaPath Technologies, formerly GTL). This is distinct from the ICSolutions phone and video service. Set up an account at [gettingout.com/kansas-doc](https://www.gettingout.com/kansas-doc/) or through the free GettingOut app for iOS and Android; GettingOut support is **1-800-574-5729**.

Messaging is a written channel, not a substitute for a phone or video call. Per-message and per-item prices are set by the vendor and change, so confirm the current cost with GettingOut before sending. Video visits are not handled through the GettingOut tablet — those run through ICSolutions, as described above.

## Sending money

Phone service through ICSolutions, messaging through GettingOut, and the resident's trust account are funded separately, through different vendors. For depositing to a resident's trust account — through Access Corrections — see [Sending Money](/states/kansas/guides/money/).

## Verify Before Acting

:::callout{variant="warning" title="Verify Before Acting"}
Kansas splits these channels across vendors, so set up each one separately: phone and video through ICSolutions (1-888-506-8407), messaging through GettingOut (1-800-574-5729). Have the resident's full name and KDOC registration number ready; the resident places phone calls. Because rates and fees change and the video session price varies by facility, confirm the current per-minute call rate, the ICSolutions service fees, the video app and session cost, and the GettingOut per-message price with the vendors before funding anything.
:::
