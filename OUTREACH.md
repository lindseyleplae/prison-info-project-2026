# Outreach Plan — Getting Real Users

Goal (Lindsey, June 2026): make this a site people actually use. Reach out to
organizations in California and Texas to (1) get expert feedback and
(2) ask them to share/link the site if they find it useful.

## Why orgs first
Families in crisis don't search for "prison visitor guide" — they ask the
organizations they already trust. One link on a trusted org's resources page
reaches more families than months of waiting for search traffic, and it builds
the search ranking that eventually brings direct traffic too.

## The two asks (keep it this small)
1. **Feedback** — "You know this system better than any website. If anything on
   our [state] pages is wrong or missing, we'll fix it." (Low-friction, starts a
   relationship, makes the site better.)
2. **Distribution, only if they find it useful** — a link on their resources
   page, a mention to members, or printing our quick-reference tools to hand out.

## The icebreaker we already have
Several of these organizations are already referenced (factually, for free) on
our facility pages — Friends Outside on every CA page, TIFA on every TX page,
Hospitality House on the Huntsville-area pages. Opening line: "your
organization is already on the pages we publish for families in your state."
