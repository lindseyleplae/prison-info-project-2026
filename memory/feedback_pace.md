Lindsey's direction (2026-04-29): "build carefully and don't rush. it is more important for these to be done correctly than quickly."

**Why:** This is a public reference site that families will rely on during real prison visits. Inaccurate or sloppy pages erode the trust the site is built on. There is no deadline pressure. Lindsey would rather wait an extra round and have a page that's right than ship something that needs follow-up cleanup.

**How to apply:**
- For any content batch, follow the full SCALING.md review checklist — no shortcuts. Tone review every page, fact review every page, render check before push.
- Do not skip the render check just because the gates passed.
- Prefer smaller batches (2-5 facilities) over big batches if a smaller batch lets you review more carefully.
- If something feels rushed or under-reviewed, slow down — it is fine to take an extra cycle on a single batch rather than push and clean up after.
- Do not propose ambitious "let's do 10 at once" batches. Default to small, well-reviewed batches.
- If Codex produces something that looks "fine" but you haven't read it word-by-word, you have not earned the right to push yet.
